复习

laundry

n.洗衣，送洗的衣物
Doing laundry is part of my housework.
我的家务之一是洗衣服。

razor

n.剃刀，刮胡刀
He shaved his face with a razor.
他正在用刮胡刀刮胡子。

authentic

adj.真实的，真正的
This diamond isn’t fake - I’m sure it’s authentic.
这颗钻石不是假的，我敢保证它是真的！

commercial

adj.商业性的，贸易的
n.商业广告
This is the busiest commercial street in the city.
这是这个城市最繁华的商业街了。

waist

n.腰
The man put the measuring tape around his waist.
这个人在腰上围了一条软尺。

tendency

n.趋向，趋势，倾向
There’s a good tendency for our company’s business.
我们公司的业务有一个良好的趋势。

coal

n.煤，煤块
Coal is a very popular form of energy.
煤是种很常见的能源。

wire

n.电缆，电线，金属线
Look, many birds are sitting on the wires!
看，有很多鸟站在电线上呢！

tense

adj.（神经）紧张的
v.使拉紧，拉紧
n.时态
I feel really tense about the coming exam.
我真的很担心即将来临的考试。

astronaut

n.宇航员
I would love to be an astronaut and go to space.
我想成为一名宇航员并进入太空。

grocery

n.食品杂货店
Every day she goes shopping in the grocery store.
她每天都去食品杂货店购物。

suspension

n.悬浮，暂停
In outer space, things are in a state of suspension.
在外太空，东西处于悬浮状态。

conscience

n.良心，良知
My conscience always tells me to do something right.
我的良心经常告诉我要做对的事。

cast

v.投，掷，放钓
n.演员表、演员阵容
He’s going to cast that rock by the seaside.
他在海边准备把石块投掷出去。

helicopter

n.直升机
We took a helicopter ride over the sea.
我们搭乘直升机飞过大海。

monument

n.纪念碑，遗迹，遗址
The Washington Monument stands in the center of Washington DC.
华盛顿纪念碑矗立在华盛顿市中心。

starve

v.（使）饿死，挨揍
The bear is starving, it doesn’t have enough food to eat.
这只熊快饿死了，它没有食物吃。

splendid

adj.壮观的，辉煌的，华丽的
Look at this splendid sunset! I love this place.
看这落日多美啊！我真喜欢这里。

servant

n.仆人
This is my servant with the drinks.
这是我送饮料的仆人。

mess

n.凌乱状态，混乱状态
v.浪费时间，瞎闹
My dog made a mess in the room.
我的狗狗把房间里弄得乱七八糟的。

architecture

n.（总称）建筑物，建筑学，建筑体系，结构
The bright colors is one feature of ancient Chinese architecture.
艳丽的色彩是中国古建筑的特点之一。

retire

v.退休，退役
The retired couple are traveling together.
这对已经退休的夫妻正在一起旅行。

harmless

adj.无害的，无恶意的，不致伤的
The tiger is locked in the cage, so it’s harmless to the people outside.
老虎被关在笼子里，所以对外面的人无害。

demand

v.强烈要求，需要
n.要求（坚决的），需求
The bad guy demanded $1,000,000, or he would kill the boy.
坏人要求100万赎金，否则他就要撕票。

currency

n.货币，流通
In different countries, we must use a different currency to buy things.
在不同的国家，我们得用不同的货币来买东西。

punctuate

v.加标点与，强调
Which one do I use to punctuate my sentence?
这个句子里我该用哪个标点呢?

balcony

n.阳台，剧院楼厅
I like to stand on my apartment’s balcony.
我喜欢站在公寓的阳台上。

assess

v.评估
How do you assess the risk level of this investment? Low, medium or high?
你对这次投资的风险评估是怎样的？低，中等，还是高风险？

deliver

v.递送，交付，履行
Santa delivers many gifts on December 25th.
圣诞老人在12月25号会递送很多礼物。

cautious

adj.小心的，谨慎的
The maid is very cautious not to make a mistake in front of the empress.
这个丫鬟在皇后面前非常谨慎，不敢犯任何错误。

liquid

n.液体
adj.液体的
Water is a kind of liquid without color.
水是一种没有颜色的液体。

flour

n.面粉，粉状物质
There is a bag of flour.
这里有一包面粉。

enquiry

n.询问，问询处
I make enquiries at the information desk.
我在咨询台咨询了一些事情。

budget

n.预算
v.预算，安排
adj.廉价的
I don’t have much money for the budget.
我的钱不够预算。

drill

v.钻孔，在…上钻孔
n.钻头
The worker drills into the wall.
工人在墙上钻孔。

sacred

adj.神圣的，宗教（性）的
In India, the elephant is a sacred animal.
在印度，大象被视为神圣的动物。

allergic

adj.过敏的，对…讨厌的
Look at his face! The poor guy was allergic to seafood.
看他的脸，这个可怜的家伙对海鲜过敏。

muddy

adj.泥泞的，浑浊的
v.显得扑朔迷离
It’ll take hours to drive down this muddy road.
驶过这段泥泞的小路要花好几个小时。

athletic

adj.运动的，体育的，强壮的
The athletic man runs for long distances without needing to rest.
这个运动员可以跑很长的距离，并且不用休息。

equipment

n.设备
High-tech equipment is used in the factory.
工厂运用高科技设备。

neat

adj.整洁的，不掺水的
The folded clothes look very neat in the drawer.
抽屉里折叠起来的衣服看起来很整洁。

sorrow

n.悲伤，苦恼，伤心
v.感到悲伤，为…悲痛
Her tears are full of sorrow.
她的眼泪充满悲伤。

circus

n.马戏团，环形广场
We saw many animals at the circus.
我们在马戏团看到了许多动物。

iron

n.熨斗
adj.刚强的
v.烫（衣），熨
Use an iron to straighten clothing.
用熨斗把衣服烫平。

ash

n.灰，灰烬
The ashtray is full of ash.
这个烟灰缸里满是烟灰。

settle

v.安顿，调停
They are getting settled in their new house.
他们在新房子里安顿下来。

symptom

n.症状
Flu symptoms include a headache, sore throat and a fever.
流感的症状包括头痛、嗓子痛和发烧。

inn

n.客栈，小旅馆
This traditional inn is surrounded by trees.
这个传统的小旅店被绿树环绕。

disappoint

v.使失望，使扫兴，使受挫折
The exam result disappointed him.
考试成绩让他很失望。

nervous

adj.紧张的，焦虑不安的
The father is nervous about his baby’s birth.
这个父亲因为宝宝的出生很紧张。

bungalow

n.平房，小屋
I live in a little bungalow by the beach.
我住在沙滩边的小屋里。

brand

n.商标，品牌
v.归为（不好的事物）
This famous brand makes many different products.
这个著名品牌有各式各样的周边产品。

clap

v.拍（手），鼓（掌）
n.轻拍（以示热情招呼），拍手（喝彩），鼓掌（欢迎），善意的拍打
In a show of support, they all began to clap their hands.
为了表示支持，他们开始鼓掌。

evolution

n.进化，演变
This picture shows the evolution of humans over millions of years.
这张图展示了百万年来人类的进化。

independence

n.（国家的）独立，（人的）自立，独立
America gained its independence from Britain in 1776!
1776年，美国脱离英国取得独立！

fear

v.害怕，担心
n.顾虑，胆怯
The little girl fears the injection.
这个小女孩害怕打针。

occupy

v.占领，占据，侵占，担任（职务），任职
The little dog has occupied her heart.
这只小狗占据了她的心。

dormitory

n.宿舍，学生宿舍，宿舍楼
Look! This dormitory is so dirty!
快看呀！这件宿舍好脏！

greedy

adj.贪婪的，贪心的，贪吃的
The greedy woman wanted more food from the pot before she’d even finished her bowl.
贪婪的她吃着碗里的，想着锅里的。

standard

n.标准，规格
adj.标准的
Should this be the standard for the perfect figure?
这种身材是完美身材的标准吗？

dioxide

n.二氧化碳
Carbon dioxide is making our planet warmer.
二氧化碳正在使地球升温。

beyond

prep.超过，在能力、理解力之外，除…之外
adv.更远处，更多，以后
I couldn’t hit him as he was beyond my reach.
我打不了他，因为我根本够不着他。

pregnant

adj.怀孕的
The pregnant woman is drinking water.
这个怀孕的女人正在喝水。

heat

n.高温，【物理学】热
vt.给…加热
Be careful of the heat from the fire!
小心这堆火的高温！

permanent

adj.永久的，持久的
Her eyebrows are being made permanent.
这位女士正在做永久纹眉。

cordless

adj.（电器）不用电线的
A cordless mouse is convenient for using your computer.
操作电脑时，无线鼠标很好用。

presentation

n.演讲，颁奖仪式
The sheep gave a presentation to his friends about protecting themselves.
这个绵羊给小伙伴们开展了一次讲座。

intention

n.意图，意向，目的，打算
I have every intention of buying a house, so I must start saving now.
我打算买房，所以现在必须开始存钱了。

adequate

adj.充足的，充分的，足够的，尚可的，过得去的
I have prepared an adequate amount of food for 5 people.
我为五个人准备了充足的食物。

delicate

adj.易碎的，易坏的，脆弱的
Porcelain cups are very delicate, do not drop them!
瓷杯易碎，小心掉落！

pulse

n.脉搏
v.（使）跳动，搏动，震动，（感情等）洋溢，充满
The doctor felt the patient’s pulse.
医生给病人把了脉。

insure

v.为…投保，保险，买保险
He insures the cars against traffic accidents.
他为车投保了事故险。

savage

n.野蛮人，粗鲁的人
adj.凶猛的，猛烈的
v.（狗等）乱咬
Their wordy conversation made me feel like a total savage!
他们文邹邹的对话让我觉得自己完全就像个野人！

glad

adj.高兴的
I am very glad to hear from my mother.
我很高兴收到母亲的来信。

rely

v.依赖，依靠，信赖
Babies rely on their parents for food and care.
宝宝们依靠爸妈照顾和提供食物。

conductor

n.指挥，售票员
The conductor is the leader of the band.
指挥是乐队的老大。

rescue

v.营救，救援
n.营救，救援
The fireman rescued the little boy from the fire.
消防员把小男孩从火中救了出来。

fee

n.费用，酬金
He was shocked by the huge amount of hidden fees behind such a haircut.
他被这个发型背后的巨额隐形性费用惊呆了。

accomplish

v.完成，做到，实现
I have accomplished my tasks on schedule.
我已经完成了计划上的任务。

organ

n.器官
Every organ plays an important role in our body.
每一个器官在我们身体中都扮演着重要的角色。

patience

n.耐心，忍耐
Fishing demands a great deal of patience.
钓鱼需要极大的耐心。

candidate

n.（官职、获奖资格等的）候选人
The candidates are presenting the speeches for the election.
候选人正在为选举发表演说。

merchant

n.商人，批发商
The merchant checks the quality of his produce.
商人在检查农产品质量。

construct

v.建造，构筑
The workers are constructing a tall building.
工人们正在建造一座高楼。

cure

v.治愈，治疗
These pills will cure your sickness.
这些药片能治好你的病。

ridiculous

adj.可笑的，荒谬的
She looks ridiculous wearing this dress when waliing in the street.
她穿着这件衣服上街，看上去太可笑了。

fluency

n.流畅，流利
He speaks with perfect fluency.
他说话极其流畅。

hug

v.拥抱，紧抱
n.拥抱
The happy couple hug in front of their new home.
这对开心的夫妇想用在他们的新房子前。

pardon

v.原谅，宽恕
excl.请再说一遍
n.原谅，饶恕，赦免
The king pardoned the prisoner.
君主赦免了那名囚犯。

fur

n.毛皮，毛皮外套，人造毛
A fur coat is very warm, but very expensive!
皮毛外套很温暖，但也很昂贵。

bacterium

n.细菌
This kind of bacterium can cause a very dangerous disease.
这种细菌能引发非常危险的疾病。

shoulder

n.肩膀，肩部
vt.肩负，承当
The old man has pain in his shoulder.
这位老年人肩膀疼。

religious

adj.虔诚的
He is a religious believer, he goes to chuch every week.
他是个虔诚的教徒，每周都会去教堂。

pupil

n.小学生
The pupils are walking to school.
小学生们正在走路去学校。

seminar

n.研讨会
A teacher and some students are attending a math seminar.
一名老师和学生们正在开数学研讨会。

salute

v.向…行举手礼，赞扬，赞颂
n.致敬，欢迎
The policeman salutes me as I walk by.
当我走过时，这位警察向我行礼。

vacation

n.休假，假期
v.休假，度假
The girl took a vacation on the beach.
这个女孩在海边度假。

pilot

n.飞行员
adj.实验性的
v.给…领航，驾驶
Wow! The pilots in the movie look so cool!
哇塞！这部电影里的飞行员啊好酷好帅啊！

mustard

n.芥末
adj.芥末色的
I like to add some mustard on my sushi.
我喜欢往寿司上加点芥末。

agriculture

n.农业
We depend on agriculture to give us food.
我们依靠农业给我们提供食物。

lip

n.嘴唇，唇状物
Her lips are the most beautiful part of her face.
她的嘴唇是她脸上最漂亮的部分。

extraordinary

adj.非同寻常的
The teacher said my daughter has extraordinary musical talent.
老师说我女儿在音乐方面天赋异禀。

wounded

adj.受伤的
The wounded soldier goes to the doctor every day.
这位负伤的士兵每天都去看医生。

pure

adj.纯净的，纯粹的，纯洁的
The water seems so pure and clean.
水看起来真纯净。
