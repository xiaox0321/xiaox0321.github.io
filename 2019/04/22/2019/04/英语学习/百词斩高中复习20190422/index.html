复习

frost

n.霜冻，严寒
v.结霜，冻死
The leaf was covered with frost.
树叶上覆盖率一层霜。

spear

n.标枪
v.刺戳
She used her full strength to throw the spear at the target.
她用尽全力把标枪向目标仍去。

cubic

adj.立方体的，立体的，立方的
There is a cubic cake on the plate.
盘子里有一块方形的蛋糕。

sight

n.视力，看见，视线，景物
v.瞄准，看见，观测
She can see the last line in the sight test!
她的视力检查中可以看清最后一行！

spread

v.伸开，伸展，散步，传播，摊开，覆盖
n.伸开，伸展，散步，传播，摊开，范围
Spread the butter evenly to all parts of the bread.
将黄油均匀地涂抹到面包的各个部分。

consistent

adj.始终如一的，持续的，一致的
Her dream of becoming a nurse remained consistent throughout her life.
在她的一生中，成为一名护士的愿望始终没有变。

exhibition

n.展览，展览会，奖学金
The art gallery had an exhibition of my photos.
这个艺术画廊举办了我的影作品展。

bush

n.灌木丛
Look, there is a rabbit in the bush!
看，那丛灌木里有一只兔子！

crop

n.作物，庄稼，一批，大量
The farmers are planting new rice crops.
农民在种新水稻。

consequence

n.结果，后果，重要性
The consequence of eating too much junk food is getting fat.
吃太多垃圾食品的后果就是长胖。

snatch

v.强夺
n.片断
The boy snatches the ball from his friend.
这个男孩从朋友手上把球夺走。

clumsy

adj.笨拙的，不灵活的
She finds yoga difficult because she’s clumsy!
她觉得瑜伽很难，因为她的身体不太灵活。

freeze

v.冻结，使冻僵
n.严寒，冻结
She was able to freeze the mountain with her magic.
她用自己的法术把整座山冻结了。

figure

n.体型，数字
v.想出，估计，计算出
Men’s figures are different from women’s figures.
男性和女性的体型是不一样的。

population

n.人口，全体居民
The population of the world is about seven billion people.
世界人口大约有70亿。

surrounding

adj.周围的，环境的，周围的（事物）
The sheep is constrained by the surrounding fence.
那只羊被周围的栅栏给关起来了。

sword

n.剑，刀
The man is holding the sword over his head.
他把剑举在头上。

curtain

n.窗帘，幕（布）
v.装帘子，遮掉
The play starts as the curtain opens.
帘幕打开的时候，戏剧就开始了。

construction

n.建筑业，建筑物
I work in construction - I build houses.
我在建筑行业工作 - 我盖房子。

heel

n.（鞋）后跟
v.给（鞋）订后掌，倾侧，紧跟
The red shoes with the high heels look great.
这双红色高跟鞋真漂亮。

interpreter

n.口译员，口译者
The interpreter helps the two people to communicate with each other.
口译员帮助这两个人相互交流。

canal

n.运河，河流
This city has many canals running through it.
这座城市有很多运河穿流其中。

criterion

n.标准，准则
The criterion for selecting each candidate varies from one to another.
我们挑选每位选手的标准各不相同。

pavement

n.人行道
Please walk on the pavement, not the grass.
请在人行道上行走，不要践踏草坪。

among

prep.在…中间，在…之中
The red apple is eye-catching among the yellow ones.
这个红苹果在黄苹果中很显眼。

arrange

v.整理，达成协议
I arrange my toys so that they can fit in a box.
我把玩具排列好，这样它们就能都放在一个盒子里。

pace

n.步，步伐
v.踱步
The ducks follow their mother’s pace to cross the road.
小鸭子们跟着妈妈的步伐过马路。

harmful

adj.有害的
Smoking is harmful to your health.
吸烟对你的健康有害。

advocate

v.提倡，为…辩护
n.提倡者
The man is advocating that we should save water.
他正在提倡大家节约用水。

powder

n.粉，火药
v.抹粉
Flour is a kind of white powder.
面粉是一种白色的粉末。

friction

n.摩擦，摩擦力
The friction heats up the wood and starts a fire.
摩擦力使得火柴着了起来。

rot

v.（使）腐烂
n.腐烂
Eat your fruit before it starts to rot.
在水果腐烂前吃掉。

joy

n.欢乐，喜悦，乐事
The old man is sitting on the stairs with a look of joy.
这个老人面带喜悦地坐在阶梯上。

cater

v.供应伙食，为（集会、婚礼等）承办酒席
We hired a fancy restaurant to cater for our party.
我们雇了一家很棒的饭店给我们的派对供应伙食。

temple

n.庙宇，圣堂，神殿，太阳穴
The old temple was beautiful at night.
这个古老的庙宇在晚上十分漂亮。

deserve

v.值得，应得，应受
She deserves such a high ranking because of her popularity.
以她的高人气，她应该得到这样的名次。

dessert

n.甜点
Do you want to have some ice cream for dessert?
你想来一杯冰激凌当甜点吗？

welfare

n.福利
The organization aims to improve the welfare of animals.
这个组织旨在提升动物福利。

bow

v.鞠躬，点头，弯腰
n.弓，蝴蝶结，鞠躬
The singer bowed to us after the show.
演出结束后，歌手向我们鞠躬致谢。

contrary

adj.相反的，对立的，矛盾的，叛逆的
The lady and the man hold contrary views.
这位女士和男士持有相反的意见。

vain

adj.徒劳的，没有真正意义的，没有真正价值的，无关紧要的
Using a basket to fetch water, all his efforts were in vain.
他用竹篮子打水，所以他所有的努力都徒劳无功的。

prejudice

n.偏见，损害
vt.使抱偏见
Some people had a prejudice against black people in the past.
过去人们对黑人有偏见。

ache

n.疼痛
v.觉得疼痛
Mark had an ache in his back.
马克背部疼痛。

absurd

adj.可笑的，荒唐的
n.荒唐
It’s absurd for a grown man to ride such a small bike.
成年人骑这么小的自行车是很可笑的。

skeptical

adj.表示怀疑的
He told me he can fly, but of course I’m skeptical.
他告诉我他可以飞，不过当然我表示怀疑。

diverse

adj.不同的，差异的，多种多样的
I can see diverse colors on their hands.
在他们手上我能看到各种各样的颜色。

ministry

n.（政府的）部，内阁，（全体）牧师
Governments have many different ministries, such as the Ministry of Health.
政府设有许多不同的部门，比如卫生部。

comb

v.梳理
n.梳理
The mother is combing her daughter’s hair.
妈妈正在帮她的女儿梳头发。

reasonable

adj.合理的，通情达理的
The snacks are sold at a reasonable price.
这些零食售价十分合理。

fold

v.折叠
n.折叠
She is good at folding paper into animals.
她很擅长折纸。

oval

adj.椭圆形的
n.椭圆形
The mirror is in oval shape.
这镜子是椭圆形的。

entire

adj.全部的，完整的，整个的
From the airplane, I could see the entire city.
在飞机上，我可以看到整个城市。

vague

adj.含糊的，模糊的，不明确的，茫然的
He didn’t understand her vague words.
他没有理解女孩含糊的话语。

attempt

vt.视图，尝试，努力去做
n.尝试
The cat attempts to catch that fish!
那个喵星人视图抓到那条鱼！

correspond

v.通信，相符，一致
We correspond by email to stay in touch.
我们靠电子邮件来保持联系。

chimney

n.烟囱
The chimney spits out smoke every day.
烟囱每天都往外喷烟。

leak

v.漏，泄露
n.漏洞
Water is leaking through the ceiling! Poor guy!
他家房顶正漏水呢！真可怜！

grain

n.谷物种子，谷物，少量
v.使成粒状
Grains like wheat are used to make bread.
像小麦这样的谷物是用来做面包的。

theory

n.理论，原理
That student is interested in all kinds of science theories.
那孩子对各种科学理论都感兴趣。

urban

adj.城市的，城镇的，都市的
I like urban environments, especially the landscape at night.
我喜欢都市的氛围，尤其是夜晚的景色。

telescope

n.望远镜
vi.缩短
adj.套筒的，伸缩式的
vt.使套入
You can see many distant stars through the telescope.
通过望远镜，你可以看到许多遥远的星星。

achieve

v.完成，达到，获得，实现
I finally achieved my goal of winning the race.
我赢了跑步比赛，终于达成了我的目标。

lack

n.欠缺
v.缺乏，不足
Because of a lack of water, few plants can grow here.
因为缺乏水，植物很难在这儿存活下来。

pack

v.打包
n.行李
My cat packed his stuff to leave.
我家的猫打包行囊，准备离家出走。

burden

n.负担，重担
v.使负重，装载，烦扰
Our burden is too heavy, because we have too much homework.
我们的负担太重了，因为每天都有很多作业。

considerate

adj.体贴的，考虑周到的
He is a considerate husband reminded his wife to take an umbrella.
他是一位体贴的丈夫，会提醒妻子带伞。

pest

n.害虫，讨厌的人（或东西）
Pests are annoying and harmful bugs.
害虫是讨厌又有害的昆虫。

disappointment

n.沮丧
His disappointment at not getting the job showed in his face.
他未得到工作，沮丧就写在脸上。

sleeve

n.袖子
One sleeve is too long while the other is fine.
这只袖子太长了点，但另一只还好。

frequent

adj.时常的，频繁的
Her frequent online shopping costs her a lot of money.
频繁地网上购物花了她很多钱。

anecdote

n.轶事，趣闻
The anecdote is about the mysterious creature in the ancient times.
这桩轶事是关于古代的神兽的。

dimension

n.维度，方面
These shapes have three dimensions.
这些图形有三个维度。

affect

v.影响，感动，作用
Second hand smoking may affect other people’s health.
二手烟会影响其他人的健康。

aware

adj.意识到，知道的
He suddenly became aware of how to figure out the problem.
他突然知道该怎么解决这道难题了。

courtyard

n.庭院，院子
The dog is having fun in the courtyard.
狗狗在院子里愉快地玩耍。

expert

n.专家
adj.熟练的，专业的
He is an expert at fixing the computer.
他是修电脑的专家。

squirrel

n.松鼠
v.存储
That squirrel looks so cute in snow.
那只松鼠在雪地里看起来真可爱。

acquisition

n.获得，掌握，获得物，取得物
Reading is essential to the acquisition of knowledge.
阅读对于获取知识来说是必要的。

plenty

n.丰富，大量
We have plenty of fruit and vegetables.
我们有充足的水果和蔬菜。

compensate

v.补偿，赔偿，给…报酬
He must compensate for his mistakes.
他必须弥补他的过失。

constitution

n.组成，结构，宪法，章程
The chemical constitution of vitamin B6 seems quite complex.
维他命b6的化学构造看起来很复杂。

professor

n.教授
The actor plays the role of a professor in that TV series.
那个演员在那部电视剧中扮演一位教授。

cathedral

n.大教堂
adj.有权威的
We visited a magnificent cathedral.
我们参观了一座宏伟的教堂。

satisfy

v.（使）满意，满足，符合…的标准
The delicious pizza satisfied that boy.
这个好吃的披萨满足了那个小朋友。

experiment

n.实验
v.进行实验
The scientist is doing an experiment.
科学家在做一项实验。

accumulate

vt.积攒，积累
vi.聚积，堆积
He accumulates money for future use.
他攒了些钱，以供将来使用。

pyramid

n.金字塔
I will travel to Egypt to look at the pyramids.
我想去埃及旅行看看金字塔。

lame

adj.瘸的，【借口或解释】无说服力的，站不住脚的，蹩脚的
He walked into the hospital with a lame leg.
他跛着腿走进医院。

fibre

n.纤维（物质），性格，质地
We use optical fibre to channel information effectively.
我们运用光纤来有效传播信息。

lorry

n.卡车，运货汽车
All the stuff was carried in the lorry.
所有货物都搬上卡车了。

undivided

adj.（注意力）专一的，（国家）没分开
He givies undivided attention to his work and doesn’t hear the phone ringing.
他专心致志地工作，并没有听到手机铃声。
