复习

besides

prep.除…之外
adv.而且，还有
As the winner, I received lots of money besides the cup.
作为胜利者，我除了得到了奖杯外，还获得了一大笔钱。

associate

v.联想，联合，联系，交往
n.伙伴，同事
adj.副的
I associate this picture with a star.
这图片让我联想到了星星。

suspect

n.嫌疑犯，可疑分子
v.怀疑，猜疑，推测
adj.可疑的
The suspect was body-searched by a policeman.
警察对嫌疑犯进行了搜身。

perfume

n.香水
v.使充满芳香
This perfume smells like flowers.
这个香水闻起来像花一样。

anxiety

n.焦虑
It is difficult to disguise her great anxiety.
很难掩饰她严重的焦虑。

botanical

adj.植物（学）的
I went to the botanical gardens to see the beautiful plants.
我们去了植物园欣赏那些美丽的植物。

league

n.社团，联盟
v.使…结盟，与…联合
This is a group photo of our football league.
这是我们足球社团的一张合影。

rough

adj.粗糙的
The hands of the farmer are rough.
这个农民的手很粗糙。

confuse

v.混淆，使混乱，使迷糊
I always confuse the girl with her twin sister.
我一直把那个女孩和她的双胞胎姐姐搞混淆。

fancy

adj.华丽装饰的，别致的，引人注目的
n.喜爱，幻想，想象力
vt.想象，幻想
This fancy gold tableware must have been so expensive!
这套华丽的金色餐具肯定很贵！

organize

v.整理，组织
Please organize the cards by number.
请按编号整理好卡片。

custom

n.风俗
adj.定制的
It’s our custom to visit relatives during the Spring Festival.
春节期间走亲访友是我们的一种习俗。

hooray

excl.万岁
Hooray! The exams are over!
万岁！考试结束了！

litter

n.垃圾
v.乱丢
Lots of litter was left on the beach by tourists.
沙滩上有很多游客留下的垃圾。

possess

v.拥有，占有，缠住，迷住
Take the key and now you possess a house.
把钥匙拿去。现在你已经拥有一套房子了。

instant

adj.即食的
n.瞬间
Making a cup of instant coffee is a simple process.
制作一杯速溶咖啡的过程很简单。

hatch

v.孵出，孵
n.船舱盖
Look, the eggs are hatching!
看啊，小鸡出壳了！

convenience

n.方便
Sometimes I eat pre-cooked meals for convenience.
有时为了方便，我会吃事先做好的饭菜。

anchor

n.锚，担任（广播电视新闻节目）的主持人
The ship dropped the anchor and hit the shark on head.
船抛出锚，打到了一只鲨鱼。

dilemma

n.进退两难的窘境
I am in a dilemma. Both ways are so dangerous!
我处在进退两难的困境里，两条路都好危险啊！

institution

n.机构
The United Nations is an international institution.
联合国是一家国际机构。

violence

n.暴力
The two boys used violence to end the quarrel.
这两个男孩动用武力结束了争吵。

offshore

adj.近海的，海岸的
adv.离岸地，近海地
Offshore oil extraction is the best way to get oil.
近海原油开采是得到石油的最好方式。

refreshments

n.点心，茶点
Help yourself to the refreshments on the table.
请自取桌上的茶点。

unconscious

adj.不省人事，未察觉的
We found him unconscious on the floor.
我们发现他倒在地上，不省人事。

devote

vt.奉献
The woman devotes her entire life to her religion.
这个女人把一辈子都献给了宗教信仰。

donate

v.捐赠
We donated a sum of money to the charity.
我们向慈善机构捐了一笔钱。

comrade

n.战友
My comrade and I are good friends.
我和我战友是好朋友。

gradually

adv.逐步地
The full moon gradually changes to a new moon.
圆月会逐渐变成新月。

expect

v.期待
She’s expecting her baby to be born soon.
她期待着宝宝的到来。

sheet

n.纸张，被单
Please write your name on this sheet of paper.
请在这张纸上写下你的名字。

alphabet

n.字母表
The English alphabet has 26 letters.
英语字母表有26个字母。

guarantee

v.保证，担保
n.保证金
I guarantee that you will have our best service.
我保证你会得到我们最优质的服务。

indeed

adv.的确，真正地，事实上
My coworkers told me that I did a good indeed.
我的同事告诉我真的干的不错！

achievement

n.成绩，成就，实现，完成
He is known for his great achievements in the sport of tennis.
他在网球运动上的成就令人瞩目。

quite

adv.相当，十分
My hair is quite soft and smooth.
我的头发很柔顺。

transparent

adj.透明的，清澈的，明净的
The bear didn’t see the transparent glass, so he hit it.
小熊没看见这块透明的玻璃，所以就撞上了。

willingness

n.自愿
The student raised his hand to show his willingness to clean the blackboard.
这位学生举手表示自愿去擦黑板。

accessible

adj.可进入的，可使用的
The building is only accessible if you have a card.
有卡才能进入这栋大楼。

temporary

adj.暂时的，临时的
She looks happy with her new temporary tattoo.
她对这个临时的新纹身很满意。

absorb

vt.吸收，汲取
Sponges can absorb a lot of water.
海绵可以吸收很多水。

tentative

adj.试探性的，犹豫不决的
When walking in darkness, every step you take is tentative.
在黑暗中行走时，你走的每一步都是试探性的。

quarrel

n.争吵，口角
v.争吵，争论
I had a terrible quarrel with my wife.
我跟我的妻子大吵了一架。

resist

v.抵抗，抵制
The overweight girl managed to resist the temptation of hamburgers.
这个胖女孩抵制住了汉堡的诱惑。

evident

adj.显然的，明显的，明白的
It is evident who the real murderer is.
真正的凶手是谁很明显。

embassy

n.大使馆，大使馆全体人员
The US embassy in the U.K. is located in London.
美国驻英大使馆位于伦敦。

beneath

prep.在…下方
adv.在下方
Most of the iceberg is beneath the surface.
冰山的大部分在海面之下。

politician

n.政治家，政客
The female politician is giving an election speech.
女政治家正为竞选做演讲。

variety

n.品种，种类，变化，多样化，多样性
There is a wide variety of fruits on the table.
桌子上有各种各样的水果。

drum

n.鼓，圆桶
v.击鼓
The little guy played that song on the drums.
这个小家伙用鼓演奏出那首曲子。

vehicle

n.交通工具，机动车
The vehicle drives off while carrying a container.
那辆车载着一个集装箱开走了。

excellent

adj.优秀的，极好的
He’s an excellent student who always gets excellent scores.
他是个优秀的学生，总是得高分。

curriculum

n.课程，（学校或专业的）全部课程
The curriculum includes math, science and English.
这些课程包含数学、科学和英语。

evidence

n.证据，证明
v.证明
They are gathering evidence to solve the case.
为了侦破案件，他们正在收集证据。

recipe

n.食谱
She follows the recipe to cook.
她照着食谱来做饭。

extreme

adj.极端的
n.极端
The roller coaster is an extreme test of courage.
过山车是对勇气的极端考验。

yummy

adj.美味的，好吃的
That pizza looks so yummy, give me a slice!
这个披萨看起来太美味了，给我一片吧！

cushion

n.坐垫
v.减轻，缓和
There are several chshions on the bed. It’s confortable.
床上有几个垫子，很舒服呢。

destination

n.目的地，终点
The destination of their journey is the west.
他们的目的地是西天。

grand

adj.壮丽的，宏伟的，重大的
n.一千美元，大钢琴
This grand building has always been an attraction for tourists.
这座宏伟的建筑一直吸引着游客前来参观。

inspire

n.激发，激励，使生灵感
His speech inspired so many people to fight for equality.
他的演讲激励了很多人为平等而奋斗。

disappointed

adj.失望的
He was disappointed that his favorite football team lost.
他很失望，因为他最喜欢的足球队输了。

proper

adj.得体的，正确的，完全的
At a cocktail party, you should wear a proper outfit.
在酒会上，你应该穿得体的衣服。

poisonous

adj.有毒的
One bite from a poisonous snake could kill you.
被毒蛇咬一口就能要你的命。

statesman

n.政治家
Statesmen always capture the attention of media.
政治家总是受到媒体的关注。

relate

v.相关，有联系，讲述
The family tree shows everyone I’m related to.
这个家谱显示了和我有关联的所有人。

sauce

n.酱，调味汁
Wow! The tomato sauce in the bowl looks so delicious.
哇！碗里的番茄酱看起来很好吃的样子。

disease

n.疾病
The man has a heart disease. Get him to the hospital!
这个男人有心脏病，快送他去医院！

desperate

adj.绝望的，孤注一掷的
The unemployed man looks so desperate.
那个失业的男人看上去如此的绝望。

bound

adj.受约束的，有义务的
v.束缚，跳跃着前行
n.跳跃
These ropes are bound to the ring.
这些绳子和铁环绑在了一起。

buffet

n.自助餐，（火车内）餐室，冷餐桌
v.反复敲打，打击，重击
You can eat as much as you like at the buffet.
吃自助餐的时候，你可以想吃多少就吃多少。

physician

n.内科医生
My physician told me to eat more fruits and vegetables.
我的内科医生告诉我要多吃水果和蔬菜。

racial

adj.种族的
Racial origin has nothing to do with making friends.
种族背景毫不影响结交朋友。

deposit

v.存款，存放
n.存款，定金
I deposit money in my piggy bank when I can.
当我有钱我会往我的存钱罐里存钱。

chest

n.胸膛
We can see his strong chest under his shirt.
我们可以看到他衬衫下强壮的胸膛。

training

n.训练，培养
You should be prepared before training.
训练前你得准备好。

sniff

v.嗅，吸气
n.嗅，吸气（声）
You might see dogs at the airport sniffing for illegal drugs.
你可能会在机场看见狗在嗅毒品的味道。

accelerate

v.加速，增长，使增速，促进
The car accelerated from 60km/h to 100km/h.
汽车从60km/h加速到了100km/h。

quantity

n.数量，数目
I eat a large quantity of food every day.
我每天都吃大量的食物。

meaning

n.意义，意思
This word has so many different meanings.
这个单词有好多不同的意思。

pancake

n.烙饼，薄饼
In the west, they like to eat pancakes for breakfast.
在西方，人们早餐喜欢吃薄烤饼。

pile

n.一堆
v.堆积，累积
What a tall pile of books!
多么高的一摞书！

awkward

adj.尴尬的，不方便的
He felt a bit awkward when receiving a bunch of crops.
收到一束大麦，他有点尴尬。

petrol

n.（英）汽油
“Please fill my car up with petrol!” the driver said.
司机说：”麻烦加满油！”

complex

adj.复杂的，难解的
n.建筑群，情结
Modern road system can be very complex.
现代道路系统十分复杂。

glory

n.光荣，荣誉
Winning the first prize brings him much glory.
得了第一名他感到非常光荣。

sculpture

n.雕刻，雕塑
The lion sculpture is made of stone.
狮子雕塑是用石头刻的。
