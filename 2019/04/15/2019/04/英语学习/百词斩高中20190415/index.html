复习

wire

n.电缆，电线，金属线
Look, many birds are sitting on the wires!
看，有很多鸟站在电线上呢！

tense

adj.（神经）紧张的
v.使拉紧，拉紧
n.时态
I feel really tense about the coming exam.
我真的很担心即将来临的考试。

astronaut

n.宇航员
I would love to be an astronaut and go to space.
我想成为一名宇航员并进入太空。

grocery

n.食品杂货店
Every day she goes shopping in the grocery store.
她每天都去食品杂货店购物。

apologise

vi.道歉，认错
I should apologise to my friend for yelling at him.
我应该向我朋友道歉，因为我对他大声吼叫了。

suspension

n.悬浮，暂停
In outer space, things are in a state of suspension.
在外太空，东西处于悬浮状态。

conscience

n.良心，良知
My conscience always tells me to do something right.
我的良心经常告诉我要做对的事情。

cast

v.投，掷，放钓
n.演员表，演员阵容
He’s going to cast that rock by the seaside.
他在海边准备把石块投掷出去。

helicopter

n.直升机
We took a helicopter ride over the sea.
我们搭乘直升机飞过大海。

abortion

n.堕胎，流产
She wants an abortion because she doesn’t want the baby.
她想堕胎，因为她不想要宝宝。

monument

n.纪念碑，遗迹，遗址
The Washington Monument stands in the center of Washington DC.
华盛顿纪念碑矗立在华盛顿市中心。

starve

v.（使）饿死，挨饿
The bear is starving, it doesn’t have enough food to eat.
这只熊快饿死了，它没有食物吃。

tick

v.用勾作为记号，发出嘀嗒声
n.勾号，（钟表）嘀嗒声，一刹那
I tick the boxes one by one.
我挨个在勾选框打了勾。

splendid

adj.壮观的，辉煌的，华丽的
Look at this splendid sunset! I love this place.
看这落日多美啊！我真喜欢这里。

servant

n.仆人
This is my servant with the drinks.
这是我送饮料的仆人。

mess

n.凌乱状态，混乱状态
v.浪费时间，瞎闹
My dog made a mess in the room.
我的狗狗把房间里弄得乱七八糟的。

architecture

n.（总称）建筑物，建筑学，建筑体系，结构
The bright colors is one feature of ancient Chinese architecture.
艳丽的色彩是中国古建筑的特点之一。

retire

v.退休，退役
The retired couple are traveling together.
这对已经退休的夫妻正在一起旅行。

demand

v.强烈要求，需要
n.要求（坚决的），需求
The bad guy demanded $1,000,000, or he would kill the boy.
坏人要求100万赎金，否则他就要撕票。

currency

n.货币，流通
In different countries, we must use a different currency to buy things.
在不同的国家，我们得用不同的货币来买东西。

punctuate

v.加标点于，强调
Which one do I use to punctuate my sentence?
这个句子里我该用哪个标点呢？

balcony

n.阳台，剧院楼厅
I like to stand on my apartment’s balcony.
我喜欢站在公寓的阳台上。

assess

v.评估
How do you assess the risk level of this investment? Low, medium or hight?
你对这次投资的风险评估是怎样的？低，中等，还是高风险？

petrol

n.（英）汽油
“Please fill my car up with petrol!” the driver said.
司机说：”麻烦加满油！”

glory

n.光荣，荣誉
Winning the first prize brings him much glory.
得了第一名他感到非常光荣。

sculpture

n.雕刻，雕塑
The lion sculpture is made of stone.
狮子雕塑是用石头刻的。

新的学习

cautious

adj.（of）小心的，谨慎的
The maid is very cautious not to make a mistake in front of the empress.
这个丫鬟在皇后面前非常谨慎，不敢犯任何错误。

blanket

n.毯子，覆盖层
adj.不加区别的
vt.用毯子盖
The baby is covered with a blanket to keep it warm.
为了保暖，宝宝盖上毛毯。

liquid

n.液体
adj.液体的
Water is a kind of liquid without color.
水是一种没有颜色的液体。

flour

n.面粉，粉状物质
There is a bag of flour.
这里有一包面粉。

enquiry

n.询问，问讯处
I make enquiries at the information desk.
我在咨询台咨询了一些事情。

budget

n.预算
v.预算，安排
adj.廉价的
I don’t have much money for the budget.
我的钱不够预算。

drill

v.钻孔，在…上钻孔
n.钻头
The worker drills into the wall.
工人在墙上钻孔。

sacred

adj.神圣的，宗教（性）的
In India, the elephant is a sacred animal.
在印度，大象被视为神圣的动物。

allergic

adj.过敏的，对…讨厌的
Look at his face! The poor guy was allergic to seafood.
看他的脸，这个可怜的家伙对海鲜过敏。

muddy

adj.泥泞的，浑浊的
v.显得扑朔迷离
It’ll take hours to drive down this muddy road.
驶过这段泥泞小路要花好几个小时。

athletic

adj.运动的，体育的，强壮的
The athletic man runs for long distances without needing to rest.
这个运动员可以跑很长的距离，并且不用休息。

equipment

n.设备
High-tech equipment is used in the factory.
工厂运用高科技设备。

neat

adj.整洁的，不掺水的
The folded clothes look very neat in the drawer.
抽屉里折叠起来的衣服看起来很整洁。

giraffe

n.长颈鹿
I wish I was tall and thin like a giraffe!
我喜欢自己像长颈鹿一样又高又瘦。

sorrow

n.悲伤，苦恼，伤心
v.感到悲伤，为…悲痛
Her tears are full of sorrow.
她的眼泪充满悲伤。

assessment

n.评估
The reson for the audit is to make an assessment about the data.
审计的职责是分析数据并作出评估。

iron

n.熨斗
adj.刚强的
v.烫（衣），熨
Use an iron to straighten clothing.
用熨斗把衣服熨平。

ash

n.灰，灰烬
The ashtray is full of ash.
这个烟灰缸里满是烟灰。

advertisement

n.广告，登广告
This advertisement is to promote a new product.
这则广告的目的是推广新产品。

settle

v.安顿，调停
They are getting settled in their new house.
他们在新房子里安顿下来。

symptom

n.症状
Flu symptoms include a headache, sore throat and a fever.
流感的症状包括头痛、嗓子痛和发烧。

inn

n.客栈，小旅馆
This traditional inn is surrounded by trees.
这个传统的小旅店被绿树环绕。

nervous

adj.紧张的，焦虑不安的
The father is nervous about his baby’s birth.
这个父亲因为宝宝的出生很紧张。

bungalow

n.平房，小屋
I live in a little bungalow by the beach.
我住在沙滩边的小屋里。

barbecue

v.烧烤，红烧
n.烤肉架
My favorite food is barbecued meat.
我最喜欢的食物是烤肉。
