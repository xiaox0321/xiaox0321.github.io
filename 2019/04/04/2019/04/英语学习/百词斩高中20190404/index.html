复习

civil

adj.公民的，民用的
The right to vote is an important civil right.
投票权是一项重要的公民权利。

temptation

n.引诱，诱惑
Faced with the temptation of money, he said no!
面对钱的诱惑，他说了不。

laser

n.激光，激光器
They are playing laser tag game.
他们在玩激光束游戏。

campaign

n.（社会性、政治性）活动，运动
v.从事活动，参加活动
Many people came to support the president’s campaign.
很多人都来这儿支持总统的竞选活动。

sickness

n.疾病
Her sickness caused frequent nosebleeds.
她的病导致她频繁地流鼻血。

gown

n.女礼服，女裙服
The bride wore a beautiful white wedding gown.
新娘穿着漂亮的白婚纱。

settler

n.移民者，定居者
The first settlers in Australia were from Britain.
最早一批移民澳大利亚的是英国人。

selfish

adj.自私的，利己的，自私自利的
The selfish boy doesn’t share the candies with other kids.
这个自私的小男孩不跟其他小朋友分享糖果。

envelope

n.信封，包装物，外壳，膜，气囊
The sender put the letter into the red envelope.
寄信的人把信放在了红色信封里。

consideration

n.体贴，体谅，顾及，考虑，思考
She shows a lot of consideration for her mother.
她很体贴她的母亲。

realize

v.认识到，体会到
“So that’s how the word is spelled!” The little boy finally realized.
男孩终于意识到，”原来这个单词是这样拼的！”

digital

adj.数字的，数码的，数字显示的
A digital stopwatch is very precise.
数字秒表非常精确。

precious

adj.珍贵的，宝贵的
The crown is decorated with precious diamonds.
这个皇冠上装饰着珍贵的钻石。

arrest

v.逮捕，拘留
n.逮捕，拘留
The police officer arrested the man for drunk driving.
警察以酒后驾车为由逮捕这个人。

atmosphere

n.大气层，空气
The atmosphere can protect the earth from solar rays.
大气层可以保护地球免受太阳射线的伤害。

redirect

v.改变方向，转变用途
The signs are there to redirect traffic.
这些标志是放在这调整车流方向的。

obtain

v.获得，赢的，取得
The policeman is obtaining evidence from the crime scene.
警察正在犯罪现场取证。

immediately

adv.立即，马上
“Yes sir, I’ll be there immediately!”
“大人，小的这就来！”

tolerate

v.忍受，容忍
They have to tolerate the smell in the elevator.
他们不得不忍受电梯里的臭味。

appendix

n.阑尾
She has an acute pain in her appendix.
她的阑尾痛得厉害。

argument

n.争吵，争论，辩论
The man and the woman are having an argument.
这个男人和这个女人之间有争吵。

recreation

n.娱乐活动，消遣
Old people like to play mah-jong for recreation.
老年人喜欢玩麻将来作为消遣。

terror

n.恐怖，惊骇
They shouted in terror when they saw the spider.
突然看见蜘蛛，他们吓得尖叫起来。

allocate

v.分配，配给
Allocate one piece of cake to each person.
给每个人都分一块蛋糕。

bay

n.海湾，港湾
I like to watch the bay and the mountains from here.
我喜欢从这里眺望山和海湾。

rough

adj.粗糙的
The hands of the farmer are rough.
这个农民的手很粗糙。

confuse

v.混淆，使混乱，使迷糊
I always confuse the girl with her twin sister.
我一直把那个女孩和她的双胞胎姐姐搞混淆。

fancy

adj.华丽装饰的，别致的，引人注目的
n.喜爱，幻想，想象力
vt.想象，幻想
This fancy gold tableware must have been so expensive!
这套华丽的金色餐具肯定很贵！

custom

n.风俗
adj.定制的
It’s our custom to visit relaives during the Spring Festival.
春节期间走亲访友是我们的一种习俗。

possess

v.拥有，占有，缠住，迷住
Take the key and now you possess a house.
把钥匙拿去。现在你已经拥有一套房子了。

新的学习

ambassador

n.（特命全权）大使
“Our country wishes to have peace,” said the ambassador.
“我们国家渴望和平”大使说道。

bark

v.狗吠
n.树皮
It scares me when the dog barks.
这狗叫的时候吓死我了。

army

n.陆军，军队，大群，大军
They are soldiers of an ancient Viking army.
他们是一只古代维京军队的士兵。

section

n.部分
v.划分
The chart has five sections in different colors.
图表有颜色不同的五个部分。

scarf

n.围巾，披肩
She tied a grey scarf around her neck.
她脖子上围了一条灰色的围巾。

commit

v.犯（罪），进行（自杀），承诺，保证
He committed a crime, so he was handcuffed.
他犯了罪，所以被拷了起来。

percentage

n.百分比，百分率
GPD fell by four percentage points this quarter - what a disaster!
这个季度GPD下降了4个百分点，真是一场灾难！

slave

n.奴隶，向奴隶般受制的人
v.奴隶般地工作，苦干
These slaves are chained together.
这几个奴隶被锁在了一起。

qualification

n.资格证明，条件，限制性条件
I’m delighted that I received this qualification from my university.
拿到了大学毕业证，我很开心。

catastrophe

n.大灾难
We lost our house in the catastrophe.
我们在灾难中失去家园。

academic

adj.学术的，学院的，大学的
n.学者
They are having an academic meeting about physics.
他们正在开一个物理领域的学术会议。

restriction

n.限制，约束
There is a restriction on fishing here.
这里限制垂钓。

agricultural

adj.农业的，农艺的
Agricultural land is important for growing crops.
农田对于种庄稼来说很重要。

brilliant

adj.聪颖的，卓越的，有才气的
The brilliant girl can solve the math problems easily.
这个聪明的女孩可以轻松解决这些数学难题。

slim

adj.苗条的
v.（用运动、节食等）减轻体重，变苗条
Ever since she started her diet, she has had a very slim figure.
她的身材自从节食开始就很苗条了。

claw

n.爪，钳
vi.抓，挖
The cat has sharp claws.
这只猫的爪子很锋利。

wheat

n.小麦
Farmers grow wheat in the field.
农民们在地里种小麦。

mountainous

adj.多山的，巨大的
Airplanes cannot land in this mountainous area.
飞机不能在这片山区着陆。

harvest

n.收获
v.收割
The farmer is happy about the harvest of his rice.
农民对稻子的收获感到高兴。

fan

n.扇子，风扇，（影，球等）迷
Turning the fan on in the summer can keep you cool.
在夏天把风扇打开就可以保持清凉。

gale

n.狂风，一阵（笑声）
A sudden gale almost knocked me down.
一阵狂风差点把我吹倒。

underwear

n.内衣裤
Underwear is what you wear under your normal clothes.
内衣是你穿在普通衣服里面的衣服。

scissors

n.剪刀
He uses scissors to cut his hair.
他用剪刀给自己剪头发。

nursery

n.托儿所
The babies are well taken care of in the nursery.
孩子们在托儿所中被照顾得很好。

gas

n.煤气
Careful! Those tanks are full of gas.
小心！那些罐子里装满了煤气。

enlarge

v.扩大，扩展，放大
I’ll use my computer to enlarge these photos.
我会用电脑来放大这些图片。

disadvantage

n.不利，不利地位，弱点
The one ant is at a disadvantage against so many others.
这一只蚂蚁在和其他许多蚂蚁的对抗中处于劣势。

tasteless

adj.无味的，枯燥无味的
Noodles cooked in plain boiled water are tasteless.
这种清水面食之无味。

reform

v.改革，革新
n.改革，革新
The civil rights movement reformed American democracy.
民权运动改革了美国的民主制度。

judgement

n.审判，判决，判断力，识别力，看法，意见
In the court’s judgement, I was guilty.
依法院判决，我是有罪的。

religion

n.宗教信仰，虔诚的追求
His religion taught him to be kind.
他的宗教信仰教他要善良。
