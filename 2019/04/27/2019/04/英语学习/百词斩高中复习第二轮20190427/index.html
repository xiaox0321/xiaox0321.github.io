复习

representative

n.代表
adj.典型的，有代表性的
They chose her as their representative to speak for them.
他们选她作为代表为他们发声。

promote

v.提升
She worked hard and was soon promoted.
她工作勤奋，不久就要得到提升了。

plastic

adj.塑料的
n.塑料，塑料制品
Plastic cups and bowls are safer for babies than glass ones.
塑料的杯和碗对于宝宝来说比玻璃的更安全。

beneficial

adj.有益的，有利的
Eating nurtitious food and exercising are beneficial to our health.
吃有营养的食物和锻炼身体的对我们的健康有益。

profit

n.利润，盈利，益处
v.得益，有益于
The girl made a profit by selling the dress.
这个女孩卖掉这件裙子，赚了一笔。

revision

n.修改，修订本
He made a lot of revisions in his novel.
他对他的小说做了很多的修改。

convince

v.使确信
The footprints convinced the wife that the cat made the mess.
地上的脚印使女主人确信是猫把房间弄乱的。

consult

v.请教，咨询
She is consulting the doctor about her illness.
她正向医生咨询她的病情。

potential

n.潜力，潜能
adj.潜在的，可能的
he has the potential to be very strong.
他又变得非常强壮的潜力。

starvation

n.饥饿
I was suffering from starvation and exhaustion after heavy work.
繁重的工作后，我又累又饿。

amusement

n.娱乐，消遣
This amusement park is still open at night.
这座游乐园晚上也会开放。

merciful

adj.慈善的，仁慈的，宽大的
The old man is merciful so he let turtles free.
老人很仁慈，他放生了海龟。

decade

n.十年，十年间
People can change a lot after a decade.
十年会给人带来很大的变化。

fragrant

adj.香的，芬芳的
The air is fragrant with the scent of coffee.
空气中弥漫着咖啡香。

appetite

n.食欲，胃口
Wow, what a huge appetite you have!
天哪，你的胃口真大！

allowance

n.零用钱，津贴，补贴
He has an allowance of 3,000 dollars every month.
他每月有3000刀零用钱。

adventure

n.冒险，冒险活动
The boy went on an adventure in the jungle.
这个男孩到丛林里冒险了。

compromise

n.妥协，折中
v.使遭受危害
The seller made a compromise and sold the dress at 50 yuan.
商家做出了妥协，50元卖出了那条裙子。

advantage

n.优势，利益，有利条件
The rocket gives him an unfair advantage!
火箭给了他不公平的优势。

appoint

v.委派，任命，约定，确定，指定
My boss has appointed me to manage this project.
我的老板任命我来管理这个项目。

stranger

n.陌生人
Kids, be careful of stranger offering candy!
小朋友，小心拿糖给你吃的陌生人。

fasten

v.扎牢，扣住
We should all fasten our seat belts before I start the car.
在开车之前，我们都应该系牢安全带。

regret

vt.懊悔，后悔
n.后悔，遗憾
I really regret not finishing school.
我好后悔没有完成学业。

virtue

n.美德，德行
Helping the old is a traditional virtue of China.
帮助老人是中华名族的传统美德。

courage

n.勇气，胆量，胆识
He jumped off with great courage.
他勇敢地一跃而下。

innocent

adj.天真的，单纯的
The new born baby looks innocent when he smiles.
这个新生儿笑的时候看起来好单纯。

scholar

n.学者，公费生，有奖学金的学生
She’s so young, but she’s quite the little scholar.
她很小，但是已经是一个小小的学者了。

ample

adj.富足的，丰满的，宽敞的
The kitchen and dining room are of ample size.
这间房子的厨房和餐厅很宽敞。
