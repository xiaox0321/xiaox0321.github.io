复习

challenging

adj.具有挑战性的，引起挑战性兴趣的
The bike trick he’s attempting is extremely challenging!
他正尝试的自行车特技真是相当的有挑战性啊！

steward

n.乘务员，服务员
The stewards are responsible fro serving passengers on the plane.
空乘人员负责为飞机上的乘客提供服务。

corner

n.角落
v.将…逼入困境
The dog is in the corner facing the wall.
那只狗在角落里面壁。

tournament

n.锦标赛，联赛
The basketball tournament will end with a single winner.
这次篮球联赛会只有一个获胜队伍。

explode

v.爆炸，激增，爆发，使爆炸，驳倒
The fireworks exploded in the air.
烟花在空中爆炸开来。

trick

n.恶作剧，诡计，技艺，戏法
v.哄骗
He played a trick on his friend.
他捉弄了一下他的朋友。

grateful

adj.感激的
I gave my mom flowers to show that I’m grateful to her.
我送妈妈一朵花来表示感谢。

mentally

adv.精神上，智力上，心理上
She looks as if she is mentally tired.
她看起来精神疲惫。

envy

n.妒忌，妒忌的对象
v.嫉妒，羡慕
The little puppy envies the big dog who has a ball to play with.
小狗嫉妒那只有球可以玩的大狗。

debate

n.辩论，讨论
v.辩论，议论
The debate between the man and the woman is so fierce.
这个男人和这个女人间的辩论太激烈了。

pretend

v.假装，装扮
I pretended to study hard, but actually I chatted on my phone.
我假装很努力地学习，但是实际上我在手机上聊天。

particular

adj.特别的，不寻常的
n.详细情况
You should pay particular attention to spelling.
你需要特别注意你的拼写。

satisfaction

n.满足，满意，乐事，补偿
I gave their service a good rating to show my satisfaction.
我给了好评，来表示我对他们的服务很满意。

illegal

adj.不合法的，非法的
Occupying a bus lane is illegal.
占用公交车道是违法行为！

windbreaker

n.风衣，防风（皮）夹克
Her windbreaker jacket protected her from the wind and the rain.
她的防风皮夹克能帮她挡风遮雨。

blind

adj.瞎的，盲目的
v.使失明
n.盲点，百叶窗
The blind woman is crossing the street with her guide dog.
这位失明的女士正和她的导盲犬一起过街。

conduct

v.指挥，进行，实施，处理，传导（电、热等），护送
n.行为，举止，指挥，带领
He is conducting an orchestra.
他正指挥着交响乐团。

temptation

n.引诱，诱惑
Faced with the temptation of money, he said no!
面对钱的诱惑，他说了不。

laser

n.激光，激光器
They are playing laser tag game.
他们在玩激光束游戏。

sickness

n.疾病
Her sickness caused frequent nosebleeds.
她的病导致她频繁地流鼻血。

settler

n.移民者，定居者
The first settlers in Australia were from Britain.
最早一批移民澳大利亚的是英国人。

envelope

n.信封，包装物，外壳，膜，气囊
The sender put the letter into the red envelope.
寄信的人把信放在了红色信封里。

consideration

n.体贴，体谅，顾及，考虑，思考
She shows a lot of consideration for her mother.
她很体贴她的母亲。

tent

n.帐篷
The couple put up a tent beside the river.
那对夫妇在河边支起了帐篷。

precious

adj.珍贵的，宝贵的
The crown is decorated with precious diamonds.
这个皇冠上装饰着珍贵的钻石。

arrest

v.逮捕，拘留
n.逮捕，拘留
The police officerr arrested the man for drunk driving.
警察以酒后驾车为由逮捕这个人。

obtain

v.获得，赢得，取得
The policeman is obtaining evidence from the crime scene.
警察正在犯罪现场取证。

tolerate

v.忍受，容忍
They have to tolerate the smell in the elevator.
他们不得不忍受电梯里的臭味。

recreation

n.娱乐活动,消遣
Old people like to play mah-jong for recreation.
老年人喜欢玩麻将来作为消遣。

skyscraper

n.摩天大楼
The skyscraper is hiding the sun!
这栋大楼把太阳都遮住了。

allocate

v.分配，配给
Allocate one piece of cake to each person.
给每个人都分一块蛋糕。

bay

n.海湾，港湾
I like to watch the bay and the mountains from here.
我喜欢从这里眺望山和海湾。

ambassador

n.（特命全权）大使
“Our country wishes to have peace”, said the ambassador.
“我们国家渴望和平”大使说道。

section

n.部分
v.划分
The chart has five sections in different colors.
图表有颜色不同的五个部分。

qualification

n.资格证明，条件，限制性条件
I’m delighted that I received this qualification from my university.
拿到了大学毕业证，我很开心。

catastrophe

n.大灾难
We lost our house in the catastrophe.
我们在灾难中失去家园。

academic

adj.学术的，学院的，大学的
n.学者
They are having an academic meeting about physics.
他们正在开一个物理领域的学术会议。

restriction

n.限制，约束
There is a restriction on finishing here.
这里限制垂钓。

agricultural

adj.农业的，农艺的
Agricultural land is important for growing crops.
农田对于种庄稼来说很重要。

brilliant

adj.聪颖的，卓越的，有才气的
The brilliant girl can solve the math problems easily.
这个聪明的女孩可以轻松解决这些数学难题。

disadvantage

n.不利，不利位置，弱点
The one ant is at a disadvantage against so many others.
这一只蚂蚁在和其他许多蚂蚁的对抗中处于劣势。

religion

n.宗教信仰，虔诚的追求
His religion taught him to be kind.
他的宗教信仰教他要善良。

referee

n.裁判员，仲裁者
The soccer referee took out a Red card.
足球裁判拿出了一张红牌。

acid

n.【化学】酸
adj.含酸过多的
Make sure not to touch the acid - it will burn.
千万不要去硼酸，它会灼伤你。

appointment

n.约会，会面，接受委派者
I have an appointment for tomorrow with my doctor.
我和医生约了明天见面。

punctual

adj.准时的，守时的
I’m always punctual, I’m never late for work.
我总是很守时，上班从来不迟到。

plain

adj.朴素的，不尚修饰的
I like plain T-shirts as they are simple and cheap.
我喜欢朴素的T恤，因为他们简约又便宜。

offense

n.犯罪，冒犯，过错，触怒，犯规，进攻
He committed a serious offense when breaking into the car.
他打破车玻璃的时候犯了严重的罪。

avenue

n.林荫道，道路
This long avenue leads to a big country house.
这条长林荫道通往一栋巨大的乡间别墅。

dull

adj.枯燥的，沉闷的
The TV show is so dull that makes him want to sleep.
电视节目无趣地让他想睡觉。

needle

n.针，针状物，指针
Sew these buttons on using this needle.
用这根针把扣子缝上。

conclusion

n.结论，推论
I finally drew a conclusion from these facts.
我终于从这些因素中得出了一条结论。

panic

n.慌乱
v.使…惊慌失措
After she fell, she reached for her shoes in a panic.
跌倒之后她慌张地捡起了鞋子。

district

n.地区，区域
The city has four districts.
这座城市由四个区域组成。

abrupt

adj.突然的，鲁莽的
The abrupt bang gave him a fright.
气球突然爆炸，把他吓了一跳。

minister

n.部长，大臣
The government’s minister gave a speech.
政府部长在演讲。

pole

n.杆，竿极（点）
The telephone pole holds the cables.
电线杆支撑着电缆。

caution

n.警告
v.劝…谨慎小心
Caution: the road is dangerous ahead.
警告：前方道路危险。

patent

n.专利
adj.显然的，明显的，独创的
v.取得…的专利权
The maker of this vehicle claimed a patent on the design of the first car.
这辆车的制造者申请了首辆汽车设计专利。

inform

v.通知，向…报告，赋特征于
Please inform us of the news as soon as you know.
请你一有消息就通知我们。

finance

v.资金供给，筹措资金
n.金融，财政，资金
The old man financed the poor student’s education.
老人资助那个贫困学生上大学。

elect

v.选举，推选，选择
We elect our next leader by voting.
我们通过投票来选举下一任领导。

dizzy

adj.眩晕的，头晕目眩的，头昏眼花的
vt.使头晕目眩
I felt so dizzy that I went to the hospital.
我头太晕了，所以我去看医生了。

shame

n.羞耻，羞愧
vt.使感到羞耻
She covers her face in shame.
她羞愧地遮住了脸。

resigh

v.辞职
After much consideration, the employee resigned from his job.
深思熟虑后，这位员工辞职了。

postpone

v.延迟，推迟，延缓
I had to postpone the meeting until later.
我不得不向后推迟一点这个会议。
