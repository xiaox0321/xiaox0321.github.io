复习

solid

adj.固体的，结实的
n.固体
Ice is the solid state of water.
冰是水的固体状态。

lounge

n.候机室，休息室，酒吧间，懒散的步子
v.（懒散地）倚，躺
People sit in the lounge waiting for boarding.
人们坐在候机室里等待登机。

dip

v.浸，蘸
n.倾斜，下降
I like to dip the cookie into the milk.
我喜欢把饼干蘸牛奶吃。

considerate

adj.体贴的，考虑周到的
He is a considerate husband reminded his wife to take an umbrella.
他是一位体贴的丈夫，会提醒妻子带伞。

tube

n.管子，管道
These tubes are made of steel.
这些管子都是钢做的。

pest

n.害虫，讨厌的人（或东西）
Pests are annoying and harmful bugs.
害虫是讨厌又有害的昆虫。

heaven

n.天堂，极乐世界
The angels opened the door to heaven.
天使打开了天堂的门。

sleeve

n.袖子
One sleeve is too long while the other is fine.
这只袖子太长了点，但另一只还好。

seaweed

n.海草，海藻
Seaweed grows in deep areas of the ocean.
这种海藻生长在深海中。

stainless

adj.不生锈的，不锈钢的
These spoons and forks are made of stainless steel.
勺子和叉子是由不锈钢制成的。

frequent

adj.时常的，频繁的
Her frequent online shopping costs her a lot of money.
频繁地网上购物花了她很多钱。

anecdote

n.轶事，趣闻
The anecdote is about the mysterious creature in the ancient times.
这桩轶事是关于古代的神兽的。

dimension

n.维度，方面
These shapes have three dimensions.
这些图形有三个维度。

aid

n.救护，援助
v.帮助，援助，有助于
The first aid kit is provided by the Red Cross.
这个急救箱是红十字会提供的。

aware

adj.意识到的，知道的
He suddenly became aware of how to figure out the problem.
他突然知道该怎么解决这道难题了。

disaster

n.灾难，天灾
Many houses were damaged in the disaster. What a pity!
很多房屋在那场灾难中损坏了，真是不幸！

pray

v.祈祷，祈求，请求，恳求
The woman closed her eyes and began to pray.
那个女人闭上眼睛开始祈祷。

expert

n.专家
adj.熟练的，专业的
He is an expert at fixing the computer.
他是修电脑的专家。

squirrel

n.松树
v.储存
That squirrel looks so cute in snow.
那只松树在雪地里看起来真可爱。

acquisition

n.获得，掌握，获得物，取得物
Reading is essential to the acquisition of knowledge.
阅读对于获取知识来说是必要的。

plenty

n.丰富，大量
We have plenty of fruit and vegetables.
我们有充足的水果和蔬菜。

compensate

v.补偿，赔偿，给…报酬
He must compensate for his mistakes.
他必须弥补他的过失。

constitution

n.组成，结构，宪法，章程
The chemical constitution of vitamin B6 seems quite complex.
维他命b6的化学构造看起来很复杂。

shabby

adj.破烂的，卑鄙的，衣衫褴褛的
Our plan was to buy and fix the shabby old house.
我们的计划是买下并整修这个破旧的老房子。

professor

n.教授
The actor plays the role of a professor in that TV series.
那个演员在那部电视剧中扮演一位教授。

cathedral

n.大教堂
adj.有权威的
We visited a magnificent cathedral.
我们参观了一座宏伟的教堂。

theatre

n.戏院，剧院
Let’s go to the theatre and watch a play!
我们去剧院看戏吧！

navy

n.海军
I joined the navy to be at sea.
我加入海军是为了出海。

chest

n.胸膛
We can see his strong chest under his shirt.
我们可以看到他衬衫下强壮的胸膛。

training

n.训练，培养
You should be prepared before training.
训练前你得准备好。

sniff

v.嗅，吸气
n.嗅，吸气（声）
You might see dogs at the airport sniffing for illegal drugs.
你可能会在机场看见狗在嗅毒品的味道。

accelerate

v.加速，增长，使增速，促进
The car accelerated from 60km/h to 100km/h.
汽车从60km/h加速到来100km/h。

新的学习

satisfy

v.（使）满意，满足，符合…的标准
The delicious pizza satisfied that boy.
这个好吃的披萨满足了那个小朋友。

composition

n.（文章或音乐的）创作
Beethoven’s orchestral compositions are regarded as masterpieces by most people.
贝多芬的作品被大多数人认为是世界上最伟大的。

mat

n.垫子，草席
The dog is sitting on the “welcome” mat.
狗狗坐在印有”欢迎”的垫子上。

experiment

n.实验
v.(on)进行实验
The scientist is doing an experiment.
科学家在做一项实验。

withdraw

v.取款，撤退，收回，撤离
I’d like to withdraw $500 from the machine.
我想从这台机器里取500美元。

recognize

v.辨别，认出
I can recognize you, though you are dressed like a sheep.
虽然你扮成羊，我还是认得出你。

narrow

adj.狭窄的
v.使变窄，缩小
n.海峡
I will meet you on the other side of the narrow alley.
我在窄巷子那头等你。

accumulate

vt.积聚，积累
vi.聚积，堆积
He accumulates money for future use.
他攒了些钱，以供将来使用。

memorial

n.纪念像，纪念物，纪念碑，纪念仪式
adj.纪念的
The gravestones are decorated with flags on National Memorial Day.
在国家公祭日，墓碑都用国旗装饰了起来。

spray

n.喷雾，浪花
vi.飞溅，喷
vt.向…扫射
We can use this spray to make the houes smell better.
我们可以用这个喷雾让室内更好闻。

fibre

n.纤维（物质），性格，质地
We use optical fibre to channel information effectively.
我们用光纤来有效传输信息。

lorry

n.卡车，运货汽车
All the stuff was carried in the lorry.
所有货物都搬上卡车了。

undivided

adj.（注意力）专一的，（国家）没分开的
He gives undivided attention to his work and doesn’t hear the phone ringing.
他专心致志地工作，并没有听到手机铃声。

laundry

n.洗衣，送洗的衣服
Doing laundry is part of my housework.
我的家务之一是洗衣服。

razor

n.剃刀，刮胡刀
He shaved his face with a razor.
他正在刮胡刀刮胡子。

commercial

adj.商业性的，贸易的
n.商业广告
This is the busiest commercial street in the city.
这是这个城市最繁华的商业街了。

waist

n.腰
The man put the measuring tape around his waist.
这个人在腰上围了一条软尺。

tendency

n.趋向，趋势，倾向
There’s a good tendency for our company’s business.
我们公司的业务有一个良好的趋势。

coal

n.煤，煤块
Coal is a very popular form of energy.
煤是种很常见的能源。

apology

n.道歉，（表达）歉意
The man put his hands together to show his apology.
他双手合十表达歉意。
