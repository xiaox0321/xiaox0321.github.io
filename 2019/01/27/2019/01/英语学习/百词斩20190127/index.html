今天学习

utmost

adj.最大的；极度的
n.极限
I drive at the utmost speed,it is so fast;
我把车开到了最大速度，真是太快了。

concept

n.设想
Let me explain the concept using this diagram.
让我用这个图表来解释这个设想。

presence

n.出席，到场
Every morning the teacher checked our presence in class.
每个早上老师在课堂上检查我们的出席。

challenge

n.挑战
v.挑战
Dare you take the ice bucket challenge like me?
你敢像我一样参加冰桶挑战吗？

consider

v.考虑，细想；认为；觉得
I will consider you offer,but I think I can get a better one.
我会考虑你给的职位的，但我觉得我能找到一个更好的。

preserve

v.贮鲜；保鲜；保护
You can preserve food by putting it into the refrigerator.
你可以把食物放进冰箱保鲜。

foundation

n.地基；基金（会）
The builders are laying the foundation of a building.
建筑工人正在为一栋高楼打地基。

perspective

n.角度，视角，观点，想法
In correct perspective, all horizontal lines meet at a single point.
在恰达的视角下，所有的水平线都交汇于一点。

provoke

v.引起；对…挑衅
The happy couples provoke the single man’s anger.
秀恩爱的情侣们成功的激起了这个单身汪的怒火。

create

v.创造；引起；产生
I created the painting all by myself.
我全靠自己创造了这幅画。

former

adj.前任的，前者的，以前的
n.创造者
These are the faces of former presidents.
这些都是前任的总统的面孔。

expose

v.暴露
Some cement fell from the wall,leaving the bricks exposed.
墙上的水泥掉下来后，砖块暴露出来了。

responsible

adj.有责任，负责的
I am responsible for the satefy of all these planes.
我对这些飞机的安全负责。

radical

n.激进分子
adj.激进的；重大的，彻底的
The radicals caused chaos at the protest.
在抗议中，激进分子制造了一片混乱。

impress

v.使有印象，使铭记
The magician impressed the audience with his amazing tricks.
这位魔术师用他惊人的伎俩，给观众留下了深刻的印象。

abstract

adj.抽象的；
n.摘要，抽象
Abstract art doesn’t look like anything.
抽象艺术看起来什么都不像。

obvious

adj.明显的，显然的
“2+2=4” should be pretty obvious to you.
“2+2=4”对你来说是显而易见的吧。

scan

v.扫描；细看
n.浏览
Scan the code to get further infomation.
获取更多的信息，请扫描二维码。

outstanding

adj.杰出的，突出的，显著的
Jack Ma is an outstanding example of entrepreneurs.
马云是企业家中的杰出代表。

institution

n.机构
The Red Cross is an institution devoted to the development of human welfare.
红十字会是一个致力于人类福利事业发展的机构。

今天复习

sensitive

adj.敏感的，过敏的；灵敏的
He’s very sensitive;even a common question might hurt him.
他很敏感，即使是一个很平常的问题也可能伤害到他。

phenomenon

n.现象
The Northem Lights is a wonderfull natural phenomenon.
北极光是一种奇妙的自然现象。

diet

n.(病人或减肥者的)特种饮食；大量
I’m trying to eat a diet of only fruits and vegetables.
我在尝试吃只有蔬果的节食餐。

boom

n.隆隆声
v.发出低沉有回想的声音；以低沉有回想的声音发出
The boombs went off with a loud “booms”.
炸弹爆炸的时候发出隆隆的一声巨响。

establish

v.建立；使成为
The two businessmen are trying to establish links.
这两个商人试图建立联系。

attraction

n.吸引，魅力，吸引力
As soon as we met,there was an instant attraction between us.
一见面，我们之间瞬间就产生了一种吸引力。

error

n.错误，过失
If you make an error, you can just erase it and try again.
如果写错了，删掉重写就可以了。

stretch

v.伸展，延伸，张开，夸大，超越
n.伸展，张开
The cat is stretching its body.
猫咪在舒展它的身体。

aspect

n.(问题等的)方面
A marketing campaign has several different aspects such as price,promotion etc.
市场营销包括很多不同的方面，比如价格，促销等。

exaggerate

vt. 夸大，夸张；使扩大
vi. 夸张
The man exaggerates how big his muscles are.
这男人夸大了自己的肌肉量。

protection

n.保护，防卫，警戒
As a true friend, Jerry is always provide protection to Tom.
杰瑞是汤姆的真朋友，他总是为了汤姆提供保护。

troublesome

adj.令人烦恼的，讨厌的
Frequently felling the call of nature can be so troublesome for old people.
对一个老人来说，总是想上厕所实在是令人烦恼的。

elbow

n.手肘；衣服肘部
v.用肘推，挤
My elbow hurts relly bad!
我的胳膊肘伤的很重！

adapt

vi.适应不同的情况
vt.使使用
The chameleon adapts to its surroundings in a second.
变色龙能够在眨眼间，融入周围的环境。

scheme

n.方案，计划；阴谋，诡计；
v.图谋，密谋
Our scheme for the next game is a 4-4-2 fromation.
我们队下一场比赛的方案是使用442阵容。

temper

n.脾气，性情；
v.锻炼，缓和
Stuck is traffic for over an hour,the man began to lose temper.
堵车了一个多小时了，这个男人控制不住开始发火了。

recommend

v.推荐，介绍
He recommended the plan to his colleague.
他向同事推荐这个方案。

precise

adj.精确的
This instrument gives precise measurements.
这台仪器显示出精确的测量结果。

confirm

v.证实
The X-ray picture confirmed that he had broken his finger.
X光片证实了他的手指已经骨折了。

pruchase

v.购买
n.购买，所购之物
The pretty shirt is so cheap that I want to purchase it at once!
这件漂亮的衬衣太便宜了，我想马上买一件。

aware

adj.意识到的，知道的
The teacher is aware of students that cheat on the exam.
老师在考试中发现了作弊的学生。

means

n.方法，手段
I’ll push the nail in by means of this hammer.
我要用这把锤子来钉钉子。

indispensable

adj.必不可少的
An umbrella is indispensable when it rains heavily.
暴雨天理伞是必不可少的。

beneficial

adj.有益的，有利的
It would be beneficial for you to go outside every once in a while.
偶尔出去走走对你有好处。

lens

n.镜头；镜片
These camera lenses cost a lot.
这些相机镜头很贵。
