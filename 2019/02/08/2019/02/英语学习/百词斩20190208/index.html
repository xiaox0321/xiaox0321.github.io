总复习

detect

v.侦察，探测，发现，发觉
The policeman is detecting the crime scene.
警察正在侦查犯罪现场。

maintain

v.维持，保持
I wash my car daily to maintain its shine.
我每天都洗车来维持它的光洁如新。

scene

n.景色，景象，（戏剧的）一场
The sunset over the road created quite a beautiful scene.
道路尽头的日落创造了一幅唯美的景象。

combat

n.战斗，格斗
v.与…战斗，与…争斗
In ancient times,fighters rode horses into combat.
古时候，士兵们骑马与敌人战斗。

available

adj.可得到的，可利用的，有空的，有效的
Seats in white are available to choose.
白色座位都是可选的。

compose

v.创作，作曲，形成，构成
The man composes new songs on his keyboard.
这位男人在他的键盘上创作新歌。

version

n.版本，译文
There are many different versions of Monkey King.
齐天大圣有很多不同的版本。

demonstrate

vt.演示，展示，（用实例，实验）论证
vi.示威
The salesman demonstrates how the device works on a watermelon.
推销员在演示了怎么操作切瓜神器。

mislead

v.把…带错路
The wrong map misleads him to the suburbs.
这张错误的地图使他误入郊区。

conclusion

n.结论，推论
I finally drew a conclusion from these facts.
我终于从这些因素中得出了一条结论。

secure

v.保卫，保护，使安全
adj.安心的
The anti-virus software secures our computers.
防毒软件可以保护我们的电脑安全。

insist

v.坚持要求，坚持认为
She insisted to me that I should clean her room.
她坚持说我应该打扫她的房间。

witness

n.目击者，证人
v.目击，亲眼看见
The little girl is the only witness of this accident.
小女孩是这场事故的唯一目击者。

concern

n.关心，担忧
v.感兴趣，涉及，关心，（使）担忧
She shows her concern by helping the old lady walk.
通过帮助那位老太太走路，她表现出来她的关心。

opponent

n.对手，敌手，对抗者
adj.对立的，敌对的
The boxer punched his opponent severely!
这个拳击手痛击对手！

cooperation

n.合作
Cooperation between team members will be necessary to finish the task.
小组成员间的合作对于完成任务是很必要的。

rigid

adj.坚硬的，死板的
My hard hat is so rigid that even a Nokia phone can’t crack it!
我的安全帽很坚硬，即使诺基亚也砸不碎。

dismiss

v.开除，解散，摒除，不再考虑
His boss dismissed him from his job for marking too many mistakes.
他在工作上犯了很多错，他的老板因此开除了他。

exception

n.例外（的人或事），除外
The ugly duck is an exception among the cute ducks.
丑小鸭是那群可爱的鸭子里面的一个例外。

participate

v.参与，加入
The teacher is happy when her students participate in class discussions.
当学生们参与课堂讨论时老师很开心。

encounter

v.邂逅，遭遇，偶遇
n.偶然碰见，遭遇
I’ve never thought that I’d encounter my favorite star one day!
我从没想过有一天可以遇到我最喜欢的明星。

discount

v.（价格、债款等）折扣
v.打折扣，不全信，怀疑地看待
Buy it this week,and you can still get a discount.
这周就买了它吧，还能享受折扣。

urge

v.驱策，力劝，敦促
n.冲动，欲望
He urged the pig to go faster.
他催促小猪跑快点儿。

behave

v.举止端正（或良好、适当）
“Now, you behave at school!” shouted the angry father.
“现在，你给我在学校好好表现！” 愤怒的父亲咆哮道。

profession

n.职业，立誓信教
They have their respective professions.
他们有各自的职业。

scope

n.瞄准镜
The sniper looked through the scope to see the target.
狙击手从瞄准镜看出去瞄准目标。

privilege

n.特权，优待
She lives a life of privilege,she’s always been rich.
她过着优越的生活，而且一直很富有。

inquire

v.打听，询问
The policeman inquired about the escaped prisoner’s whereabouts.
警察正在询问逃犯的行踪。

convince

v.使确信
The footprints convinced the wife that the cat made the mess.
地上的脚印使女主人确信是猫把房间弄乱的。

authority

n.权威，威信
She read a bunch of books and became an authority in this field.
她读了非常多的书，成了这个领域的专家。

according to

phr.根据，据…所说
According to the law, you are guilty.
根据法律，你罪名成立。

amuse

v.娱乐，消遣，使发笑
Their talk show amused the audience.
他们的脱口秀逗乐了观众。

channel

n.频道
v.引导
These channels are the most popular ones on television.
这些是最受欢迎的电视频道。

suppose

vt.想，认为
I suppose I could put a ring in my nose.
我想，也许我能在鼻子上放一个环。

conceal

v.隐藏，隐瞒，掩盖
I use this mask to conceal my face.
我用面具来隐藏我的脸。

criminal

n.犯罪
adj.犯罪的，违法的，刑法的
That criminal was put into jail for killing two people.
那个罪犯因为杀害了两个人而被关进了监狱。

sensible

adj.明智的，理智的
It’s sensible to avoid drinking or smoking too much.
避免喝太多酒和抽太多烟是很明智的。

assumption

n.假定，设想
After marriage,we make the assumption that a baby will soon follow.
结婚后，我们设想很快就会有个宝宝了。

arrange

v.整理，达成协议
I arrange my toys so that they can fit in a box.
我把玩具排列好，这样它们就能都放在一个盒子里。

enormous

adj.巨大的，极大的，极大量的
The little girl was shocked by the enormous dog.
小女孩被这只巨大的泰迪犬震撼到了。

quit

vt.辞职，停止
The office worker was so happy after he quit his job.
这名员工辞掉工作后感到很开心。

administration

n.管理，管理层，管理部门，政府，（药的）配给
He’s the top man in the administration of the organization.
他是管理这个组织的一把手。

edit

v.编辑
I edit the story using a pencil.
我用铅笔编辑故事。

dine

v.用正餐（晚餐），招待…吃饭
Those friends enjoy going out to dine at Friday night.
这几个朋友很享受周五晚上出去吃饭。

suffering

n.受难，苦楚
My mother’s suffering is intense-her back really hurts.
我妈妈正在承受很大的痛苦看，她的背真的很痛。

temporary

adj.暂时的，临时的
These tents are just temporary as they’ll move soon.
这些帐篷只是临时的，因为他们会很快搬走。

accomplish

v.实现
The boy has accomplished his goal of building a tower.
男孩实现了搭建小塔的目标。

spokesman

n.发言人
The spokesman on the stage is answering questions.
台上的发言人正在回答问题。

mature

adj.成熟的
vt.使成熟
vi.成熟
She looks mature with heavy make-up.

blank

adj.（纸等）空白的，茫然的
n.空白，空白处
Your notepad is blank,write some notes about the meeting!
你的笔记本是空白的，写点会议记录吧！

scale

n.天平，磅秤
v.攀登
Stand on the weighing scales to get your weight.
站在体重秤上来测自己的体重吧。

cultivate

v.耕作，养殖，栽培
The farmer is using a machine to cultivate the land.
这个农民正在用机器开垦荒地。

ignore

v.忽视
The boy’s parents totally ignored him as they were fixated on their phones.
男孩的父母完全忽略了他，只顾着玩手机。

jounal

n.日记，定期刊物，杂志
The man writes everything down in his journal.
这个人把发生的事情都记在日记里。

leap

n.跳跃
v.跳跃
Neil Armstrong makes a glant leap forward for mankind.
阿姆斯特朗为人类的发展迈出了巨大的一步。

category

n.类别，范畴
Please put the documents in the correct category.
请把文档放进正确的分类里。

intention

n.意图，意向，目的，打算
I have every intention of buying a house,so I must start saving now.
我打算买房，所以现在必须开始存钱了。

dispute

n.争论，争端
v.争执，驳斥
They had a dispute over who should be his dance partner.
谁该死他的舞伴，她们在这个问题上发生了争执。

branch

n.树枝，分支（学科）
The man cut off a branch from the tree.
那个男人从树上剪下了一根树枝。

exert

v.尽（力），运用，发挥
I exerted all my strength to lift all the boxes.
我用尽全力举起这些箱子。

congatulate

v.祝贺，向…致贺词
Her colleagure are congratulating her for the prommotion.
她的同事正在祝贺她升职了。

reception

n.前台，接待处，接收，欢迎，招待会
The young couple got their room key at the reception of the hotel.
这对年轻的夫妇在酒店前台拿到了房间钥匙。

series

n.连续，接连，系列，系列节目，连续比赛
For this card trick,please put the cards in series first.
对于这个纸牌戏法，要先把纸牌按顺序放好。

disguise

n.伪装物
She wore a disgulse to hide her true identity.
她戴了一张伪装面具以掩盖自己的真实身份。

controversy

n.争论，辩论
The question of who should clean the kitchen caused lots of controversy.
谁应该打扫厨房的问题引起了许多争论。

equip

v.装备，具备
The worker equips himself with several items.
这名工人用多种物品装备自己。

impose

v.把…强加
My money-minded mum always imposes her ideas on me!
我那个爱钱如命的妈妈总是把她的想法强加在我身上。

cure

v.治愈，治疗
These pills will cure your sickness.
这些药片能治好你的病。

recall

v.回想起，记起，回忆
I could not recall his name.
我记不起他的名字了。

security

n.安全，防护措施
You must go through the security check at the airport.
在机场，你必须进行安全检查。

relieve

v.消除，减轻
A nice massage will relieve your back pain.
舒服的按摩可以缓解你的背部的疼痛。

expense

n.开销，开支，花费，费用
Our expenses far outweigh our income.
我们的支出远大于我们的收入。

routine

n.常规，例行公事，一套固定舞步（或动作）
adj.例行的，普通的，惯例的
Brushing my teeth is part of my daily routine.
刷牙时我每天都要做的事。

reaction

n.反应
The audience have different reactions to the same mmovie.
对于同一部电影，观众们有着不同的反应。

conclude

v.使结束，推断出，缔结
After looking over the reports,she concluded that her idea didn’t work.
在仔细看了报告后，她断定她的想法行不通。

interview

n.面谈，面试，采访
v.面试，采访
During the job interview,the man seemed nervous.
面试过程中，这位应聘者似乎很紧张。

schedule

v.时间表，日程，计划表
v.预订，安排
According to the schedule,I have lots of work to do today.
根据计划表，我今天有很多事情要做。

restore

v.恢复
The skin treatment will restore the youthfulness to your skin.
皮肤治疗法还你年轻靓丽的皮肤。

trend

n.时尚，趋势
v.转向，使趋势
The new trend is for girls to wear very colorful clothing.
女孩儿们现在流行穿多彩的服饰。

confuse

v.混淆，使混乱，使迷糊
I always confuse the girl with her twin sister.
我一直把那个女孩和她的双胞胎姐姐搞混淆。
