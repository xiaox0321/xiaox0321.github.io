新的学习

bounce

vi.弹起，蹦跳
n.弹跳，弹力，活力
vt.使弹起
The ball hit the ground and bounced.
球撞到地上又弹起来了。

ordinary

adj.普通的，平常的，平凡的
I’m just an ordinary man.
我只是个再平常不过的男人。

capture

v.捕获，俘获，捕捉，留存
n.战利品
The rabbit was captured by the hunter.
猎人抓到了一只兔子。

wreck

n.（事故中）遭严重损坏的汽车（飞机等）,沉船，失事船
v.破坏，使毁灭
The wreck of the plane was left here.
飞机残骸被遗留在这里。

accent

n.口音，腔调，重音符号
v.重读
He has such a funny accent!
他的口音太搞笑了。

tame

adj.驯服的，温顺的，顺从的，枯燥的
v.驯服
Don’t worry, this lion is tame - it won’t hurt you.
别担心，这只狮子很温驯，它不会伤害你的。

resistant

adj.有抵抗力的，抗…的
This watch is water resistant.
这块手表具有防水性能。

quote

v.引用，援引，报价
n.引用
He likes to quote the words of Shakespeare.
他喜欢引用莎士比亚的话。

deny

v.否认，拒绝承认，拒绝给予，自制
I’m trying to deny that I just farted.
我试图否认刚刚是我在放屁。

crawl

v.爬，爬行
The baby crawls forward on his arms and legs.
小婴儿手脚并用地爬行向前。

mysterious

adj.神秘的
On my God! A mysterious man is running in my yard!
天呐！一个神秘人正在我的院子里跑！

prevail

v.流行，盛行，战胜，压倒
Hats prevailed in the 19th century.
帽子在19世纪很流行。

stripe

n.条纹，长条
The zebra has black and white stripes on its sides.
斑马身上两侧有黑白相间的条纹。

influential

adj.有影响的，有影响力的
He is such an influential star that many people copy his look.
他是一位很有影响力的明星，许多人都在模仿他的穿着。

flesh

n.肉，肉体，果肉
The lion feeds on flesh.
狮子是肉食动物。

suburb

n.郊区
In the suburbs, there are fewer houses and more trees.
郊区房子少但是树多。

paste

v.粘贴，裱糊
n.糊状物，浆糊，糊
I’m pasting wallpaper to the wall.
我正把墙纸粘贴到墙上。

immune

adj.免疫的，有免疫力的，免除的
People who exercise often are proven to have stronger immune systems.
经常锻炼的人被证实会有更强的免疫系统。

inferior

adj.（等级、地位等）下等的，低等的，低劣的
n.部下，地位低的人，次品
He makes me feel I’m inferior to him.
他让我感觉在他面前低人一等。

elastic

adj.可伸缩的，（系统或计划）灵活的
n.松紧带，橡皮圈
The spring is a very elastic thing that can stretch far.
弹簧是一种可伸缩的东西，可以拉伸得很远。

congress

n.国会（美），代表大会，立法机关
This is the place where the congress members would have a meeting.
这就是国会成员开会的地方。

jury

n.陪审团，全体评审员，评判委员会
The jury was showed proof of the crime in court.
法庭上，犯罪的证物被展示给了陪审团。

velocity

n.速度
The velocity of the free kick was over 100km/h!
这叫任意球的速度达到了惊人的100km/h以上。

capacity

n.容量，产能
The capacity of this cop is 2 liters.
这个杯子的容量是2L.

cement

n.水泥，混凝土，胶合剂
We pour the cement in before it dries.
我们趁水泥还没干，把它倒进去。

summit

n.山顶，顶点，（政府间的）峰会
He eventually climbed to the summit of the mountain.
他终于爬到了峰顶。

exhaust

v.使精疲力尽，耗尽，详尽论述
n.排气装置，废气
Doing housework exhausts me.
做家务让我精疲力尽。

purchase

v.购买
n.购买，所购之物
The pretty shirt is so cheap that I want to purchase it at once!
这件漂亮的衬衣太便宜了，我想买上买一件。

horizon

n.地平线，眼界，见识
The sun is slowly rising from the horizon.
太阳从地平线缓缓升起。

installation

n.安装，装置
The installation of air conditioning was a little difficult.
安装空调有一些难度。

decent

adj.体面的，正直的，像样的，合宜的
That decent gentleman enjoys his leisure time with wine and cigar.
那个体面的绅士喜欢在闲暇时间里品红酒、抽雪茄。

radius

n.半径，范围
The circle’s radius is about 10 centimeters.
这个圆的半径大约是10厘米。

wrap

v.裹，包，捆
She wrapped the gift with some colorful paper.
她用彩色的纸把礼物包起来。

concise

adj.简洁的，简要的，简明的（版本）
His word is always concise, which shows his unique attitude.
他说话总是很简洁，显示了他独特的态度。

reservoir

n.水库
The city must rely on safe drinking water from the reservoir.
这个城市肯定是靠这个水库提供给安全饮用水。

consent

n.同意，准许，赞成
v.同意
I give you my consent, you can do it if you want.
我赞成你的想法，如果你想要做你就做吧。

applause

n.鼓掌，掌声
The audience broke into enthusiastic applause for the three boys.
观众们对三个男孩报以热烈的掌声。

identical

adj.相同的，同样的，一模一样的
n.完全相同的食物22
My mother dresses my twin btothers in identical clothing.
我妈妈给我的双胞胎弟弟穿一模一样的衣服。

withstand

v.忍受，抵挡
We all have to withstand our own pressures in life.
我们都不得不承受住各自的生活压力。

subsequent

adj.随后的
Please this button to play the subsequent track.
按这个按钮就可以播放下一首歌。

alternative

adj.可供选择的
n.可供选择的事物
She provides an alternative choice for the girl who wants to buy a diamond ring.
她给那位想买钻戒的女生提供了另一种选择。

dramatic

adj.戏剧的，戏剧性的，引人注目的，逼真的，刺激的
The dramatic decline of the stock value was a big blow for him.
这次股市的戏剧性下跌对他来说是重重的一击。

tear

v.撕裂，撕碎
n.眼泪
The broken-hearted man tears the photo into pieces.
这个心碎的男人撕碎了照片。

sway

v.摇摆，摇曳，动摇（某人）
n.摇摆
The reeds sway back and forth in the wind.
芦苇在风中摇曳。

gratitude

n.感激，感谢
On Father’s Day, we show our gratitude to our fathers for raising us.
父亲节的那天，我们向父母表达退他辛勤养育的感激之情。

subordinate

n.下属，部下
adj.次要的，从属的，下级的
v.使从属于，使处于次要地位
The status of subordinates is lower than their superior.
下属的地位比上司的地位要低。

revelation

n.揭露，（出乎意料的）被揭示的真相
The revelation of the masked man’s identity surprised us.
蒙面男身份揭露后，我们大吃一惊。

sticky

adj.粘的，黏糊糊的
The glue on her hand is so sticky.
她手上的胶水好粘啊。

plow

v.犁，耕
n.犁
People can use machines to plow the field now.
现在人们可以用机器来犁地。

sensible

adj.明智的，理智的，可察觉的
It’s sensible to avoid drinking or smoking too much.
避免喝太多酒和抽太多烟是很明智的。

magnetic

adj.有磁性的，有吸引力的
This iron is magnetic, so it attracts other things made of iron.
这块铁带有磁性，所以它能吸住其他铁质的东西。

conjunction

n.连接词
In language, conjunctions are words like “and” and “but”.
在语言中，连词就是像”和”和”但是”这种词。

dense

adj.稠密的，浓密的
The forest is so dense, there are trees absolutely everywhere.
这片森林很浓密，到处都是树。

tolerance

n.忍耐力，容忍，宽恕
I’ve had to develop a tolerance for loud noise at the place where I work.
在我上班的地方，我培养出了耐受巨大噪声的能力。

explosive

adj.爆炸的，引起爆炸的，爆炸性（局势）
n.炸药
The scientist was hurt by explosive liquid!
这个科学家被易爆的液体伤到了！

obstacle

n.障碍物，妨碍
The bunny jumps over the obstacles.
小兔子跳过了这些障碍。

liver

n.肝脏
The largest internal organ in a human body is the liver.
人体最大的内脏是肝脏。

agenda

n.议事日程
What topics should we put on the agenda for our afternoon meeting?
我们应该把那些议题放入下午的会议议程中？

enthusiastic

adj.狂热的，极感兴趣的，热情的
They are enthusiastic about reading.
他们热爱读书。

eternal

adj.永恒的，永不改变的
Man is on eternal journey to understand the universe.
人类在探索宇宙的永恒道路上。

suicide

n.自杀
I’ll commit suicide if you don’t feed me fish!
你不要给我喂小鱼，我就死给你看！

negotiation

n.谈判
The negotiation went well and they finally reached an agreement.
谈判进行得很顺利，最后他们达成了协议。

nervous

adj.紧张的，焦虑不安的
I felt nervous, hoping the teacher would not ask me to answer the question.
我非常的紧张，希望老师别叫我回答问题。

collapse

v.（使）倒塌，（使）崩溃，（使）瓦解
n.倒塌，崩溃，暴跌
The building collapsed after the earthquake.
地震后这栋建筑倒塌了。

breadth

n.宽度
You can measure the breadth of the room with this measuring tool.
你可以用这个度量工具测量房屋的宽度。

accord

n.协议
v.授予
Finally they all reached an accord about what the treaty should say.
他们终于就条约的内容达成了协议。

frustrate

v.使灰心
The endless amount of work frustrates the workers.
无休止的工作使这些员工很沮丧。

adverb

n.副词
Adverbs can be used to modify verbs.
副词可以用来修饰动词。

speculate

vt.推测
vi.投机，做投机买卖，推测，猜测
I speculate the trend of foreign exchange market to make money.
我推测国际外汇市场的走向以此盈利。

strategy

n.谋略，策略，计谋
He used a wise strategy in the battle.
他在战争中使用了明智的策略。

density

n.密度，密集，稠密
The high density of buildings makes the city so crowded.
这个城市的建筑密度很高，所以城市也非常拥挤。

acquaintance

n.熟人，相识的人，认识
They greet each other out of manners, since they are just acquaintances.
他们互相打招呼是出于礼貌，因为他们只是泛泛之交。

chamber

n.会议室
The chamber has enough seats for a meeting.
这个会议室有足够的座位来开会。

corporation

n.企业，公司
In our corporation, we listen to everyone’s opinion.
在我们公司，我们会听取所有人的意见。
