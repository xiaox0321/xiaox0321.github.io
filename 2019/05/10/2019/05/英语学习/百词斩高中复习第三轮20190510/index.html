复习

amateur

adj.业余爱好的，业余（身份）的
n.业余爱好者，外行
This office worker is also an amateur soccer player.
这个上班族也是一名业余球员。

constant

adj.持续不断的，不变的，始终如一的
n.常数，恒量
The constant noise from the construction site makes me crazy.
建筑工地持续不断的噪音让我快疯啦！

glance

v.看一下
n.一瞥，扫视
She galnced at his computer screen to see what he was watching.
她扫了一眼他的电脑想知道他在看什么。

comprehension

n.理解力，测试
These guys must have a high level of comprehension to understand this.
这俩人肯定有非常好的理解力才能看懂这幅画。

spiritual

adj.精神（上）的，宗教（上）的
Living a simple life, she devoted herself to the pursuit of spiritual world.
她过着简朴的生活，致力于追求自己的精神世界。

situation

n.形势，局面，处境，状况
It’s hard to choose which way to go in this situation.
这种情形下很难选择出路。

hobby

n.业余爱好，癖好
People have different hobbies, some like reading books and some like playing football.
人们有不同的业余爱好，有人喜欢读书，有人喜欢踢球。

coincidence

n.巧合，巧事
They’re wearing the same dress today, what a coincidence!
她们今天穿了一样的衣服，真是太巧了！

delighted

adj.欣喜的，快乐的
She was very delighted that she got full marks in the final exam.
期末考试得了一百分，她开心得不得了！

wage

n.薪水，报酬
v.开展，进行（战争、运动）
The worker was so happy when she got her wages.
这个工人拿到工资的时候，很开心。

bandage

n.绷带
v.用绷带绑扎
I hurt my hand, so I wrapped it in a bandage.
我把手弄伤了，所以包了绑带。

superior

adj.较高级的，上层的，出众的
n.上级，高手
The boy’s five-stripe badge shows that he is superior to the other boy.
他肩上的五道杠表明他比另一个人能级别高。

deed

n.行为
Superman did many good deeds for people.
超人为人民做了很多好事。

awful

adj.糟糕的，讨厌的，可怕的，极坏的
This fish smells awful. Take it away!
这条鱼气味太糟糕了，快把它拿走！

roof

n.屋顶，顶部
The cats climbed on the roof of the house.
猫咪爬上了屋顶。

amaze

v.使惊奇，使惊诧
This news really amazes me!
这条消息真的让我感到很吃惊！

swear

v.发誓，郑重地说，咒骂
I swear I’m telling the truth.
我发誓我讲的是实话。

perfume

n.香水
v.使充满芳香
This perfume smells like flowers.
这个香水闻起来像花一样。

distinction

n.优秀，卓越，杰出，差别，区别
Receiving a medal is a sign of distinction.
获得奖牌是优秀的象征。

consultant

n.顾问，会诊医师，顾问医生
The consultant will explain how we should run our business.
顾问将会解释我们应该如何经营我们的事业。

foresee

v.预知，预料，预测
He foresaw that the stock market would rise.
他预测了股市行情。

anxiety

n.焦虑
It is difficult to disguise her great anxiety.
很难掩饰她严重的焦虑。

botanical

adj.植物（学）的
I went to the botanical gardens to see the beautiful plants.
我们去了植物园欣赏那些美丽的植物。

drug

n.药物，麻醉品，毒品
v.下麻药
This drug will lower your blood pressure.
这个药会降低你的血压。

challenge

v.挑战
n.挑战
He like to climb and challenge himself.
他喜欢攀岩和挑战自我。

digest

v.消化
n.摘要
The food he ate was hard to digest.
他吃的食物很难消化。

visual

adj.看的，视觉的
The color of this dress is just a visual trick.
这条裙子的颜色只是一个视觉游戏罢了。

tear

v.撕裂，撕碎
n.眼泪
The hands tear the wall wide open.
一双手撕裂了这面墙。

arithmetic

n.算术，计算
The boy is doing arithmetic homework. It’s easy for him.
那个小男孩在写算术作业，这对他来说很容易。

resemble

v.像，想像，相似
My dog resembles a brown mop.
我的狗像一根棕色的拖把。

betray

v.背叛，（非故意地）暴露
My girlfriend betrayed me. She fell in love with someone else.
我的女朋友背叛了我，她跟别人好上了。

conservative

adj.保守的，保守党的
n.保守主义者
The man has such a conservative attitude he even despises the couple holding hands.
他保守的连情侣牵手都看不惯。

widespread

adj.分布式的，普遍的，广泛的
My love story quickly became widespread through the Internet.
通过网络，我的爱情故事很快传播开了。

effect

n.效果，影响，所有物
v.使发生，引起
I like the light effects on the stage.
我喜欢舞台上的灯光效果。

anniversary

n.周年纪念日
adj.周年的，（有关）周年纪念的
He gave his wife a diamond ring on their tenth anniversary.
在结婚十周年之际，他送了老婆一枚钻戒。

apparent

adj.显然的，明白的，清晰可见的，表面上的，貌似（真实）的
It’s apparent that the boy is taller than the girl.
很明显，那个男孩比女孩高。

statue

n.雕像
There stands a huge statue in New York city.
一座巨大的雕像坐落在纽约市。

adolescence

n.青春期，青春
He began to shave after hitting adolescence.
进入青春期后，他开始刮胡子了。

indicate

vt.表明，表示，显示
Can you guess what the graph indicates?
你能猜到这个图标表明着什么吗？

shock

v.（使）震惊，（使）震动
n.震动，震惊，冲击，休克
She was shocked to see how much her stock value had fallen.
看到股票跌了这么多，她真是惊呆了！

eager

adj.热切的，渴望的，急不可耐的
The kids are eager to eat the cake on the table.
孩子们急不可耐想要吃掉桌上的蛋糕。

federal

adj.联邦制的，联邦政府的
The USA is a federal republic.
美国是一个联邦共和宫。

stubborn

adj.顽固的，难对付的
Both of them are stubbom and don’t want to say sorry first.
他俩都很顽固，不愿意第一个道歉。

rough

adj.粗糙的
The hands of the farmer are rough.
这个农民的手很粗糙。

fancy

adj.华丽装饰的，别致的，引人注目的
n.喜爱，幻想，想像力
vt.想象，幻想
This fancy gold tableware must have been so expensive!
这套华丽的金色餐具肯定很贵！

litter

n.垃圾
v.乱丢
Lots of litter was left on the beach by tourists.
沙滩上有很多游客留下的垃圾。

possess

v.拥有，占有，缠住，迷住
Take the key and now you possess a house.
把钥匙拿去。现在你已经拥有了一套房子了。

convenience

n.方便
Sometimes I eat pre-cooked meals for convenience.
有时为了方便，我会吃事先做好的饭菜。

institution

n.机构
The United Nations is an international institution.
联合国是一家国际机构。

gradually

adv.逐步地
The full moon gradually changes to a new moon.
圆月会逐渐变成新月。

guarantee

v.保证，担保
n.保证金
I guarantee that you will have our best service.
我保证你会得到我们最优质的服务。

absorb

vt.吸收，汲取
Sponges can absorb a lot of water.
海绵可以吸收很多水。

quarrel

n.争吵，口角
v.争吵，争论
I had a terrible quarrel with my wife.
我跟我的妻子大吵了一架。

resist

v.抵抗，抵制
The overweight girl managed to resist the temptation of hamburgers.
这个胖女孩抵制住了汉堡的诱惑。

vehicle

n.交通工具，机动车
The vehicle drives off while carrying a container.
那辆车载着一个集装箱开走了。

evidence

n.证据，证明
v.证明
They are gathering evidence to solve the case.
为了侦破案件，他们正在收集证据。

recipe

n.食谱
She follows the recipe to cook.
她照着食谱来做饭。

extreme

adj.极端的
n.极端
The roller coaster is an extreme test of courage.
过山车是对勇气的极端考验。

soldier

n.士兵，军人
v.当兵
The soldier looked for enemies.
这个士兵在寻找敌人。

possession

n.财产，所有物，拥有
My house is my most expensive possession.
我的房子是我最贵重的财产。

cushion

n.坐垫
v.减轻，缓和
There are several cushions on the bed. It’s comfortable.
床上有几个垫子，很舒服呢。

destination

n.目的地，终点
The destination of their journey is the west.
他们的目的地是西天。

disappointed

adj.失望的
He was disappointed that his favorite football team lost.
他很失望，因为他最喜欢的足球队输了。

irrigation

n.灌溉
The farmers have a new irrigation system to water the fields.
农民们有了新的灌溉系统来浇灌农田。

desperate

adj.绝望的，孤注一掷的
The unemployed man looks so desperate.
那个失业的男人看上去如此的绝望。

deposit

v.存款，存放
n.存款，定金
I deposit money in my piggy bank when I can.
当我有钱我会往我的存钱罐里存钱。

accelerate

v.加速，增长，使增速，促进
The car accelerated from 60km/h to 100km/h.
汽车从60km/h加速到了100km/h。
