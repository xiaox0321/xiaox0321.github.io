复习

burglar

n.窃贼
The burglar is trying to break into the house.
窃贼正试图闯进这个家。

controversial

adj.引起争论的，有争议的
The couple is discussing a controversial topic.
这对夫妇正在讨论一个争议性话题。

acquaintance

n.熟人，相识的人，认识
They are acquaintances because they have been neighbors for many years.
他们是老相识，因为她们做了许多年邻居。

lecture

n.讲演，讲课
v.讲演，向…讲演
He gave a lecture in front of many people.
他在很多人面前发表了演讲。

flee

v.逃离，避开，逃走，（时间）飞逝
He wants to flee from danger.
他想要逃离危险。

casual

adj.随便的
n.临时工
She prefers casual cloting when hanging out on the street.
在街上闲逛时，她偏好休闲的穿着。

astonish

v.使惊讶，使吃惊
The magic astonished the kids.
整个魔术使孩子们感到很吃惊。

fond

adj.喜欢的，喜爱的，溺爱的，精神错乱的
v.显出钟爱之情
The man is very fond of his horse.
这位男士很喜欢他的马。

yard

n.院，场地，码
What a large yard! We can have a barbecue.
这个院子真大！我们可以来个烧烤。

collar

n.项圈，衣服，轴环
v.抓住，为…戴上项圈，为…上领子
I put a collar on my dog to prevent him getting lost.
我给我的狗狗带上项圈是为了防止他走丢。

contradictory

adj.反驳的，自相矛盾的
n.矛盾的事物，矛盾因素
These signs are contradictory - I can’t obey them both.
这些标志是矛盾的，我不能同时遵守它们。

fuel

n.燃料
v.给…加燃料
A vehicle can run with fuel.
一辆汽车有了燃料就可以行使。

persuade

v.说服，劝说，使相信
The salesman is persuading us to buy a car.
这个销售员在劝我们买辆车。

defense

n.防御，防护
The best defense is a good attack!
最好的防御就是一记好的攻击！

scold

v.怒骂，责骂，训斥
The mother scolds her child.
那位母亲斥责她的小孩。

damage

v.毁坏
n.损害
Oh no! My car was badly damaged after hitting the tree.
噢不！我的车在撞上树后严重损坏了。

deliberately

adv.故意地，慢条斯理地
He put a leg out to deliberately make the man fall.
他故意伸出一条腿绊倒老人。

curious

adj.好奇的，好管闲事的
The cats are curious about what’s behind the door.
小猫们对门后面是什么很好奇。

discrimination

n.歧视，偏见
He shows discrimination between the poor and rich.
他嫌贫爱富。

argue

v.争吵
The boy is arguing with his mother.
男孩在和妈妈争吵。

regular

adj.有规律的，定期的，经常的，匀称的
n.正式队员
We have a regular meeting on every Monday morning.
我们每个星期一早晨都会有例会。

reach

v.够得着，抵达
n.范围，影响力
The baby boy is trying to reach for the red ball.
小宝宝正努力伸手去够红球。

scar

n.伤疤，伤痕，内心创伤
v.（给…）留下伤痕
There is a lightening-shaped scar on his forehead.
他额头上有一道闪电形的伤疤。

rigid

adj.坚硬的，死板的
Buildings made from steel tend to be very rigid.
钢筋建构的房屋非常牢固。

mild

adj.味淡的，轻微的
Compared to the spicy hot pot, the chicken soup is milder.
和麻辣火锅相比，鸡汤味道要清淡许多。

preserve

v.保护
It’s our duty to preserve our environment together.
齐心协力保护环境是我们的责任。

stove

n.炉，火炉，电炉
Turn off the stove, or the food will be overcooked.
快把炉子关掉，不然食物要烧焦了。

stain

v.弄脏,沾污，染色
n.污渍，污迹
The man’s cloting is stained with the boy’s cola.
男孩喷出的可乐把他的衣服弄脏了。

afford

v.买得起
Finally, I can afford this car.
我终于买得起这辆车了。

chaos

n.混乱，纷乱
This kitchen is in chaos!
厨房里一片狼藉！

consensus

n.一致同意，合意
We reached a consensus in the end.
最后我们统一了意见。

collision

n.碰撞，冲突
A black car is in collision with a red car.
黑车与红车相撞了。

abandon

v.抛弃，放弃
The mother has abandoned her baby.
这个母亲抛弃了她的孩子。

influence

n.影响，势力
v.影响
Stop smoking! It will have a bad influence on your child.
别抽烟了！孩子会受你影响！

fiction

n.虚构，想象
I like science fiction movies with spaceships.
我喜欢有太空飞船的科幻片。

agenda

n.议事日程
The meeting’s agenda still needs to be written.
会议议程还需要写下来。

seize

v.抓住，逮捕，夺取
They seized one another by the wrist.
他们抓住了对方的手腕。

triangle

n.三角形
She drew a triangle with a black pen.
她用黑笔画了一个三角形。

spare

adj.备用的，多余的，闲置的
n.零件，备件
v.抽出，腾出
I have a spare tire for my car.
我的车有一个备胎。

fortunate

adj.幸运的
He won the lottery! How fortunate!
他中了彩票！多幸运啊！

calm

adj.平静的，镇静的
n.平静，安宁
v.（使）平静，（使）镇定
She is so calm although the endless work bothers her.
尽管工作不胜其烦，但她依然淡定如山。

anxious

adj.忧虑的，担心的，焦急的
The boy is anxious about the exam.
考试让小男孩很焦虑。

predict

v.预言，预告，预测
The magician pretended to be able to predict the future through the crystal ball.
这位魔术师假装能够通过水晶球预测未来。

challenging

adj.具有挑战性的，引起挑战性兴趣的
The bike trick he’s attempting is extremely challenging!
他正尝试的自行车特技真是相当的具有挑战性啊！

tournament

n.锦标赛，联赛
The basketball tournament will end with a single winner.
这次篮球联赛会只有一个获胜队伍。

mentally

adv.精神上，智力上，心理上
She looks as if she is mentally tired.
她看起来精神疲惫。

merry

adj.欢乐的，愉快的
The girls felt merry and happy at the party.
在派对上这些女孩儿感到愉快高兴。

debate

n.辩论，讨论
v.辩论，议论
The debate between the man and the woman is so fierce.
这个男人和这个女人间的辩论太激烈了。

particular

adj.特别的，不寻常的
n.详细情况
You should pay particular attention to spelling.
你需要特别注意你的拼写。

satisffaction

n.满足，满意，乐事，补偿
I gave their service a good rating to show my satisfaction.
我给了好评，来表示我对他们的服务很满意。

campaign

n.（社会性、政治性）活动，运动
v.从事活动，参加活动
Many people came to support the president’s campaign.
很多人都来这儿支持总统的竞选活动。

envelope

n.信封，包装物，外壳，膜，气囊
The sender put the letter into the red envelope.
寄信的人把信放在了红色信封里。

consideration

n.体贴，体谅，顾及，考虑，思考
She shows a lot of consideration for her mother.
她很体贴她的母亲。

tent

n.帐篷
The couple put up a tent beside the river.
那对夫妇在河边支起了帐篷。

precious

adj.珍贵的，宝贵的
The crown is decorated with precious diamonds.
这个皇冠上装饰着珍贵的钻石。

atmosphere

n.大气层，空气
The atmosphere can protect the earth from solar rays.
大气层可以保护地球免受太阳射线的伤害。

obtain

v.获得，赢的，取得
The policeman is obtaining evidence from the crime scene.
警察正在犯罪现场取证。

tolerate

v.忍受，容忍
They have to tolerate the smell in the elevator.
他们不得不忍受电梯里的臭味。

recreation

n.娱乐活动，消遣
Old people like to play mah-jong for recreation.
老年人喜欢玩麻将来作为消遣。

ambassador

n.（特命全权）大使
“Our country wished to have peace” said the ambassador.
“我们国家渴望和平”大使说道。

section

n.部分
v.划分
The chart has five sections in different colors.
图表有颜色不同的五个部分。

qualification

n.资格证明，条件，限制性条件
I’m delighted that I received this qualification from my university.
拿到了大学毕业证，我很开心。

academic

adj.学术的，学院的，大学的
n.学者
They are having an academic meeting about physics.
他们正在开一个物理领域的学术会议。

gale

n.狂风，一阵（笑声）
A sudden gale almost knocked me down.
一阵狂风差点把我吹倒。

scissors

n.剪刀
He uses scissors to cut his hair.
他用剪刀给自己剪头发。

disadvantage

n.不利，不利地位，弱点
The one ant is at a disadvantage against so many others.
这一只蚂蚁在和其他许多蚂蚁的对抗中处于劣势。

acid

n.【化学】酸
adj.含酸过多的
Make sure not to touch the acid - it will burn.
千万不要去碰酸，它会灼伤你。

cuisine

n.菜肴
What kind of cuisine does this restaurant serve？
这家饭店提供哪种菜肴？

avenue

n.林荫道，道路
This long avenue leads to a big country house.
这条长林荫道通往一栋巨大的乡间别墅。

corrupt

v.贿赂，使腐败，变腐败，堕落
adj.腐败的
He is trying to corrupt the man with money.
他想用钱贿赂这个人。

dull

adj.枯燥的，沉闷的
The TV show is so dull that makes him want to sleep.
电视节目无趣地让他想睡觉。
