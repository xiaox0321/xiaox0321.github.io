复习

erupt

v.（火山、喷泉，蒸汽等）喷发，喷射出
This volcano erupts three times a day.
这座火山一天喷发三次。

steady

adj.坚定的，扎实的，镇定的，稳定的，不变的
v.（使）稳固，（使）稳定
The stone house is quite steady under the extreme weather.
石屋在极端天气中非常稳固。

fever

n.发热，狂热
The girl has a fever,so she needs to have a good rest.
那个女孩发烧了，所以她需要好好休息。

swallow

v.咽下，吞下
n.燕子
vi.做吞咽动作，咽口水
I can’t believe that the actor can swallow a sword!
我简直没法相信，那个演员居然能吞一把剑。

messy

adj.脏乱的
How can you live in such a messy home?
你怎么能忍受住在这个脏乱差的屋子里？

tough

adj.健壮的，棘手的
Stay away from that man - he looks tough.
离那个男的远一点，他看起来很健壮。

dare

v.敢，胆敢
n.激将
He’s daring to accept the challenge.
他敢于接受这个挑战。

arrangement

n.布置，组合体
The arrangement of tables looks so crowded.
桌子布置得很挤。

wrestle

v.摔跤，角斗，斗争，努力
n.摔跤，角力，扭斗
I loved to wrestle and fight in college.
上大学时，我喜欢玩摔跤搏斗。

prairie

n.大草原，（尤指）北美大草原
Many cows eat grass on the prairie.
许多奶牛在大草原上吃草。

attitude

n.态度，看法
A positive attitude is what you need to solve problems.
你需要积极的态度来解决困难。

bathrobe

n.浴衣
I wear a bathrobe and drink coffee after a bath.
洗完澡后，我穿着浴袍喝了点咖啡。

creature

n.生物
This creature comes from outer space.
这是一只外星生物。

worn

adj.用旧的，疲倦的
Look at this pair of worn shoes,you need to buy a new pair.
看看你这双旧鞋，你得买双新的了。

jaw

n.鄂，下巴
The lower part of the jaw is highlighted in blue.
下颚被用蓝色高亮了出来。

astronomy

n.天文学
I’ve always loved looking at the stars,so now I study astronomy.
我一直都很喜欢看星星，所以现在我在研究天文学。

optimistic

adj.乐观的，乐观主义的
Even though he cannot walk,he is still optimistic about life.
尽管他不能走路，他仍然乐观对待生活。

theoretical

adj.理论（上）的
These books are too theoretical,I cannot read them.
这些书理论性太强了，我都读不懂。

valley

n.山谷，溪谷，低谷
You can see the entire valley from up here.
你可以从这上面看到整个山谷。

decoration

n.装饰
They’re putting decorations on the Christmas tree.
她们在往圣诞树上放装饰品。

steak

n.牛排
The steak looks so delicious.
这牛排看起来美味极了。

distinction

n.优秀，卓越，杰出，差别，区分
Receiving a medal is a sign of distinction.
获得奖牌是优秀的象征。

consultant

n.顾问，会诊医生，顾问医生
The consultant will explain how we should run our business.
顾问将会解释我们应该如何经营我们的事业。

occur

vi.发生
Car accidents can occur very quickly.
车祸会发生在一瞬间。

trainer

n.教练
My personal trainer makes sure that I exercise scientifically.
我的私人教练确保我科学地锻炼。

condition

n.环境，形势，条件
The hotel room is in poor condition.
这家宾馆的条件挺差。

foresee

v.预知，预料，预测
He foresaw that the stock market would rise.
他预测到了股市行情。

anxiety

n.焦虑
It is difficult to disguise her great anxiety.
很难掩饰她严重的焦虑。

orbit

v.环绕…的轨道运行
n.轨道
vi.环绕…的轨道运行
All of our planets orbit the sun.
所有行星环绕太阳运行。

baggage

n.行李
We put our baggage in the back of the car.
我们把行李放在车的后备箱里。

honest

adj.诚实的
“Wow,the emperor is naked!”,shouted an honest boy.
“哇哦，这个皇帝没穿衣服！”一个诚实的小男孩大叫道。

aim

v.瞄准
n.目标
The man aimed at the center of the mark.
这个男士瞄准了靶子的中心。

botanical

adj.植物（学）的
I went to the botanical gradens to see the beautiful plants.
我们去了植物园欣赏那些美丽的植物。

insect

n.昆虫
There’re many kinds of insects.
昆虫有很多不同的种类。

新的学习

enthusiastic

adj.狂热的，极感兴趣的，热情的
He was very enthusiastic about his new video game.
他非常热衷于新的电子游戏。

struggle

v.奋斗，挣扎，抗争
n.斗争
She’s struggling to reach the top of the cliff.
她为了自己登顶的目标而奋斗。

continent

n.大陆，洲
The world has seven continents.
世界有七大洲。

psychology

n.心理学，心理状态
I study psychology to learn about how people think.
我学心理学是为了了解人们如何思考。

conservation

n.保护，维持
Conservation of our environment is very important.
保护环境很重要。

harbour

n.港湾，港口，安全处
v.包庇，庇护
The view of the harbour at night is amazing!
这片港湾的夜景真是棒极了！

thrill

n.兴奋，激动，引起激动的事物
v.（使）非常兴奋，（使）非常激动
It gave me a real thrill to ride the rollercoaster!
坐过山车真是太刺激啦！

fool

n.笨蛋，傻瓜
v.玩弄，愚弄
He is such a fool that he can’t answer this question.
他连这个问题都答不出，真是个傻瓜。

tiny

adj.微小的
The cat is so tiny that it could be held in one hand.
这只猫咪好小，一只手就可以把它托起。

hay

n.干草
Farmers always roll up hay so it’s easier to move around.
农民总是把干草卷起来方便运输。

pain

n.痛，痛苦，努力，劳苦
I am feeling some pain in my head.
我觉得头有点疼。

spoonful

n.一匙，一匙的量
I like one spoonful of sugar in my tea.
我喜欢在茶里加一勺糖。

eggplant

n.茄子，黑紫色
The eggplant was cut into slices.
茄子被切成了片状。

sink

n.水槽，洗碗池
v.（使）下沉，（使）降低
I wash the dishes in the sink.
我在水槽里洗碗。

apron

n.围裙
The girl looks good in that black apron.
系着黑色围裙的那个女孩看起来很好看。

chairman

n.议长，会长，董事长，主席
Bill Gates is the chairman of Microsoft.
比尔盖茨是微软公司的董事长。

spit

v.吐（唾沫）
n.唾液，唾沫，叉子
The boy is so rude that he spits towards the dog!
这个小男孩真是太粗鲁了，他居然向狗狗吐口水！

concept

n.概念，观念
Copernicus presented a new concept regarding the solar system.
哥白尼提出了太阳系的新概念。

nail

n.指甲
v.将…钉牢，钉住
She’s painting my nail.
她正给我涂指甲。

affair

n.事情，事务，恋爱事件
The CEO is handling some commercial affairs.
这位CEO正在处理一些商务事宜。

furnished

adj.有家具的
The house that we rented came fully furnished.
我们租的房子里有全套的家具。

enemy

n.敌人，危害物
The green soldier is attacking his enemy from behind.
绿衣战士从背后攻击敌人。

inspect

v.检查，审查
She inspects my ticket before I get on the train.
在我上火车前，她检查了我的火车票。

symphony

n.交响乐
The musicians will give a symphony concert this week.
这些音乐家这周将举办一场交响音乐会。

alternative

n.可供选择的事物
adj.可供选择的
He has the alternative of either riding or driving.
他可以在骑车或开车中选择一种。

fountain

n.泉水，喷泉
The fountain in the city center is beautiful.
市中心的喷泉好美。

appeal

n.（人或物的）吸引力
vi.有吸引力，呼吁，请求，申诉
Money has great appeal to most people.
金钱对一些人有很大的吸引力。

pillow

n.枕头
v.垫，枕着
I love sleeping with soft pillows.
我爱抱着软软的枕头入睡。

spoon

n.匙，调羹
vt.用匙舀
These wooden spoons are used for serving food.
这些木勺是用来盛食物的。

skilled

adj.熟练的，有技能的，需要熟练技术的
He is a skilled worker in the factory.
他是位技能熟练的工厂工人。

peasant

n.农民，农夫，庄稼人，小耕农
The peasants are working on the farm land.
农民在地里劳作。

ordinary

adj.普通的，平常的，平凡的
I’m just an ordinary man.
我只是一个再平凡不过的男人。

canteen

n.食堂，小卖部
Let’s go get some food at the canteen.
我们一起去食堂吃点东西吧。

surgeon

n.外科医生
My surgeon decided to give me an operation.
我的外科医生决定给我动手术。

track

v.跟踪追赶，用纤拉船
n.跑道，小路
He follows the footprints to track the bad guy.
他跟着脚印追踪那个坏蛋。

correction

n.改正，修正
The teacher made a correction on my homework.
老师修改了我的家庭作业。

brochure

n.手册，小册子
Travel brochures contain many beautiful pictures.
旅行手册里有许多漂亮的图片。

theater

n.剧场，戏院，电影院
The theater quickly emptied when the performance ended.
表演结束之后，戏院很快就空了。

committee

n.委员会，全体委员
The finance committee discussed the company’s financial plans.
财务委员会讨论了公司的财务计划。
