复习

paperwork

n.日常文书工作
He is busy with handing much paperwork in the company.
他忙于处理公司各种文书工作。

sympathy

n.同情，同感
He felt a lot of sympathy for the dog and gave it a bone.
他很同情那条小狗并给了它一块骨头。

assistance

n.帮助，补助
The doctor provided medical assistance for the old lady.
医生为老太太提供了医疗帮助。

flexible

adj.易曲的，柔软的，灵活的
The pencil is flexible enough to be knotted.
这铅笔非常柔韧，甚至能打个结。

react

v.起反应，有影响，对…做出反应
Different people react differently when shown a horror movie.
看恐怖电影时不同的人有不同的反应。

vocabulary

n.词汇（量），词（汇）表
This software will help increase your English vocabulary!
这个软件会帮你增加你的英语词汇量。

announcement

n.宣告，发表，一项公告
The famous couple made an official announcement about their marriage.
这对明星夫妻官方宣布了她们的婚事。

addicted

adj.上了瘾的，入了迷的
He is addicted to computer games.
他对电脑游戏上瘾了。

fairness

n.公平，美好，清晰
This statue is a symbol of fairness.
这座雕像是公平的象征。

partner

n.伙伴，搭档，配偶，伴侣
v.成为…的合伙人
They are great dancing partners.
他们是很好的舞伴。

circumstance

n.条件，情况
Sucess depends on many different circumstances.
成功取决于许多不同的条件。

moral

adj.道德（上）的，道义的
n.道德，品行
To return the money you found or not ,it’s a moral dilemma.
捡到的钱要不要还回去，还是一个道德难题。

society

n.社会，社交界
A balanced society consists of all kinds of people.
均衡的社会是由各种各样的人组成的。

purpose

n.目的，意图
v.打算
They ran the race together,but with a different purpose in mind.
虽然他们都在跑步，但是他们各自的目的不同。

broadcast

n.广播（节目）
v.播放，广播，播音
I watch the news broadcast every morning.
我每天早上都看新闻节目。

ambiguous

adj.含糊不清的，引起歧义的，模棱两可的
Do you see a vase or two faces? It’s pretty ambiguous.
你看到的是一个花瓶还是两张脸呢？这张图相当模棱两可。

leather

n.皮革，皮制品
The sofa and pillow are covered in real leather.
沙发和枕头都是真皮做的。

effort

n.努力，成就
I made every effort to push the ball up.
我用尽全力把球往上推。

participate

v.参加，加入
The students participate fully in the class.
学生们积极参与到课堂中。

fortune

n.运气
What a good fortune!I won the first prize.
运气真好！我中了一等奖。

expression

n.表情，神情，表达，表述
She took many pictures of different expressions.
她照了很多不同表情的照片。

centimeter

n.厘米，公分
The giraffe is about 150 centimeters tall.
这只长颈鹿高约150厘米。

thermos

n.保温瓶，热水瓶
She is pouring hot water from the thermos.
她在用保温瓶倒水。

bend

v.（使）弯曲，（使）改变（主意），使屈服
n.弯曲，弯曲处，倾向，屈服
She is bending her waist to do some exercise.
她弯下她的腰，来做运动。

cock

n.公鸡
The cock has a beautiful tail.
这只公鸡的尾巴很漂亮。

athlete

n.运动员，体育家，强壮的人
He was a great athlete who ran really fast.
他以前是个优秀的运动员，跑的非常快。

riddle

n.谜，谜语
v.解谜，出谜题，满是弹孔
What is the answer to the riddle?
这个谜语的谜底是啥啊?

attractive

adj.吸引力的，有魅力的，引起兴趣的
I find orange food attractive.
我觉得橙色的食物很吸引人。

vast

adj.辽阔的
This vast desert looks impossible to pass.
这片辽阔的沙漠看上去不可能穿越。

mass

n.众多，群众
v.聚集
A mass of people are waiting.
一大堆人在等待。

serious

adj.认真的，严肃的
He is always very serious while working.
他工作时总是很认真。

emergency

n.紧急情况
It’s an emergency.Get her to the hospital now!
情况紧急，即刻送她去医院！

instruct

v.指导，通知
My mom instructs me how to play golf.
我妈妈教我怎么打高尔夫。

strange

adj.奇怪的，陌生的
I met a guy wearing strange clothes on the subway.
我在地铁上遇到了一个衣着奇怪的男人。

新的学习

freezing

adv.冷冻一样的
adj.冷冻用的
The weather is freezing cold today.
今天天气非常冷！

tissue

n.面巾纸，（动植物的）组织，纱布
The little girl uses a tissue when she sneezes.
打喷嚏时，小女孩扯了张纸巾。

defence

n.防御，防务，辩护
The woman learns self defence.
这个女人学习自卫。

feast

n.盛宴，节日
v.尽情地吃
We are having a big Thanksgiving feast.
我们正在吃感恩节大餐。

chick

n.小鸡
adj.胆小的
Chicks like to huddle together.
小鸡们喜欢挤在一起。

litre

n.升
This cup can measure fluids by the half litre.
这个杯子能量半升液体。

relevant

adj.有关的，贴切的
Before you wirte this essay you should read some books relevant to the subject.
在写论文之前，你应该阅读一些和主题相关的书籍。

detective

n.侦探，私人侦探
The detective followed the footprints to find the killer.
侦探跟着脚印找到了凶手。

metal

n.金属，合金，金属制品
vt.（以金属）覆盖、装配
Wow,these roses are made of metal.
哇，这些玫瑰花是金属做的。

sick

adj.有病的，恶心的
n.病人
v.呕吐
All this cold has made me sick,and now I can’t stop sneezing!
这大冷天把我冻生病了，我现在一直打喷嚏！

camp

n.露营
n.阵营
This boy likes to camp in nature in his own tent.
这个男孩喜欢到野外搭帐篷露营。

civilization

n.文明，开化，教养
The Egyptiian civilization lasted for thousands of years.
埃及文明延续了上千年。

contribution

n.贡献，投稿稿件，捐献物
Thanks to all of you for making a contribution to our organization.
感谢各位给我们慈善段团体的捐献。

cab

n.出租车，司机室，驾驶室
He uses his mobile phone to call a cab.
他用手机预订了一台出租车。

appearance

n.外貌，外表，公开露面
Her appearance will become more beautiful after make-up.
她的外表在化妆之后变得更漂亮了。

ancient

adj.远古的，古代的，古老的
These ancient statues are over 2000 years old.
这些古代雕像有2000多年的历史了。

recite

vt.朗诵
vi.朗诵
vt.背诵
He watched the lady reciting the poem.
他看着这个女人朗诵诗。

departure

n.离去，离开，起程
Breaking up with her boyfriend results in her departure.
她的离开是因为她和男朋友分手了。

ancestor

n.祖宗
It was said that the ancestors of mankind looked like apes.
据说人类的祖宗看起来像猿猴。

mutton

n.羊肉，羊
The man is selling the food that is made from mutton.
这男人正在卖羊肉串。

restrict

v.限制
This sign board indicates that all vehicles are restricted to enter.
这个标志表示所有机动车辆禁止通行。

bury

v.埋葬，隐藏，埋藏
When people are dead,we bury them here.
人死后被埋葬在这儿。

mineral

adj.矿物的
n.矿物，矿物质
It’s good for our health to drink mineral water.
多喝矿物质水对我们的健康有好处。

boil

n.烧开，沸腾
You can drink hot water when the water starts to boil.
水开了你就能喝热水了。

primary

adj.初等教育的，首要的
n.（美）初选
Children usually go to a primary school at the age of 6.
通常小孩子6岁就上小学了。

pub

n.酒吧，酒馆
Let’s go down to the pub to get some drinks!
我们去酒吧里喝点酒吧！

overlook

v.忽视，未注意到
n.忽视，远眺
She’s so busy on her tablet that she’s totally overlooking her daughter.
她忙着玩平板，完全忽视了她的女儿。

foreigner

n.外国人，外人
We have two new classmates,and they are foreigners!
我们班来了两个新同学，他们是外国人！

remain

v.剩余，保持不变
Only one puppy remained in the cage as the rest were sold.
笼子里只剩下一只狗狗，其他的都售出了。

passive

adj.被动的，消极的
n.被动语态
He played a passive role in the marriage.
他在婚姻中扮演了一个被动的角色。

ballet

n.芭蕾舞，芭蕾舞剧
Miss Elephant’s dream is to become a ballet dancer.
大象小姐的梦想是成为一个芭蕾舞者。

voluntary

adj.自愿的，无偿的
n.即兴曲
Giving blood is 100% voluntary.nobody will force you to do it.
献血是百分之百自愿的，没人会强迫你去做。

supreme

adj.至高的，最重要的
The king is the supreme leader of the country.
国王是国家里至高无上的领导者。

slight

adj.轻微的，微小的
vt.轻视，忽略
n.轻视，蔑视
There’s a slight imperfection on this antique teapot.
这个古董茶壶上面有一个小瑕疵。
