复习

cage

n.笼，鸟笼
v.关进（笼子）
Get me out of this cage!Where’s the key?!
把我从这笼子里面放出去！钥匙在哪里？！

garlic

n.大蒜
Garlic makes any meal taste better.
大蒜让所有菜肴更好吃了。

sentence

n.句子
v.判决，宣判
Note the importance of this sentence.
注意这个句子的重要性。

pilot

n.飞行员
adj.实验性的
v.给…领航，驾驶
Wow!The pilots in the movie look so cool!
哇塞！这部电影里的飞行员啊好酷好帅啊！

mustard

n.芥末
adj.芥末色的
I like to add some mustard on my sushi.
我喜欢往寿司上加点芥末。

agriculture

n.农业
We depend on agriculture to give us food.
我们依靠农业给我们提供食物。

against

prep.反对，对（着），逆
Only one person stood actively against him.
只有一个人站在那主动反对他。

cap

n.帽子
The policeman wears a blue cap.
这个警察戴着蓝帽子。

lib

n.嘴唇，唇状物
Her lips are the most beautiful part of her face.
她的嘴唇是她脸上最漂亮的部分。

weed

n.杂草，野草
The worker is cutting the weeds.
工人正在除草。

extraordinary

adj.非同寻常的
The teacher said my daughter has extraordinary musical talent.
老师说我女儿在音乐方面天赋异禀。

barber

n.理发师，理发店
I asked my barber to give me a new style this time.
这一次我让我的理发师给我理一个新发型。

veal

n.小牛肉
My favorite meat is veal.
我爱吃的肉是小牛肉。

yoghurt

n.酸奶
Yoghurt tastes very delicious with cookies.
酸奶和饼干一起吃很美味哟！

wounded

adj.受伤的
The wounded soldier goes to the doctor every day.
这位负伤的士兵每天都去看医生。

pill

n.药丸
The woman took some pills when she was ill.
这个女人生病了，就吃了一些药。

goal

n.（进球）得分，目标，球门
They finally cracked the defence and scored a goal.
他们终于突破了防守，射进一球。

pure

adj.纯净的，纯粹的，纯洁的
The water seems so pure and clean.
水看起来真纯净。

regulation

n.法规，条例，规章
The traffic police is introducing traffic rules and regulations to us.
交警在向我们介绍交通法规。

liberty

n.自由
The bird flew from the cage and got liberty.
这只鸟飞出了鸟笼得到了自由。

irrigate

v.灌溉，冲洗
This machine can irrigate the field.
这台机器可以灌溉田地。

accompany

v.陪伴，为…伴奏（或伴唱）
I always accompany my children to their school.
我总是陪着我的小孩们去学校。

typist

n.（尤指专职）打字员
The typist is finishing her documents.
打字员正在完成她的文件。

barrier

n.障碍
The road was closed by a barrier.
这条路被一个路障封锁了。

abroad

adv.到国外，在国外
The happy couple traveled abroad and took photos together.
这对幸福的夫妻出国旅游，并拍了照片。

biography

n.传记文学，传记
He likes reading the biography of famous people.
他喜欢读名人传记。

voyage

n.航行，航海
v.航海
The ship is on a voyage now.
这艘船正在航行中。

besides

prep.除…之外
adv.而且，还有
As the winner,I received lots of money besides the cup.
作为胜利者，我除了得到了奖杯外，还获得了一大笔钱。

fiber

n.纤维（物质）
We can see the fibers of the rope under a magnifying glass.
在放大镜下，我们能看见编成这条绳子的纤维。

roller

n.滚柱，滚筒，滚轴
Use the roller to paint the walls.
用滚筒来刷墙。

chew

v.咀，咀嚼
n.嚼碎，咀嚼
The boy is chewing the rice.
他在咀嚼米饭。

conventional

adj.依照惯例的，依照传统的
Although we can now pay online,some people still prefer the conventional method of cash.
尽管我们现在可以线上支付了，部分人仍然喜欢传统的现金支付手段。

sweet

adj.甜的，可爱的，美好的，芳香的
n.糖果，甜食
Most kids love sweet food such as candy.
大多数小孩都喜欢吃甜食，比如说糖。

vast

adj.辽阔的
This vast desert looks impossible to pass.
这片辽阔的沙漠看上去不可能穿越。

mass

n.众多，群众
v.聚集
A mass of people are waiting.
一大堆人在等待。

serious

adj.认真的，严肃的
He is always very serious while working.
他工作时总是很认真。

emergency

n.禁忌情况
It’s an emergency.Get her to the hospital now!
情况紧急，即刻送她去医院。

absolute

adj.完全的，绝对的
Motion is absolute,while stillness is relative.
运动是绝对的，静止是相对的。

tongue

n.舌头
vi.吹管乐器
The boy sticks out his tongue to make a face to me.
男孩伸出了舌头来对我做鬼脸。

instruct

v.指导，通知
My mom instructs me how to play golf.
我妈妈教我怎么打高尔夫。

nylon

n.尼龙，尼龙长袜
The strings are made of nylon.
这线是尼龙做的。

strange

adj.奇怪的，陌生的
I met a guy wearing strange clothes on the subway.
我在地铁上遇到了一个衣着奇怪的男人。

bacon

n.熏猪肉，培根
I like to have bacon and eggs for my breakfast.
早餐我想吃培根和蛋。

outspoken

adj.直言的，坦率的
Her outspoken remarks always reveal facts.
她直言不讳地言语总是戳中现实。

dormitory

n.宿舍，学生宿舍，宿舍楼【英国英语】
Look!This dormitory is so dirty!
快看呀！这间宿舍好脏！

sideways

adj.向侧面的
adv.斜着的
Please turn to the side – we want a sideways view.
请往侧面转一下身，我们想要一个侧面照。

greedy

adj.贪婪的，贪心的，贪吃的
The greedy woman wanted more food from the pot before she’d even finished her bow!
贪婪的她吃着碗里的，想着锅里的。

新的学习

go by

phr.（时间）流逝，走过，过去
Don’t waste your time as it goes by quickly.
不要浪费时间因为时光飞逝。

badminton

n.羽毛球，羽毛球运动
Playing badminton on the grass makes the little boy so happy.
在草地上打羽毛球让小男孩特别开心。

bench

n.长凳，条凳，长形工作台
The boy is sitting on the bench with his mother.
小男孩正在和他的妈妈坐在长凳上。

seaman

n.水手，海员
He looks really happy in the seaman uniform.
他穿着水手服看起来很开心。

bookshelf

n.书架
The bookshelf was filled with all sorts of books.
书架上装满了各种各样的书。

dismond

n.钻石
Diamond rings are very popular among young couples.
钻戒在年轻夫妻中十分流行。

bucket

n.水桶，（机器上的）桶装物,大量
The cute cat is hiding in a bucket.
那只可爱的小猫躲在一个桶子里。

Antarctic

n.南极区
adj.南极的
The Antarcitc is a cold place - only penguins can survive there.
南极是一个寒冷的地方，只有企鹅能在那里生存。

graduation

n.毕业，毕业典礼
Throwing hats is a tradition during graduation.
抛帽子是毕业时的一项传统。

soil

n.泥土，土壤
v.污损
He held some soil in his hand.
他手里捧着一些泥土。

punishment

n.惩罚，处罚
The student was given punishment for doing something wrong.
这个学生因为做错事受到了惩罚。

interrupt

n.打扰，插嘴
Do not interrupt me.I am listening to the music.
不要打扰我，我在听音乐呢。

disgusting

adj.令人厌恶的，令人无法接受的
He finds the smell of the sock disgusting.
袜子的味道实在让他恶心。

non-violent

adj.非暴力的
Every conflict has a non-violent solution.
每个争端都有非暴力的解决方式。

apartment

n.（美）公寓，一套房间
This apartment is so bright with these windows.
这套公寓窗户多，很明亮。

sparrow

n.麻雀
Look!The little sparrow is resting on the tree branch.
快看这只小麻雀，在树枝上休息呢。

mud

n.泥
On no! I think our car is stuck in the mud!
哦不！我想我们的车卡泥里了。

colleague

n.同事，同行
I really learned a lot by discussing with my colleagues.
通过和同事讨论，我真的学到了很多。

arrow

n.箭
I shoot three arrows at the center.
我往靶心射了三支箭。

associate

v.联想，联合，联系，交往
n.伙伴，同事
adj.副的
I associate this picture with a star.
这图片让我联想到了星星。

easy-going

adj.随和的
My friend and I are easy-going.
我和朋友都很随和。

slice

v.把…切成片，大幅度削减，切开
n.薄片
The chef is slicing the bread.
大厨正在把面包切成片。

senior

adj.年长的，资格老的，地位较高的
n.（大学或高中）毕业生
Senior citizens should be respected.
老年人应该被尊重。

parcel

n.包裹
The person has a lot of parcels to send on November 11th.
这个人在双十一有很多包裹要送。

suspect

n.嫌疑犯，可疑分子
v.怀疑，猜疑，推测
adj.可疑的
The suspect was body-searched by a policeman.
警察对嫌疑犯进行了搜身。

hand in

phr.上交，递交
He’s handing in his homework to the teacher.
他正在把自己的作业上交给老师。

ladder

n.梯子，阶梯
The man is standing on a ladder.
这个男人正站在梯子上。

weakness

n.（性格、系统）弱点，虚弱
Vampires’weakness is that they are afraid of sunshine.
吸血鬼的弱点是害怕阳光。

French

adj.法国的，法国人的
n.法语
The Eiffel tower is a French landmark.
埃菲尔铁塔是法国的地标。

million

The businessman embraces one million dollars in his arms.
这个商人怀里抱着一百万美元。

minibus

n.小型公共汽车，小型巴士
Let’s take the minibus on a trip!
我们坐小巴去旅游吧！

point

v.指向，指出
n.点，尖端
They are pointing at each other.
他们互相指着对方。

department store

n.百货商场
There’re a lot of people in the department store.
百货商店里好多人呀。

forgive

v.原谅，饶恕，宽恕
“Please forgive me,my dear.” the man said to his girlfriend.
“原谅我吧，亲爱的。”这个男人向他女朋友说。

brotherhood

n.手足情谊，兄弟关系
The brotherhood between the boys will keep them close forever.
这些男孩之间的兄弟情谊让他们永远彼此亲近。

statistics

n.统计数字，数据
I use facts and statistics to prove my point.
我用事实和统计数字来证明我的观点。

decision

n.决定
I’ve made my decision,I find the defendant guilty!
我已做出决定，我认为被告罪名成立！

typhoon

n.台风，强热带风暴
The typhoon destroyed many houses last year.
台风去年毁坏了许多房屋。

rid

v.摆脱
Thank god!Finally I have gotten rid of all my debts.
谢天谢地！我终于摆脱了全部债务！

topic

n.话题，题目，主题
He wrote down a topic for us to discuss.
他写下一个主题以供讨论。

zebra

n.斑马
The zebras are running across the river.
这群斑马正在横渡河流。

amateur

adj.业余爱好的，业余（身份）的
n.业余爱好者，外行
This office worker is also an amateur soccer player.
这个上班族也是一名业余球员。

marble

n.大理石，（游戏用的）玻璃弹子
The table is made of marble.
这张桌子是由大理石做的。

tortoise

n.龟
The tortoise is slowly crossing the field.
乌龟慢慢地在地上爬。

radiation

n.辐射物，放射物
Don’t get closer to radiation if you are not in protective suit.
没有穿防辐射服就不要靠近。

anyhow

adv.不用用何种方法，无论如何
Someone has to wash the dishes anyhow,so I’ll do them.
反正得有人洗碗，那就我洗吧。

constant

adj.持续不断的，不变的，始终如一的
n.常数，恒量
The constant noise from the construction site makes me crazy.
建筑工地持续不断的噪音让我快疯了。

carpenter

n.木匠，木工
The carpenter works on the house.
木匠从事房屋建造工作。

abolish

v.彻底废除
The black slavery of Ameria was abolished by the president in 1865.
这位总统位于1865年废除了美国的奴隶制度。

glance

v.看一下
n.一瞥，扫视
She glanced at his computer screen to see what he was watching.
她扫了一眼他的电脑想知道他在看什么。
