复习

savage

n.野蛮人，粗鲁的人
adj.凶猛的，猛烈的
v.（狗等）乱咬
Their wordy conversation made me feel like a total savage!
他们文邹邹的对话让我感觉得自己完全就像个野蛮人！

glad

adj.高兴的
I am very glad to hear from my mother.
我很高兴收到母亲的来信。

represent

v.代表，阐明
This hand gesture represents love.
这个手势代表了爱。

modest

adj.谦虚的，谦逊的
These two businessmen may be rich,but they’re still very modest!
这两位企业家也许非常富有，但他们仍然很谦虚。

conductor

n.指挥，售票员
The conductor is the leader of the band.
指挥是乐队的老大。

rescue

v.营救，援救
n.营救，救援
The fireman rescued the little boy from the fire.
消防员把小男孩从火中救了出来。

fee

n.费用，酬金
He was shocked by the huge amount of hidden fees behind such a haircut.
他被这个发型背后的巨额隐性费用惊呆了。

autonomous

adj.自动驾驶的，自治的，独立自主的
The man is relaxing,reading a book in his automomous car.
这个男人正在他的自动驾驶汽车中悠闲的读书。

accomplish

v.完成，做到，实现
I have accomplished my tasks on schedule.
我已经完成了计划上的任务。

neighbourhood

n.社区
I live in a friendly neighbourhood where everyone knows each other.
我住在一个友好的社区里，那里每个人都相互认识。

organ

n.器官
Every organ plays an important role in our body.
每一个器官在我们身体中都扮演着重要的角色。

ceremony

n.典礼，仪式
The student was awarded his certificate during the graduation ceremony.
毕业生在毕业典礼上接受了他的毕业证书。

inventor

n.发明家
Thanks to the inventor of the telephone,we can talk anytime anywhere.
对亏了电话的发明者，我们才能随时随地地聊天。

draft

n.草稿
v.起草
This is just the draft design of a building.
这只是建筑的草图设计。

patience

n.耐心，忍耐
Fishing demands a great deal of patience.
钓鱼需要极大的耐心。

candidate

n.（官职、获奖资格等的）候选人
The candidates ar presenting the speeches for the election.
候选人正在为选举发表演说。

disturb

v.打扰，妨碍，使烦恼
His snoring really disturbs me at night!
晚上，他的呼噜声真的很打扰我！

merchant

n.商人，批发商
The merchant checks the quality of his produce.
商人在检查农产品质量。

construct

v.建造，构筑
The workers are constructing a tall building.
工人们正在建造一座高楼。

dismiss

v.开除，解散，摒除，不再考虑
The boss was angry and dismiss the staff.
老板很生气，并且开除了那个职员。

rapid

adj.迅速的，急促的
The rabbit ran at a rapid speed.
那只兔子飞快的跑了。

humor

n.幽默，诙谐
The show is full of humor,hahaha!
这个节目太幽默了，哈哈哈！

ridiculous

adj.可笑的，荒谬的
She looks ridiculous wearing this dress when walking in the street.
她穿着这件衣服上街，看上去太可笑了。

rare

adj.稀有的，难得的，罕见的
Pandas are hard to find because they’re so rare.
熊猫很难被找到，因为它们太稀有了。

fluency

n.流畅，流利
He speaks with perfect fluency.
他说话极其流利。

yawn

v.打哈欠
n.乏味或枯燥的事物
v.打着哈欠说
The cat yawned lying on the grass.So cute!
猫咪躺在草地上打哈欠。太可爱了！

boycott

n.抵制
vt.抵制
The office workers are putting up a boycott.
员工们在举行抗议活动。

certificate

n.合格证书，文凭，证明，证明书
I got my graduation certificate after finishing colege.
在完成大学教育后，我获得了毕业证书。

hug

v.拥抱，紧抱
n.拥抱
The happy couple hug in front of their new home.
这对开心的夫妇相拥在他们的新房子前。

pardon

v.原谅，宽恕
excl.请再说一遍
n.原谅，饶恕，赦免
The king pardoned the prisoner.
君主赦免了那名囚犯。

fur

n.皮毛，皮毛外套，人造毛
A fur coat is very warm,but very expensive!
皮毛外套很温暖，但也很昂贵。

jet

n.喷气式飞机
v.乘坐喷气式飞机
Nowadays most long-distance travel is by jet plane.
现在，大多数长途旅行都使用喷气式飞机。

bacterium

n.细菌
This kind of bacterium can cause a very dangerous disease.
这种细菌能引发非常危险的疾病。

shoulder

n.肩膀，肩部
vt.肩负，承当
The old man has pain in his shoulder.
这位老年人肩膀疼。

polite

adj.有礼貌的，客气的，文雅的
The student is very polite to his teacher.
那个学生对老师很有礼貌。

religious

adj.虔诚的
He is a religious believer,he goes to church every week.
他是个虔诚的教徒，每周都会去教堂。

pupil

n.小学生
The pupils are walking to school.
小学生们正在走路去学校。

seminar

n.研讨会
A teacher and some students are attending a math seminar.
一名老师和学生们正在开教学研讨会。

sour

adj.酸的
The lemon has such a sour taste!
柠檬真酸啊！

salute

v.向…行举手礼，赞扬，赞颂
n.致敬，欢迎
The policeman salutes me as I walk by.
当我走过时，这位警察向我行礼。

material

n.材料，原料，资料
adj.物质的，实体的
Many different materials are needed for construction work.
建造工程需要很多种不同的建筑材料。

pine

n.松树
v.（因死亡、离别等）难过，悲伤
The pine tree on Huangshan is very famous.
黄山的迎客松非常有名。

give up

phr.放弃
The girl has given up doing her homework on the laptop.
那个女生放弃用电脑做作业了。

vacation

n.休假，假期
v.休假，度假
The girl took a vacation on the beach.
这个女孩在海边度假。

新的学习

relay

n.接力赛跑
v.转播
In a 400-meter relay,a runner passes the baton to the next person.
在400米的接力比赛中，运动员将接力棒传给下一个人。

bean curd

n.豆腐
Bean curd is healthy and tasty.
豆腐机既健康又美味。

editor

n.编辑
My editor always corrects my writing mistakes.
我的编辑一直在改正我的拼写错误。

jam

n.果酱
v.（使）阻塞，使塞满
I like eating the bread with sweet jam.
我喜欢吃除了甜果酱的面包。

greet

v.问候，招待
This gentleman takes his hat off to greet people.
这位绅士摘下他的帽子，向人们问好。

former

adj.前任的，前者的，以前的
n.创造者
The first photograph is our former headmaster.
第一张照片是我们前任校长。

cage

n.笼，鸟笼
v.关进（笼子）
Get me out of this cage!Where’s the key?!
把我从这笼子里面放出去！钥匙在哪儿？！

garlic

n.大蒜
Garlic makes any meal taste better.
大蒜让所有菜肴更好吃了。

sentence

n.句子
v.判决，宣判
Note the importance of this sentence.
注意这个句子的重要性。

pilot

n.飞行员
adj.实验性的
v.给…领航，驾驶
Wow!The pilots in the movie look so cool!
哇塞！这部电影里的飞行员好酷好帅啊！

mustard

n.芥末
adj.芥末色的
I like to add some mustard on my sushi.
我喜欢往寿司上加点芥末。

agriculture

n.农业
We depend on agriculture to give us food.
我们依靠农业给我们提供食物。

against

prep.反对，对（着），逆
Only one person stood actively against him.
只有一个人站在那主动地反对他。

cap

n.帽子
The policeman wears a blue cap.
这个警察戴着蓝帽子。

plate

n.盘子，板，片
v.电镀
I can put much food on this big plate.
我可以放很多食物在这个大盘子里。

discovery

n.发现，被发现的事物
She makes a discovery in the field of flowers.
她在研究花的领域里有了新的发现。

outline

n.大纲，概要
v.描绘，画出…的轮廓
This outline of my invention should give you some idea of what I want.
这张关于我的发明的大纲图应该会让你知道我想要什么。

zip code

n.邮政编码
You must write a zip code on the envelope before mailing a letter.
寄信钱，你必须在信封上写上邮政编码。

lip

n.嘴唇，唇状物
Her lips are the most beautiful part of her face.
她的嘴唇是她脸上最漂亮的部分。

weed

n.杂草，野草
The worker is cutting the weeds.
工人正在除草。

vertical

adj.垂直的
n.垂直线，垂直面
The boy found the only vertical tree in the woods.
这个男孩找到了树林里唯一一颗垂直的树。

extraordinary

adj.非同寻常的
The teacher said my daughter has extraoidinary musical talent.
老师说我女儿在音乐方面天赋异禀。

death

n.死，死亡，灭亡
The death of her father broke her heart.
她父亲的死让她心碎。

tip

n.小费
v.给小费
I paid $5 to the waiter for tip.
我给了那个服务员5美元作为小费。

barber

n.理发师，理发店
I asked my barber to give me a new style this time.
这一次我让我的理发师给我理一个新发型。

veal

n.小牛肉
My favorite meat is veal.
我最爱吃的肉是小牛肉。

yoghurt

n.酸奶
Yoghurt tastes very delicious with cookies.
酸奶和饼干一起吃很美味哟！

wounded

adj.受伤的
The wounded soldier goes to the doctor every day.
这位负伤的士兵每天都去看医生。

pill

n.药丸
The woman took some pills when she was ill.
这个女人生病了，就吃了一些药。

goal

n.（进球）得分，目标，球门
They finally cracked the defence and scored a goal.
他们终于突破了防守，射进一球。

pure

adj.纯净的，纯粹的，纯洁的
The water seems so pure and clean.
水看起来真纯净。

regulation

n.法规，条例，规章
The traffic police is introducing traffic rules and regulations to us.
交警在向我们介绍交通法规。

Africa

n.非洲
Africa is home to some of the world’s oldest cultures.
非洲是世界上一些最古老文明的故乡。

motherland

n.祖国
Everyone has their own motherland.
每个人都有自己的祖国。

liberty

n.自由
The bird flew from the cage and got liberty.
这只鸟飞出了鸟笼得到了自由。

irrigate

v.灌溉，冲洗
This machine can irrigate the field.
这台机器可以灌溉田地。

accompany

v.陪伴，为…伴奏（或伴唱）
I always accompany my children to their school.
我总是陪着我的小孩们去学校。

typist

n.（尤指专职）打字员
The typist is finishing her documents.
打字员正在完成她的文件。

barrier

n.障碍
The road was closed by a barrier.
这条路被一个路障封锁了。

abroad

adv.到国外，在国外
The happy couple traveled abroad and took photos together.
这对幸福的夫妻出国旅游，并拍了照片。

biography

n.传记文学，传记
He likes reading the biography of famous people.
他喜欢读名人传记。

voyage

n.航海，航行
v.航海
The ship is on a voyage now.
这艘船正在航行中。

besides

prep.除…之外
adv.而且，还有
As the winner,I received lots of money besides the cup.
作为胜利者，我除了得到了奖杯外，还获得了一大笔钱。

fiber

n.纤维（物质）
We can see the fibers of the rope under a magnifying glass.
在放大镜下，我们能看见编成这条绳子的纤维。

roller

n.滚柱，滚筒，滚轴
Use the roller to paint the walls.
用来滚筒来刷墙。

chew

v.嚼，咀嚼
n.嚼碎，咀嚼
The boy is chewing the rice.
他在咀嚼米饭。

headmistress

n.女校长
Our headmistress is giving us a speech.
我们的女校长正在给我们演讲。

conventional

adj.依照惯例的，依照传统的
Although we can now pay online,some people still prefer the conventional method of cash.
尽管我们现在可以线上支付了，部分人仍然喜欢传统的现金支付手段。

sweet

adj.甜的，可爱的，美好的，芳香的
n.糖果，甜食
Most kids love sweet food such as candy,
大多数小孩都喜欢吃甜食，比如说糖。

yourselves

pron.你们自己，你们本人
Help yourselves to some food.
大家随便吃。

vast

adj.辽阔的
This vast desert looks impossible to pass.
这片辽阔的沙漠看上去不可能穿过。

toast

n.敬酒
v.为…祝酒
He held his glass and made a toast.
他举杯祝酒。

cyclist

n.骑脚踏车的人
The cyclist was determined to win the race.
这名车手决心赢得这场胜利。

department

n.部门，部，系
Our company consists of financial department, sales department,and technical department.
我们的公司由财务部、销售部和技术部组成。

mass

n.众多，群众
v.聚集
A mass of people are waiting.
一大堆人在等待。

serious

adj.认真的，严肃的
He is always very serious while working.
他工作时总是很认真。

emergency

n.紧急情况
It’s an emergency.Get her to the hospital now!
情况紧急，即刻送她去医院！

absolute

adj.完全的，绝对的
Motion is absolute,while stillness is relative.
运动是绝对的，静止是相对的。

tongue

n.舌头
vi.吹管音乐
The boy sticks out his tongue to make a face to me.
男孩伸出了舌头来对我做鬼脸。

instruct

v.指导，通知
My mom instructs me how to play golf.
我妈妈教我怎么打高尔夫。

nylon

n.尼龙，尼龙长袜
The strings are made of nylon.
这线是尼龙做的。

strange

adj.奇怪的，陌生的
I met a guy wearing strange clothes on the subway.
我在地铁上遇到了一个衣着奇怪的男人。
