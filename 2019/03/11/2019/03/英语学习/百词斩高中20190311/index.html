复习

omelet

n.煎蛋卷
I cooked myself a big omelet for breakfast.
我给自己做了一个大大的煎蛋卷当早餐。

journalist

n.记者，新闻工作者
The journalist is talking to that man.
记者正在和那个让你交谈。

thus

adv.因此，这样，如此
She studied hard,thus she got high marks.
她用功读书，因此获得高分。

amaze

v.使惊奇，使惊诧
This news really amazes me!
这条消息真的让我感到吃惊！

mourn

v.哀悼，哀伤
They are mourning the death of their friend.
他们在哀悼死去的朋友。

rat

n.老鼠，耗子
Look!The three rats are waving to us.
看，这三只老鼠在跟我们挥手。

heroine

n.女英雄
Every mother is a great heroine!
每位母亲都是伟大的英雄。

swear

v.发誓，郑重地说，咒骂
I swear I’m telling the truth.
我发誓我讲的是实话。

significance

n.重要性
The necklace is of great significance to her.
这条项链对她有着重要意义。

desert

n.沙漠
v.离开
I finally found water in the desert.
我终于在沙漠里找到水了。

perfume

n.香水
v.使充满芳香
This perfume smells like flowers.
这个香水闻起来像花一样。

whisper

vt.低声低语地讲，瞧瞧地说
v.耳语，密谈
n.私语，谣传
My friiend whispered a secret in my ear.
我的朋友在我耳边悄悄地告诉了我一个秘密。

uniform

n.制服，军服
adj.相同的，一致的，统一的
The nurses wear white uniforms when they work.
护士门工作的时候，穿着白色的制服。

fireplace

n.壁炉
We need to put more wood in the fireplace.
我们需要往壁炉里多加些木头。

virus

n.病毒，病毒性疾病
This virus is really dangerous.
这种病毒很危险。

distinction

n.优秀，卓越，杰出，差别，区分
Receiving a medal is a sign of distinction.
获得奖牌是优秀的象征。

quarter

n.四分之一
A quarter is one of four same parts that make it whole.
四分之一是四个完整相等且凑成整体的部分。

consultant

n.顾问，会诊医生，顾问医生
The consultant will explain how we should run our business.
顾问将会解释我们应该如何经营我们的事业。

occur

vi.发生
Car accidents can occur very quickly.
车祸会发生在一瞬间。

trainer

n.教练
My personal trainer makes sure that I exercise scientifically.
我的私人教练确保我科学地锻炼。

condition

n.环境，形势，条件
The hotel room is in poor condition.
这家宾馆的条件挺差。

foresee

v.预知，预料，预测
He foresaw that the stock market would rise.
他预测到了股市行情。

anxiety

n.焦虑
It is difficult to disguise her great anxiety.
很难掩饰她严重的焦虑。

orbit

v.环绕…的轨道运行
n.环境
vi.环绕…的轨道运行
All of our planets orbit the sun.
所有行星环绕太阳运行。

honest

adj.诚实的
“Wow,the emperor is naked!”,shouted an honest boy.
“哇哦，这个皇帝没穿衣服！”一个诚实的小男孩大叫到。

aim

v.瞄准
n.目标
The man aimed at the center of the mark.
这个男士瞄准了靶子的中心。

botanical

adj.植物（学）的
I went to the botanical gardens to see the beautiful plants.
我们去了植物园欣赏那些美丽的植物。

insect

n.昆虫
There’re many kinds of insects.
昆虫有很多不同的种类。

drug

n.药物，麻醉品，毒品
v.下麻药
This drug will lower your blood pressure.
这个药会降低你的血压。

cough

v.咳嗽
n.咳嗽
It’s polite to cover your mouth when you cough.
咳嗽的时候挡着嘴是种礼貌。

sidewalk

n.人行道
We walked down the sidewalk and enjoyed the nice summer day.
我们走在人行道上，享受着美好的夏日。

tremble

v.发抖，摇动
n.震颤
That movie made him tremble with fear.
这部电影让他害怕得发抖。

league

n.社团，联盟
v.使…结盟，与…联合
This is a group photo of our football league.
这是我们足球社团的一张合影。

founding

n.成立，建立
We celebrate the founding of the People’s Republic of China on every 1st October.
我们在每年十月一号都会庆祝中华人民共和国的成立。

crowd

n.人群，一群，朋友
v.挤满
There was a large crowd of people at the concert.
音乐会上有很多人。

cotton

n.棉花，棉线，棉布
Cotton feels soft and can be used to make clothes.
棉花摸起来很柔软，可以用来制作衣服。

challenge

v.挑战
n.挑战
He likes to climb and challenge himself.
他喜欢攀岩和挑战自我。

digest

v.消化
n.摘要
The food he ate was hard to digest.
他吃的食物很难消化。

necklace

n.项链
She likes to wear many necklaces.
她喜欢戴很多项链。

lavatory

n.厕所
The lavatory in the middle is for the disabled.
中间的厕所是我残疾人用的。

drawer

n.抽屉
Remember to keep all your files in the drawer.
记得把你所有文件的都放在抽屉里。

forehead

n.前额，前部
She cares so much about her forehead.
她非常在意她的前额。

botany

n.植物学
Botany is the study of plants.
植物学是研究植物的学科。

savage

n.野蛮人，粗鲁的人
adj.凶猛的，猛烈的
v.（狗等）乱咬
Their wordy conversation made me feel like a total savage!
他们文邹邹的对话让我觉得自己完全就像个野人！

actual

adj.实际的，真实的
The actual fact is that crocodiles walk underwater instead of swimming.
事实上，鳄鱼在水下是靠走而不是游泳。

新的学习

bomb

n.炸弹
v.投弹于，轰炸
Run!The bomb will go off in three seconds!
快跑！炸弹三秒钟后就会爆炸！

visual

adj.看的，视觉的
The color of this dress is just a visual trick.
这条裙子颜色只是一个视觉游戏罢了。

gravity

n.重力，引力
An apple fell on his head because of gravity.
苹果因为重力砸到了他的头上。

tear

v.撕裂，撕碎
n.眼泪
The hands tear the wall wide open.
一双手撕裂了这面墙。

glue

n.胶水
v.用胶粘
Glue is very useful for sticking things together!
要把东西粘一起，胶水会十分有用。

aluminium

n.铝
This can is made of aluminium.
这个罐子是铝做的。

unfortunately

adv.遗憾的是，可惜的是，不幸的是
My mom bought me an ice cream,but unfortunately I dropped it.
我妈给我买了个冰激淋，不幸的是，我把它掉地上了。

delight

n.高兴，愉快，高兴事
v.使高兴
“I finally got this bag!she yelled with delight.
“我终于买到这个包包了！”她高兴地尖叫着。

arithmetic

n.算术，计算
The boy is doing arithmetic homework.It’s easy for him.
那个小男孩在写算术作业，这对他来说很容易。

period

n.时期
Each class period is one hour.
每节课的时间是一小时。

electric

adj.电的，电动的，带电的
My wet hand caused me to get an electric shock.
我用湿手触碰了开关，然后就触电了。

reservation

n.（住处、席位的）预订
The couple had a reservation,so the table was saved for them.
这对夫妻已经有预订了，所以饭桌被保留了。

resemble

v.像，想像，相似
My dog resembles a brown mop.
我的狗像一根棕色的拖把。

betray

v.背叛，（非故意地）暴露
My girlfriend betrayed me.She fell in love with someone else.
我的女朋友背叛了我，她跟别人好上了。

abuse

v.虐待，辱骂，（对麻醉毒品、兴奋剂等的）非法服用
Stop abusing her,you evil old lady!!
不要再虐待她了，你这个坏嬷嬷！！

brake

v.刹车
n.刹车，制动器，闸
Not again - I hate having to brake my car for cows.
不要吧，又来？我讨厌为了牛刹车！

purple

adj.紫色的
n.紫色
The flower is purple.
这朵花是紫色的。

supper

n.晚餐
They are having their last supper together.
他们正在吃最后的晚餐。

fill in

phr.填充
Please fill in the form if you want to join our club.
如果你想加入我们俱乐部，请先填写这张表格。

conservative

adj.保守的，保守党的
n.保守主义者
The man has such a conservative attitude he even despises the couple holding hands.
他保守得连情侣牵手都看不惯。

widespread

adj.分布广的，普遍的，广泛的
My love story quickly became widespread through the Internet.
通过网络，我的爱情故事很快传播开了。

attraction

n.吸引，魅力，吸引力
There is a strong attraction between the lovers.
这对恋人之间有着强烈的吸引力。

immigration

n.移民，移居入境
Immigration to Germany has risen in recent times.
如今，向德国的移民越来越多了。

popcorn

n.爆米花
I like eating popcorn when I watch a movie.
我喜欢在看电影时吃爆米花。

spy

n.间谍，密探
v.当间谍，暗中监视（或侦查）
The spy is passing messages to our enemy.
那个间谍正在给我们的敌人传递情报。

effect

n.效果，所有物
v.使发生，引起
I like the light effects on the stage.
我喜欢舞台上的灯光效果。

afterwards

adv.然后
They had a romantic dinner and decided to watch a movie afterwards.
他们吃了一顿浪漫的晚餐，然后决定去看电影。

violin

n.小提琴
She can play the violin well.
她小提琴拉的很好。

attach

v.贴，系，附上
The card player has several pieces of paper attached to his face.
这个打牌的人脸上被贴了好多纸条啊。

anniversary

n.周年纪念日
adj.周年的，（有关）周年纪念的
He gave his wife a diamond ring on their tenth anniversary.
在结婚十周年之际，他送了老婆一枚钻戒。

apparent

adj.显然的，明白的，清晰可见的，表面上的,貌似（真实）的
It’s apparent that the boy is taller than the girl.
很明显，那个男孩比女孩高。

benefit

n.好处，益处
v.受益，有助于，使受益
Jogging has a lot of benefits for people’s health.
慢跑对人体健康有诸多好处。

kind

adj.善良的，仁慈的，亲切的，和蔼的
n.种类
He’s a kind young man who always helps others.
他是个善良的年轻人，总是帮助别人。

statue

n.雕像
There stands a huge status in New York city.
一座巨大的雕像坐落在纽约市。

adolescence

n.青春期，青春
He began to shave after hitting adolescence.
进入青春期后，他开始刮胡子了。

grape

n.葡萄，葡萄藤
There are a lot of grapes in the garden.
果园里有很多葡萄。

receipt

n.收据，收条，收入
You will get a receipt when you buy things in a store.
当你在店里买东西时，会得到一个收据。

biochemistry

n.生物化学
Biochemistry is a difficult area of science to study.
生化是科学中最难学习的领域。

tax free

adj.免税的
Many people go to Hong Kong for tax free goods.
许多人去香港买免税商品。

taste

v.品尝，尝起来，品鉴
n.鉴赏力
This ice cream bar tastes so good!
这个雪糕真好吃！

unbreakable

adj.牢不可破的，不易损坏的
This iron door seems to be unbreakable.
这扇铁门看起来牢不可破。

washroom

n.盥洗室
May I know where the washroom is?
卫生间在哪里？

education

n.教育，培养
Education is important for kids.
教育对孩子十分重要。

choir

n.（教会的）歌唱队，唱诗班
A penguin choir sings Christmas songs.
一个企鹅唱诗班唱着圣诞歌。

rewind

v.倒回（磁带等），倒带
Rewind the song,I want to hear it again.
回放这首歌，我想再听一遍。

sow

v.播种
n.母猪
The first stage to growing crops is to first sow the seeds.
培育庄稼的第一步是播种。

indicate

vt.表明，表示，显示
Can you guess what the graph indicates?
你能猜到这个图表表明着什么吗？

shock

v.（使）震惊，（使）震动
n.震动，震惊，冲击，休克
She was shocked to see how much her stock value had fallen.
看到股票跌了这么多，她真是惊呆了！

cosy

adj.温暖而舒适的，安逸的
The cat seems so cosy sleeping in the soft blanket.
小猫睡在那个柔软的毯子里面，看起来舒适极了。

eager

adj.热切的，渴望的，急不可耐的
The kids are eager to eat the cake on the table.
孩子们急不可耐想要吃掉桌上的蛋糕。

federal

adj.联邦制的，联邦政府的
The USA is a federal republic.
美国是一个联邦共和国。

entertainment

n.娱乐，招待，款待
We go to the movies for entertainment.
我们看电影是为了娱乐。

stubborn

adj.顽固的，难对付的
Both of them are stubborn and don’t want to say sorry first.
他俩都很顽固，不愿意第一个道歉。
