复习

badminton

n.羽毛球，羽毛球运动
Playint badminton on the grass makes the little boy so happy.
在草地上打羽毛球让小男孩特别开心。

bench

n.长凳，条凳，长形工作台
The boy is sitting on the bench with his mother.
小男孩正和他的妈妈坐在长凳上。

diamond

n.钻石
Diamond rings are very popular among young couples.
钻戒在年轻夫妻中十分流行。

bucket

n.水桶，（机器上的）桶装物，大量
The cute cat is hiding in a bucket.
那只可爱的小猫躲在一个桶子里。

Antarctic

n.南极区
adj.南极的
The Antarctic is a cold place - only penguins can survive there.
南极是一个寒冷的地方，只有企鹅能在那里生存。

graduation

n.毕业，毕业典礼
Throwing hats is a tradition during graduation.
抛帽子是毕业时的一项传统。

punishment

n.惩罚，处罚
The student was given punishment for doing something wrong.
这个学生因为做错事受到了惩罚。

disgusting

adj.令人厌恶的，令人无法接受的
He finds the smell of the sock disgusting.
袜子的味道在让他恶心。

apartment

n.（美）公寓，一套房间
This apartment is so bright with these windows.
这套公寓窗户多，很明亮。

aparrow

n.麻雀
Look!The little aparrow is resting on the tree branch.
快看这只小麻雀，在树枝上休息呢。

mud

n.泥
On no!I think our car s stuck in the mud!
哦不！我想我们的吃卡泥里了！

colleague

n.同事，同行
I really learned a lot by discussing with my colleagues.
通过和同事们讨论，我真的学到了很多。

associate

v.联想，联合，联系，交往
n.伙伴，同事
adj.副的
I associate this picture with a star.
这图片让我联想到了星星。

senior

adj.年长的，资格老的，地位较高的
n.（大学或高中）毕业生
Senior citizens should be respected.
老年人应该被尊重。

parcel

n.包裹
The person has a lot of parcels to send on November 11th.
这个人在双十一有很多包裹要送。

suspect

n.嫌疑犯，可疑分子
v.怀疑，猜疑，推测
adj.可疑的
The suspect was body-searched by a policeman.
警察对嫌疑犯进行了搜身。

ladder

n.梯子，阶梯
The man is standing on a ladder.
这个男人正站在梯子上。

weakness

n.（性格、系统）弱点，虚弱
Vampires’weakness is that they are afraid of sunshine.
吸血鬼的弱点是害怕阳光。

forgive

v.原谅，饶恕，宽恕
“Please forgive me,my dear” the man said to his girlfriend.
“原谅我吧，亲爱的”这个男人向他女朋友说。

decision

n.决定
I’ve made my decision,I find the defendant guilty!
我已做出决定，我认为被告罪名成立！

rid

v.使摆脱
Thank god!Finally I have gotten rid of all my debts.
谢天谢地！我终于摆脱了全部债务！

amateur

adj.业余爱好，业余（身份）的
n.业余爱好者，外行
This office worker is also an amateur soccer player.
这个上班族也是一名业余球员。

marble

n.大理石，（游戏用的）玻璃弹子
The table is made of marble.
这张桌子是有大理石做的。

tortoise

n.龟
The tortoise is slowly crossing the field.
乌龟慢慢地在地上爬。

radiation

n.辐射物，放射物
Don’t get closer to radiation if you are not in protective suit.
没有穿防辐射服就不要靠近。

constant

adj.持续不断的，不变的，始终如一的
n.常数，恒量
The constant noise from the construction site makes me crazy.
建筑工地持续不断的噪音让我快疯啦！

carpenter

n.木匠，木工
The carpenter works on the house.
木匠从事房屋建造工作。

abolish

v.彻底废除
The black slavery of America was abolished by the persident in 1865.
这位总统于1865年废除了美国的奴隶制度。

glance

v.看一下
n.一瞥，扫视
She glanced at his computer screen to see what he was watching.
她扫了一眼他的电脑想知道他在看什么。

beyond

prep.超过，在能力、理解力之外，除…之外
adv.更远处，更多，以后
I couldn’t hit him as he was beyond my reach.
我大不了他，因为我更不够不着他。

pregnant

adj.怀孕的
The pregnant woman is drinking water.
这个怀孕的女人正在喝水。

新的学习

call on

phr.拜访，呼吁，请求
The family called on their neighbor on Christmas Day.
这贱人在圣诞节拜访了他们的邻居。

dawn

n.黎明
v.破晓
The rooster standing on the field crows at dawn.
田野里的公鸡在黎明时分打鸣。

zip

v.拉拉链，飕飕飞过，注入活力
n.拉链，活力，飞射声
I zip up my jeans pocket to keep my money safe.
我拉上牛仔裤兜的拉链来确保钱的安全。

comprehension

n.理解力，测试
These guys must have a high level of comprehension to understand this.
这两人肯定有非常好的理解力才能看懂这幅画。

exploit

v.剥削，利用，开发，开拓
n.辉煌功绩
The greedy employer exploits his workers for his own profits.
这个贪婪的老板为了自己的利益剥削工人。

grasp

v.抓紧，领会
n.紧握
I grasp the money in my right hand.
我把钱紧紧地抓在右手里。

breast

n.胸膛，乳房，胸脯肉
She held her baby to her breast to give some milk.
她把宝宝放在胸前喂母乳。

unfair

adj.不公平的，不公正的
It’s unfair!Her food is much more than mine!
这不公平！她的食物比我多那么多！

spiritual

adj.精神（上）的，宗教（上）的
Living a simple life,she devoted herself to the pursuit of spiritual world.
她过着简朴的生活，致力于追求自己的精神世界。

assumption

n.假定，设想
Having a baby is just their assumption.
有个宝宝只是他们的设想而已。

strait

n.海峡，窘迫
It may take 2 hours to swim across the strait.
有过这湾海峡可能得花两个小时。

situation

n.形势，局面，处境，状况
It’s hard to choose which way to go in this situation.
这种情形下很难选择出路。

algebra

n.代数
I don’t know how to solve this algebra porblem.
我不知道怎么解决这个代数题。

hobby

n.业余爱好，癖好
People have different hobbies,some like reading books and some like playing football.
人们有不同的业余爱好，有人喜欢读书，有人喜欢踢球。

tired

adj.疲劳的
She is so tired after running.
她跑完步很累。

Switzerland

n.瑞士
There are many snowy mountains in Switzerland.It’s so cold!
瑞士有很多雪山，非常冷。

port

n.港，港口
The city has a port where you can dock your boat.
这个城市有一个能让你的船靠岸的港口。

coincidence

n.巧合，巧事
They’re wearing the same dress today.what a coincidence!
她们今天穿了一样的衣服，真是太巧了！

liter

n.升，公升
This bottle can be filled with 2 liters of coke.
这个瓶子可以装两升可乐。

frighten

My brother jumped out of the closet and frightened me.
我的弟弟从衣柜里跳出来，吓到我了。

ink

n.墨水，油墨
Spilled ink is hard to clean.
洒出来的墨水很难清理。

ruin

v.毁灭，毁掉，（使）破产
n.毁灭，废墟
These buildings were ruined by a powerful earthquake.
一场大地震摧毁了这些建筑。

excite

v.使激动，使兴奋
She was excited when she received email from the interviewer.
收到面试官的邮件时她很激动。

direction

n.方向，方位，指令，说明
The road goes in many directions.
道路四通八达。

delighted

adj.欣喜的，快乐的
She was very delighted that she got full marks in the final exam.
期末考试得了一百分，她开心得不得了。

adopt

v.收养
The family decided to adopt this dog at the shelter.
这家人决定收养收容所这条狗。

liberation

n.解脱，解放
The slave got his liberation at last.
这个奴隶最终得以解放。

foggy

adj.有雾的，模糊的
We got lost in the foggy forest.
我们在迷雾森里中迷路了。

republic

n.共和国，共和政体
The United States is a republic.
美国是一个共和国。

wage

n.薪水，报酬
v.开展，进行（战争、运动）
The worker was so happy when she got her wages.
这个工人拿到工资的时候很开心。

bandage

n.绷带
v.用绷带绑扎
I hurt my hand,so I wrapped it in a bandage.
我把手弄伤了，所以包了绷带。

distant

adj.遥远的
The destination is still distant,we will not get there for a while.
目的地还很远，我们一时半会到不了。

bond

n.联系，束缚物，联结物
vi.团结一致
Everyone has a bond with another person in this web.
在这张网络中，每个人都与其他人有关系。

basin

n.水池，流域，盆
The basin in our bathroom was very clean.
我们浴室的水池很干净。

condemn

v.谴责，责备
We all condemned him for his bad behavior.
我们都指责他的坏行为。

superior

adj.较高级的，上层的，出众的
n.上级，高手
The boy’s five-stripe badge shows that he is superior to the other boy.
他肩上的五道杠表明他比另一个人级别高。

deed

n.行为
Superman did many good deeds for people.
超人为人们做了很多好事。

vivid

adj.（颜色）鲜艳的，强烈的（颜色，光），生动的，清晰的（记忆、描述）
She is dressed in vivid colors.
她穿的很鲜艳。

ownership

n.所有权
He was excited about his ownership of a new car.
拥有一辆新车真让他感到兴奋。

useless

adj.无用的，无价值的，无效的
The black computer is useless.
这台黑色电脑没用了。

existence

n.存在，生活方式
Do you believe in the existence of dragons?
你相信龙的存在吗？

awful

adj.糟糕的，讨厌的，可怕的，极坏的
This fish smells awful.Take it away!
这条鱼的气味太糟糕了，快把它拿走！

prevent

v.预防，阻止
The mother prevented her child from eating sweets.
妈妈不让她的小孩吃糖。

instrument

n.乐器，工具
Which is your favorite musical instrument?
你喜欢的乐器是哪一种？

circulate

v.（使）循环
Use the fan to circulate the air in the room.
开风扇让空气在房间里循环起来。

entrance

n.入口，通道，大门口
The entrance is over there.Follow the red carpet.
入口在哪边，顺着红毯过去吧。

roof

n.屋顶，顶部
The cats climbed on the roof of the house.
猫咪爬上了屋顶。

telegram

n.电报
They used to use telegrams for messages.
他们曾经用电报发信息。

fortnight

n.两星期，十四日
She can keep the book for a fortnight,which means two weeks.
她借书期限是两周。

drawback

n.缺点，障碍，不利条件
His short arms are his biggest drawback,he’s struggling to reach the toilet paper!
他的小短手是他最大的缺点，他很努力地去拿厕纸。
