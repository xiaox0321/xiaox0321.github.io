复习

visual

adj.看的，视觉的
The color of this dress is just a visual trick.
这条裙子颜色只是一个视觉游戏罢了。

gravity

n.重力，引力
An apple fell on his head because of gravity.
苹果因为重力砸到了他的头上。

tear

v.撕裂，撕碎
n.眼泪
The hands tear the wall wide open.
一双手撕裂了这面墙。

glue

n.胶水
v.用胶粘
Glue is very useful for sticking things together!
要把东西粘在一起，胶水会十分有用。

aluminium

n.铝
This can is made of aluminium.
这个罐子是铝做的。

unfortunately

adv.遗憾的是，可惜的是，不幸的是
My mom bought me an ice cream,but unfortunately I dropped it.
我妈给我买了个冰激淋，不幸的是，我把它掉地上了。

delight

n.高兴，愉快，高兴事
v.使高兴
“I finally got this bag!” she yelled with delight.
“我终于买到这个包包了！”她高兴地尖叫着。

arithmetic

n.算术，计算
The boy is doing arithmetic homework.It’s easy for him.
那个小男孩在写算术作业。这对他来说很容易。

period

n.时期
Each class period is one hour.
每节课的时间是一小时。

reservation

n.（住处、席位的）预订
The couple had a reservation,so the table was saved for them.
这对夫妇已经有预订了，所以饭桌被保留了。

resemble

v.像，想像，相似
My dog resembles a brown mop.
我的狗像一根棕色的拖把。

betray

v.背叛，（非故意地）暴露
My girlfriend betrayed me.She fell in love with someone else.
我的女朋友背叛了我，她跟别人好上了。

abuse

v.虐待，辱骂
Stop abusing her,you evil old lady!
不要再虐待她了，你这个坏嬷嬷！

brake

v.刹车
n.刹车，制动器，闸
Not again – I hate having to brake my car for cows.
不要吧，又来，我讨厌为牛刹车。

purple

adj.紫色的
n.紫色
The flower is purple.
这朵花是紫色的。

supper

n.晚餐
They are having their last supper together.
他们正在吃着最后的晚餐。

conservative

adj.保守的，保守党的
n.保守主义的
The man has such a conservative attitude he even despises the couple holding hands.
他保守得连情侣牵手都看不惯。

widespread

adj.分布广的，普遍的，广泛的
My love story quickly became widespread through the Internet.
通过网络，我的爱情故事很快传播开了。

attraction

n.吸引，魅力，吸引力
There is a strong attraction between the lovers.
这对恋人之间有强烈的吸引力。

immigration

n.移民，移民入境
Immigration to Germany has risen in recent times.
如今，向德国的移民越来越多了。

popcorn

n.爆米花
I like eating popcorn when I watch a movie.
我喜欢在看电影时吃爆米花。

spy

n.间谍，密探
v.当间谍，暗中监视（或侦察）
The spy is passing messages to our enemy.
那个间谍正在给我们的敌人传递情报。

effect

n.效果，所有物
v.使发生，引起
I like the light effects on the stage.
我喜欢舞台上的灯光效果。

afterwards

adv.然后
They had a romantic dinner and decided to watch a movie afterwards.
他们吃了一顿浪漫的晚餐，然后决定去看电影。

violin

n.小提琴
She can play the violin well.
她小提琴拉的很好。

attach

v.贴，系，附上
The card player has several pieces of paper attached to his face.
这个打牌的人脸上被贴了好多纸条。

anniversary

n.周年纪念日
adj.周年的，（有关）周年纪念的
He gave his wife a diamond ring on their tenth anniversary.
在结婚十周年之际，他送了老婆一枚钻戒。

apparent

adj.显然的，明白的，清晰可见的，表面上的，貌似（真实）的
It’s apparent that the boy is taller than the girl.
很明显，那个男孩比女孩高。

benefit

n.好处，益处
v.受益，有助于，使受益
Jogging has a lot of benefits of people’s health.
慢跑对人体健康有诸多好处。

kind

adj.善良的，仁慈的，亲切的，和蔼的
n.种类
He’s a kind young man who always helps others.
他是个善良的年轻人，总是帮助别人。

statue

n.雕像
There stands a huge statue in New York city.
一座巨大的雕像坐落在纽约市。

adolescence

n.青春期，青春
He began to shave after hitting adolescence.
进入青春期后，他开始刮胡子了。

grape

n.葡萄，葡萄藤
There are a lot of grapes in the garden.
果园里有很多葡萄。

receipt

n.收据，收条，收入
You will get a receipt when you buy things in a store.
当你在店里买东西时，会得到一个收据。

biochemistry

n.生物化学
Biochemistry is a difficult area of science to study.
生化是科学中很难学习的领域。

unbreakable

adj.牢不可破的，不易损坏的
This iron door seems to be unbreakable.
这扇铁门看起来牢不可破。

washroom

n.盥洗室
May I know where the washroom is?
卫生间在哪里？

indicate

vt.表明，表示，显示
Can you guess what the graph indicates?
你能猜到这个图表表明着什么吗？

shock

v.（使）震惊，（使）震动
n.震动，震惊，冲击，休克
She was shocked to see how much her stock value had fallen.
看到股票跌了这么多，她真是惊呆了。

cosy

adj.温暖而舒适的，安逸的
The cat seems so cosy sleeping in the soft blanket.
小猫睡在那个柔软的毯子里面，看起来真是舒服极了。

eager

adj.热切的，渴望的，急不可耐的
The kids are eager to eat the cake on the table.
孩子们急不可耐想要吃掉桌上的蛋糕。

federal

adj.联邦制的，联邦政府的
The USA is a federal republic.
美国是一个联邦共和国。

entertainment

n.娱乐，招待，款待
We go to the movies for entertainment.
我们看电影是为了娱乐。

stubborn

adj.顽固的，难对付的
Both of them are stubborn and don’t want to say sorry first.
他俩都很顽固，不愿意第一个道歉。

organ

n.器官
Every organ plays an important role in our body.
每一个器官在我们身体中都扮演着重要的角色。

ceremony

n.典礼，仪式
The student was awarded his certificate during the graduation ceremony.
毕业生在毕业典礼上接受了他的毕业证书。

patience

n.耐心，忍耐
Fishing demands a great deal of patience.
钓鱼需要极大的耐心。

merchant

n.商人，批发商
The merchant checks the quality of his produce.
商人在检查农产品质量。

新的学习

handsome

adj.英俊的，帅气的，漂亮的，大方的
The handsome man posed for pictures.
这位英俊的男子摆好姿势准备照相。

slavery

n.奴隶制，奴隶身份
Lincoln announced the slavery should be abolished.
林肯宣布奴隶制度应被废除。

celebrate

v.庆祝，赞美
We are celebrating the coming New Year!
我们正为新年的到来而庆祝！

rough

adj.粗燥的
The hands of the marmer are rough.
这个农民的手指很粗糙。

fancy

adj.华丽装饰的，别致的，引人注目的
n.喜爱，幻想，想像力
vt.想像，幻想
This fancy gold tableware must have been so expensive!
这套华丽的金色餐具肯定很贵！

immediate

adj.立即的，立刻的
This letter demands my immediate response.
这封信需要我立即回复。

walnut

n.核桃，胡桃
Look,this walnut has a heart-shaped core.
看，这个核桃仁是心性的。

organize

v.整理，组织
Please organize the cards by number.
请按编号整理好卡片。

skipping rope

n.（跳绳用）绳
My skipping rope keeps me fit!
跳绳帮助我保持身材。

warehouse

n.仓库
The warehouse is full of boxes.
仓库里堆满了货箱。

litter

n.垃圾
v.乱丢
Lots of litter was left on the beach by tourists.
沙滩上有很多游客留下的垃圾。

liberate

v.解放，释放
She felt her hear liberated together with the birds.
她感到自己的心随着鸟儿一起被解放了。

national

adj.国家的，民族的
n.国民
Baseball is the national game of the U.S.
棒球是美国的国民运动。

humorous

adj.幽默的，搞笑的，诙谐的
This guy was so humorous and made everyone laugh.
这个男人很幽默，把大家都逗笑了。

instant

adj.即食的
n.瞬间
Making a cup of instant coffee is a simple process.
制作一杯速溶咖啡的过程很简单。

hatch

v.孵出，孵
n.船舱盖
Look,the eggs are hatching!
看啊，小鸡出壳了！

thunder

n.雷，雷声，轰隆声
v.大声抱怨，吼叫
Thunder and lightning scare me so much!
打雷和闪电吓坏我了！

convenience

n.方便
Sometimes I eat pre-cooked meals for convenience.
有时为了方便，我会吃事先做好的饭菜。

container

n.集装箱
There are a lot of containers at the port.
港口有很多集装箱。

anchor

n.锚，担任（广播电视新闻节目）的主持人
The ship dropped the anchor and hit the shark on head.
船抛出锚，打到了一只鲨鱼。

wedding

n.婚礼，婚宴
The couple kissed each other at their wedding.
新婚夫妇在婚礼上亲吻对方。

dilemma

n.进退两难的窘境
I am in a dilemma.Both ways are so dangerous!
我处在进退两难的困境里，两条路都好危险啊！

institution

n.机构
The United Nations is an international institution.
联合国是一家国际机构。

violence

n.暴力
The two boys used violence to end the quarrel.
这两个男孩动用武力结束了争吵。

offshore

adj.近海的，海岸的
adv.离岸地，近海地
Offshore oil extraction is the best way to get oil.
近海原油开采是得到石油的最好方式。

Atlantic

adj.大西洋的
n.大西洋
The northern part of Atlantic Ocean lies between Europe and North America.
北大西洋位于欧洲合和北美洲之间。

specialist

n.专家，专科医生
adj.专家的
He is a specialist in robot.
他是个机器人专家。

refreshments

n.点心，茶点
Help yourself to the refreshments on the table.
请自取桌上的茶点。

unconscious

adj.不省人事的，未察觉的
We found him unconscious on the floor.
我们发现他倒在地上，不省人事。

devote

vt.奉献
The woman devotes her entire life to her religion.
这个女人把一辈子都献给了宗教信仰。

donate

v.捐赠
We donated a sum of money to the charity.
我们向慈善机构捐了一笔钱。

comrade

n.战友
My comrade and I are good friends.
我和我的战友是最好的朋友。

gradually

adv.逐步地
The full moon gradually changes to a new moon.
圆月会逐渐变成新月。

expect

v.期待
She’s expecting her baby to be born soon.
她期待着宝宝的到来。

ballpoint

n.圆珠笔
Ballpoint pens are cheap and easy to write with.
圆珠笔便宜又好用。

sheet

n.纸张，被单
Please write your name on this sheet of paper.
请在这张纸上写下你的名字。

alphabet

n.字母表
The English alphabet has 26 letters.
英语字母表有26个字母。

guarantee

v.保证，担保
n.保证金
I guarantee that you will have our best service.
我保证你会得到我们最优质的服务。

indeed

adv.的确，真正地，事实上
My coworkers told me that I did a good job indeed.
我的同事告诉我真的干的不错！

achievement

n.成绩，成就，实现，完成
He is known for his great achievements in the sport of tennis.
他在网球运动上的成就令人瞩目。

quite

adv.相当，十分
My hair is quite soft and smooth.
我的头发很柔顺。

transparent

adj.透明的，清澈的，明净的
The bear didn’t see the transparent glass,so he hit it.
小熊没看见这块透明的玻璃，然后就撞上了。

due to

prep.由于，取决于
She cannot hear things clearly due to her old age.
由于年岁过高，她的耳朵听不太清。
