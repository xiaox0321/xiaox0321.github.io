复习

comprehension

n.理解力，测试
These guys must have a high level of comprehension to understand this.
这俩人肯定有非常好的理解力才能看懂这幅画。

exploit

v.剥削，利用，开发，开拓
n.辉煌功绩
The greedy employer exploits his workers for his own profits.
这个贪婪的老板为了自己的利益剥削工人。

grasp

v.抓住，领会
n.紧握
I grasp the money in my right hand.
我把钱紧紧地抓在右手里。

breast

n.胸膛，乳房，胸脯肉
She held her baby to her breast to give some milk.
她把宝宝放在胸前喂母乳。

spiritual

adj.精神（上）的，宗教（上）的
Living a simple life,she devoted herself to the pursuit of spiritual world.
她过着简朴的生活，致力于追求自己的精神世界。

assumption

n.假定，设想
Having a baby is just their assumption.
有个宝宝只是他们的设想而已。

strait

n.海峡，窘迫
It may take 2 hours to swim across the strait.
游过这湾海峡可能得花2小时。

situation

n.形势，局面，处境，状况
It’s hard to choose which way to go in this situation.
这种情形下很难选择出路。

algebra

n.代数
I don’t know how to solve this algebra problem.
我不知道怎么解这个代数题。

hobby

n.业余爱好，癖好
People have different hobbies,some like reading books and some like playing football.
人们有不同的业余爱好，有人喜欢读书，有人喜欢踢球。

coincidence

n.巧合，巧事
They’re wearing the same dresss today,what a coincidence!
她们今天穿了一样的衣服，真是太巧了！

liter

n.升，公升
This bottle can be filled with 2 liter of coke.
这个瓶子可以装两升可乐。

righten

v.使惊恐，使害怕
My brother jumped out of the closet and frightened me.
我弟弟从衣柜里跳出来，吓到我了。

ruin

v.毁灭，毁坏，（使）破产
n.毁灭，废墟
These buildings were ruined by a powerful earthquake.
一场大地震摧毁了这些建筑。

excite

v.使激动，使兴奋
She was excited when she received email from the interviewer.
收到面试官的邮件时她很激动。

direction

n.方向，方位，指令，说明
The road goes in many directions.
道路四通八达。

delighted

adj.欣喜的，快乐的
She was very delighted that she got full marks in the final exam.
期末考试得了一百分，她开心得不得了！

adopt

v.收养
The family decided to adopt this dog at the shelter.
这家人决定收养收容所的这只狗。

liberation

n.解脱，解放
The slave got his liberation at last.
这个奴隶最终得以解放。

foggy

adj.有雾的，模糊的
We got lost in the foggy forest.
我们在迷雾森林中迷路了。

wage

n.薪水，报酬
v.开展，进行（战争、运动）
The worker was so happy when she got her wages.
这个工人拿到工资的时候，很开心。

bandage

n.绷带
v.用绷带绑扎
I hurt my hand,so I wrapped it in a bandage.
我把手弄伤了，所以包了绷带。

distant

adj.遥远的
The destination is still distant,we will not get there for a while.
目的地还很远，我们一时半会儿到不了。

bond

n.联系，束缚物，联结物
vi.团结一致
Everyone has a bond with another person in this web.
在这张网络中，每个人都与其他人有关系。

basin

n.水池，流域，盐
The basin in our bathroom was very clean.
我们浴室的水池很干净。

condemn

v.谴责，责备
We all condemned him for his bad behavior.
我们都指责他的坏行为。

superior

adj.较高级的，上层的，出众的
n.上级，高手
The boy’s five-stripe badge shows that he is superior to the other boy.
他肩上的五道杠表明他比另一个人级别高。

deed

n.行为
Superman did many good deeds for people.
超人为人们做了很多好事。

vivid

adj.（颜色）鲜艳的，强烈的（颜色，光），生动的，清晰的（记忆、描述）
She is dressed in vivid colors.
她穿的很鲜艳。

awful

adj.糟糕的，讨厌的，可怕的，极坏的
This fish smells awful.Take it away!
这条鱼的气味太糟糕了，快把它拿走！

prevent

v.预防，阻止
The mother prevented her child from eating sweets.
妈妈不让她的小孩吃糖。

instrument

n.乐器，工具
Which is your favorite musical instrument?
你最喜欢的乐器是哪一种？

circulate

v.（使）循环
Use the fan to circulate the air in the room.
开风扇让空气在房间里循环起来。

entrance

n.入口，通道，大门口
The entrance is over there.Follow the red carpet.
入口在那边，顺着红毯过去吧。

roof

n.屋顶，顶部
The cats climbed on the roof of the house.
猫咪爬上了屋顶。

telegram

n.电报
They used to use telegrams for messages.
他们曾经用电报发送信息。

fortnight

n.两星期，十四日
She can keep the book for a fortnight, which means two weeks.
她的借书期限是两周。

drawback

n.缺点，障碍，不利条件
His short arms are his biggest drawback,he’s struggling to reach the toilet paper!
他的小短手是他最大的确定，他很努力地去拿厕纸。

component

n.零件
adj.组成的
These are the components of a car.
这些是一辆车的零部件。

permanent

adj.永久的，持久的
Her eyebrows are being made perrmanent.
这位女士正在做永久纹眉。

smog

n.烟雾
There is too much smog in this big city.
这个大城市里烟雾缭绕。

geometry

n.几何学，几何形状
Geometry is the study dealing with different kinds of shapes.
几何学是研究各种不同图形的学科。

adequate

adj.充足的，充分的，足够的，尚可的，过得去的
I have prepared an adequate amount of food for 5 people.
我为五个人准备了充足的食物。

delicate

adj.易碎的，易坏的，脆弱的
Porcelain cups are very delicate,do not drop them!
瓷杯易碎，小心掉落！

beast

n.野兽
The beast dances with the beauty.
野兽与美女共舞。

pulse

n.脉搏
v.（使）跳动，搏动，震动，（感情等）洋溢，充满
The doctor felt the patient’s pulse.
医生给病人把了脉。

新的学习

omelet

n.煎蛋卷
I cooked myself a big omelet for breakfast.
我给自己做了一个大大的煎蛋卷当早餐。

endless

adj.没完没了的
This road seems endless.When can we get there?
这条路看起来没有尽头。我们什么时候才可以到?

journalist

n.记者，新闻工作者
The journalist is talking to that man.
记者正在和那个人交谈。

thus

adv.因此，这样，如此
She studied hard.thus she got high marks.
她用功读书，因此获得高分。

amaze

v.使惊奇，使惊诧
This news really amazes me!
这条消息真的让我感到很吃惊！

mourn

v.哀悼，哀伤
They are mourning the death of their friend.
他们在哀悼死去的朋友。

rat

n.老鼠，耗子
Look!The three rats are waving to us.
看，这三只老鼠在跟我们挥手。

heroine

n.女英雄
Every mother is a great heroine!
每位母亲都是伟大的英雄！

swear

v.发誓，郑重地说，咒骂
I swear I’m telling the truth.
我发誓我讲的是实话。

significance

n.重要性
The necklace is of great significance to her.
这条项链对她有很重要意义。

desert

n.沙漠
v.离开
I finally found water in the desert.
我终于在沙漠里找到水了。

prize

n.奖品，或赏
adj.作为奖品的，获奖的
v.珍视，宝贵
The winner of the tournament will be given this prize.
联赛的冠军会被授予这个奖杯。

perfume

n.香水
v.使充满芳香
This perfume smells like flowers.
这个香水闻起来像花一样。

whisper

vt.低声低语地讲，悄悄地说
v.耳语，密谈
n.私语，谣传
My friend whispered a secret in my ear.
我的朋友在我耳边悄悄地告诉了我一个秘密。

uniform

n.制服，军服
adj.相同的，一致的，统一的
The nurses wear white uniforms when they work.
护士门工作的时候，穿着白色的制服。

fireplace

n.壁炉
We need to put more wood in the fireplace.
我们需要往壁炉里多加些木头。

virus

n.病毒，病毒性疾病
This virus is really dangerous.
这种病毒很危险。

distinction

n.优秀，卓越，杰出，差别，区分
Receiving a medal is a sign of distinction.
获得奖牌是优秀的象征。

quarter

n.四分之一
A quarter is one of four same parts that make it whole.
四分之一是四个完整相等且凑成整体的部分。

consultant

n.顾问，会诊医师，顾问医生
The consultant will explain how we should run our business.
顾问将会解释我们应该如何经营我们的事业。

occur

vi.发生
Car accidents can occur very quickly.
车祸会发生在一瞬间。

trainer

n.教练
My personal trainer makes sure that I exercise scientifically.
我的私人教练确保我科学地锻炼。

condition

n.环境，形势，条件
The hotel room is in poor condition.
这家宾馆的条件挺差。

foresee

v.预知，预料，预测
He foresaw that the stock market would rise.
他预测到了股市行情。

anxiety

n.焦虑
It is difficult to disguise her great anxiety.
很难掩饰她的严重的焦虑。

orbit

v.环绕…的轨道运行
n.轨道
vi.环绕…的轨道运行
All of our planets orbit the sun.
所有行星环绕太阳运行。

honest

adj.诚实的
“Wow,the emperor is naked!”shouted an honest boy.
“哇哦，这个皇帝没穿衣服！”一个诚实的小男孩大叫道。

aim

v.瞄准
n.目标
The man aimed at the center of the mark.
这个男人瞄准了靶子的中心。

botanical

adj.植物（学）的
I went to the botanical gardens to see the beautiful plants.
我们去了植物园欣赏那些美丽的植物。

insect

n.昆虫
There’re many kinds of insects.
昆虫很很多不同的种类。

drug

n.药物，麻醉品，毒品
v.下麻药
This drug will lower your blood pressure.
这个药会降低你的血压。

pineapple

n.凤梨，菠萝
Pineapple are my favorite fruit in the summer.
菠萝是我在夏天最喜欢吃的水果。

cough

v.咳嗽
n.咳嗽
It’s polite to cover your mouth when you cough.
咳嗽的时候挡着嘴是种礼貌。

sidewalk

n.人行道
We walked down the sidewalk and enjoyed the nice summer day.
我们走在人行道上，享受着美好的夏日。

tremble

v.发抖，摇动
n.震颤
That movie made him tremble with fear.
这部电影让他害怕的发抖。

league

n.社团，联盟
v.使…结盟，与…联合
This is a group photo of our football league.
这是我们足球社团的一张合影。

crowd

n.人群，一群，朋友
v.挤满
There was a large crowd of people at the concert.
音乐会上有很多人。

cotton

n.棉花，棉线，棉布
Cotton feels soft and can be used to make clothes.
棉花摸起来很柔软，可以用来制作衣服。

digest

v.消化
n.摘要
The food he ate was hard to digest.
他吃的食物很难消化。

necklace

n.项链
She like to wear many necklaces.
她喜欢戴很多项链。

lavatory

n.厕所
The lavatory in the middle is for the disabled.
中间的厕所是给残疾人用的。

drawer

n.抽屉
Remember to keep all your files in the drawer.
记得把你所有文件的都放在抽屉里。

forehead

n.前额，前部
She cares so much about her forehead.
她非常在意她的前额。

heavily

adv.重重地，沉重地，沉闷地，猛烈的
The truck was heavily loaded with goods.
这辆车转载了很重要的货物。

botany

n.植物学
Botany is the study of plants.
植物学是研究植物的学科。
