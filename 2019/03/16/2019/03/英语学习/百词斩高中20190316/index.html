复习

hydrogen

n.氢，氢气
The girl’s hydrogen balloon just flew away.Quick,catch it!
女孩的氢气球飞走了，快，抓住它！

tin

n.罐头
These tins contain different colors of paint.
这些罐子里装着不同颜色的颜料。

encourage

v.鼓励，支持
I encourage my girlfriend to do exercise.
我鼓励女友做运动。

contribute

v.捐献，捐助
Many people contribute money to help people in need.
很多人捐钱来帮助有需要的人们。

expense

n.花费，费用
Buying a car can be a great expense.
买车是笔大费用。

unconditional

adj.绝对的，无条件的
Mothers have unconditional love for their children.
妈妈对孩子的爱是无条件的。

nature

n.大自然
We all like to enjoy the beauty of nature.
我们都喜欢享受自然之美。

brick

n.砖块，砖
Builders are using bricks to build the wall.
建筑工人在用砖块砌墙。

confidential

adj.机密的，表示信任的
These confidential documents are top secret.
这些秘密文件是绝对机密。

silly

adj.傻的，愚蠢的
n.淘气鬼，傻孩子
He looks so silly with his head in his clothes.
他把头缩进衣服，看起来真傻。

bathtub

n.浴盆，浴缸
I left my red towel hanging on the edge of the bathtub.
我把一条红毛巾忘在浴缸沿上了。

defeat

v.打败
n.击败，战胜
Black defeated White.
黑棋战胜了白旗。

leftover

n.剩菜，残余物
adj.残余的
The leftovers were a feast for the hungry dog.
对于饥肠辘辘的狗来说剩菜都是宴席。

acquire

v.获得
The little boy acquired knowledge of the universe from reading.
小男孩通过阅读学到了很多关于宇宙的知识。

approximately

adv.近似地，大约
Pi is approximately wqual to 3.1416.
圆周率大约等于3.1416.

mushroom

n.蘑菇
vi.迅速发展
“Give me back my hat”said the mushroom sadly.
“把帽子还给我！”蘑菇难过的说道。

concern

n.关心，担忧
v.感兴趣，涉及，关心，（使）担忧
She shows her concern by helping the old lady walk.
通过帮助那位老太太走路，她表现出了她的关心。

fundamental

adj.基本的，根本的，重要的
n.基本原理
In modern society,Wi-Fi is the most fundamental need.
现代社会中，无线网络成了人们最基本的需求。

shore

n.岸，滨
There is a big “2017” on the shore.
在海岸上有一个大大的”2017”.

subjective

adj.主观（上）的，个人的
The taste in art is a highly subjective matter.
品味艺术是一件主观性很强的事。

jewel

n.宝石，宝石饰物
This necklace is made of beautiful jewels.
这个项链是由漂亮的宝石做的。

chopsticks

n.筷子
There are four pairs of chopsticks on the bamboo mat.
竹垫上有四双筷子。

carpet

n.地毯
My dream is to have a flying carpet.
我的梦想是拥有一张飞毯。

lip

n.嘴唇，唇状物
Her lips are the most beautiful part of her face.
她的嘴唇是她脸上最漂亮的部分。

extraordinary

adj.非同寻常的
The teacher said my daughter has extraordinary musical talent.
老师说我女儿在音乐方面天赋异禀。

tip

n.小费
v.给小费
I paid %5 to the waiter for tip.
我给了那个服务员5美元作为小费。

barber

n.理发师，理发店
I asked my barber to give me a new style this time.
这一次我让我的理发师给我理一个新发型。

veal

n.小牛肉
My favorite meat is veal.
我最爱吃的肉是小牛肉。

yoghurt

n.酸奶
Yoghurt tastes very delicious with cookies.
酸奶和饼干一起吃很美味呦！

wounded

adj.受伤的
The wounded soldier goes to the doctor every day.
这位负伤的士兵每天都去看医生。

新的学习

dignity

n.威严，威风，尊严，高贵
The policeman and the firefighter serve with dignity and honor.
警察和消防队员怀着尊严和荣誉任职。

bureaucratic

adj.官僚的，官僚政治的
This company has many bureaucratic procedures you need to go through.
这个公司有很多繁文缛节。

omelette

n.煎蛋卷
If you enjoy eating eggs, you might like an omelette for breakfast.
如果你喜欢吃蛋类，你应该会喜欢早餐吃煎蛋吧。

roast

v.烤，烘，挖苦
adj.烤的，烘的
I’m roasting meat for dinner.
我正在烤肉。

steel

n.钢，钢铁，钢制品
v.使坚强
adj.钢的，与钢有关的
Steel can be used to reinforce concrete in the construction of buildings.
在建造房屋时，钢铁可以用于加固。

ferry

n.渡船，渡轮
v.渡运
We took the ferry down the river.
我们乘坐了渡船顺流而下。

reserve

n.储备，保护区
v.预订
It is always good to have some stock in reserve.
储备些存货总没错。

facial

adj.表面的，面部的
n.面部按摩，美容
His funny facial expressions always make me laugh.
他面部表情太搞笑了，我简直笑到停不下来。

abundant

adj.大量的
This garden has an abundant amount of flowers.
这个花园有大量的花朵。

tend

v.照料，倾向
Thet tend the tree with care.
他们小心照料这棵树。

protect

v.保护，警戒
We need to protect this plant – it’s very special.
我们需要保护这株植物。

united

adj.团结的，和睦的
Let’s stay united through these difficult times.
让我们在这困难的时刻团结一致。

bid

n.出价,努力争取
v.出价
The winning bid was just %5.
中标价仅是5美元。

dialogue

n.对话，对白
These two women are having a dialogue over their babies.
这两个女人在谈论他们的小孩。

harmony

n.和睦，和谐
They live together in perfect harmony.
他们和睦地住在一起。

literature

n.文学，宣传册，文献
These four books are the classics of Chinese literature.
这四本书是中国的文化经典。

vote

v.选举，表决，投票
n.投票，选举权，选举，投票结果
Voting is a fair way for selection.
投票是一种公平的选举方式。

skateboard

n.冰鞋，滑板
Riding a skateboard is a cool way to travel.
滑滑板去旅行是很酷的方式。

negotiate

v.谈判，交涉，商议
Thet’re negotiating a deal,but neither is willing to make a compromise.
他们在协商一门生意，但双方都不愿意让步。

wound

n.创伤，伤口
v.伤，伤害
She has a small wound on her knee.
她膝盖上有一个小伤口。

generation

n.一代，一代人
My family includes three generations of people.
我们家三代同堂。

fantasy

n.幻想,想象
In his fantasy,he is a superhero!
在他的幻想中，他是个超级英雄。

shave

v.剃，刮
n.剃须，修免，剃刀
The man is shaving in front of the mirror.

aggression

n.攻击行为，（挑衅）行为，侵略
This cat looks full of aggression.
这只猫看起来充满了攻击性。

jungle

n.丛林，密林，危险地带
I love to explore deep into the jungle.
我喜欢去丛林深处探险。

gallon

n.加仑
The bottle can hold 1 gallon of water.
这个瓶子能装1加仑的水。

embarrass

vt.使窘迫，使尴尬，困窘，困扰
He embarrassed himself by farting in front of a lady.
当着女生的面放屁，他超级尴尬。

refer

v.参看，归因于
She refers to the book while she cooks.
在做菜时她会参考那本书。

pedestrian

n.步行者，行人
adj.平庸无奇的，无聊的
Many pedestrians are crossing the street.
许多行人正在过马路。

poison

n.毒物，毒药
v.放毒，毒害
Don’t drink that poison.It will kill you.
不要喝那瓶毒药，它会置你于死地。

offence

n.犯罪，冒犯，攻击，过错，令人讨厌的事物。
He must be punished for this terrible offence.
他必须因为这严重的犯罪行为而受到惩罚。

strengthen

v.加强，变坚固，变强大
She trains every day to strengthen her arms.
她每天坚持锻炼来强壮手臂。

damp

adj.潮湿的，有湿气的
London is damp and wet.
伦敦很潮湿。

combine

v.使混合（结合、组合、联合），联合
She combines eggs with other food to make a cake.
她把鸡蛋和其他食材混合起来用于做蛋糕。

picnic

n.郊游，野餐
The family enjoyed a nice picnic outside.
一家人在室外享用了一顿很好的野餐。

amount

n.数量，总额
v.等同，接近
The amount of sugar in this product is 28g.
这类食物中唐的总量是28克。

snack bar

phr.小吃店
I always have my lunch in the nearby snack bar.
我总在附近一家快餐店吃午餐。

meanwhile

adv.同时，与此同时
n.其间
The turtle finishes the race,meanwhile the rabbit is still sleeping.
乌龟完成了比赛，这个时候兔子还在睡觉。

passenger

n.乘客，旅客
All the passengers are seated and ready for the trip.
乘客们已经做好了，准备开始旅程。

worthless

adj.无价值的
The broken coin is worthless,you can’t buy anything with it.
硬币碎裂了后变的毫无价值，你用它来买不到任何东西。

missile

n.导弹，投射出的物体或武器
Missiles are used to attack enemies from a long distance.
导弹被用于远距离攻击敌人。

queen

n.女王，出众的女人，皇后
She is the queen of the United Kingdom.
她是英国的女王。

butter

n.黄油，奉承话
vt.涂黄油于…上，用黄油烹调
Toast tastes much better with butter on it.
在吐司上抹点黄油会更加美味。

universal

adj.普遍存在的，全体的，通用的
n.普遍原则
The mobile phones make universal communication possible.
手机使世界通讯成为了可能。

union

n.工会，融洽
The small union is having a meeting.
小协会在举行会议。
