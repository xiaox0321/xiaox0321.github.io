复习

clap

v.拍（手），鼓（掌）
n.轻拍（以示热情招呼），拍手（喝彩），鼓掌（欢迎），善意的拍打
In a show of support,they all began to clap their hands.
为了表示支持，他们开始鼓掌。

evolution

n.进化，演变
This picture shows the evolution of humans over millions of years.
这张图展示了百万年来人类的进化。

bounce

vt.使弹起
vi.弹起，蹦跳
n.弹跳，弹力，活力
You have to bounce the ball in basketball.
打篮球得让球弹起来。

independence

n.（国家的）独立，（人的）自立，独立
America gained its independence from Britain in 1776!
1776年，美国脱离英国取得独立。

yell

v.叫喊
I’ve never seen someone yelling so loud on the phone before!
我从没见过谁对着电话那样咆哮。

fear

v.害怕，担心
n.顾虑，胆怯
The little girl fears the injection.
这个小女孩害怕打针。

undertake

v.承担，承包
I want everyone to know that you undertake this fishpond!
我要让所有人都知道，这个鱼塘被你承包了！

stadium

n.（周围有看台的）体育场，露天大型运动场
This is one of the biggest football stadiums in the world.
这是世界上最大的足球场之一。

straw

n.吸管，稻草，麦秆
Why do you need two straws for one drink?
你一杯饮料要两根吸管来干嘛？

beg

v.乞求，恳求，乞讨
The homeless man was begging for some food and money on the street.
这个无家可归的男人在大街上乞讨一些食物和钱。

occupy

v.占领，占据，侵占，担任（职务），任职
The little dog has occupied her heart.
这只小狗占据了她的心。

bacon

n.熏猪肉，培根
I like to have bacon and eggs for my breakfast.
早餐我想吃培根和蛋。

outspoken

adj.直言的，坦率的
Her outspoken remarks always reveal facts.
她直言不讳的言语总是戳中现实。

struggle against

phr.同…作斗争
To get across the park,I had to struggle against the bad weather.
为了穿过公园，我必须与恶劣的天气作斗争。

break off

phr.折断，掰掉，突然停止，暂停，中断
Let me break off a small piece of chocolate for you.
让我掰一小块巧克力给你。

dormitory

n.宿舍，学生宿舍，宿舍楼【美国英语】
Look!This dormitory is so dirty!
快看呀！这间宿舍好脏！

sideways

adj.向侧面的
adv.斜着地
Please turn to the side – we want a sideways view.
请往侧面转一下身，我们想要一个侧面照。

greedy

adj.贪婪的，贪心的，贪吃的
The greedy woman wanted more food from the pot before she’d even finished her bow!
贪婪的她吃着碗里的，想着锅里的。

tourism

n.旅游，旅游业
Nowadays,the tourism industry is booming all around the world.
现如今，世界各地的旅游业都在蓬勃发展。

dioxide

n.二氧化碳
Carbon dioxide is making our planet warmer.
二氧化碳正在使地球升温。

beyond

prep.超过，在能力、理解力之外，除…之外
adv.更远处，更多，以后
I couldn’t hit him as he was beyond my reach.
我打不了他，因为我根本够不着他。

pregnant

adj.怀孕的
The pregnant woman is drinking water.
这个怀孕的女人正在喝水。

heat

n.高温，【物理学】热
vt.给…加热
Be careful of the heat from the fire!
小心这堆火的高温！

component

n.零件
adj.组成的
These are the components of a car.
这些是一辆车的零部件。

permanent

adj.永久的，持久的
Her eyebrows are being made permanent.
这位女士正在做永久纹眉。

smog

n.烟雾
There is too much smog in this big city.
这个大城市里烟雾弥漫。

cordless

adj.（电器）不用电线的
A cordless mouse is convenient for using your computer.
操作电脑时，无线鼠标很好用。

backache

n.背痛
Since I got an office job,my backache has gotten much worse.
自从我得到一份办公室工作，我的背痛越来越严重。

geometry

n.几何学，几何形状
Geometry is the study dealing with different kinds of shapes.
几何学是研究各种不同图形的学科。

tractor

n.拖拉机，牵引车
We use tractors to farm.
我们用拖拉机来耕地。

adequate

adj.充足的，充分的，足够的，尚可的，过得去的
I have prepared an adequate amount of food for 5 people.
我为五个人准备了充足的食物。

delicate

adj.易碎的，易坏的，脆弱的
Porcelain cups are very delicate,do not drop them!
瓷杯易碎，小心掉落！

beast

n.野兽
The beast dances with the beauty.
野兽与美女共舞。

pulse

n.脉搏
v.（使）跳动，搏动，震动，（感情等）洋溢，充满
The doctor felt the patient’s pulse.
医生给病人把了脉。

新的学习

fit

n.匹配，相配，合身
v.适合
adj.合适的
They two are a perfect fit and I’m sure they are good friends!
这两个人太搭了！它们肯定是好朋友！

insure

v.为…投保，保险，买保险
He insures the cars against traffic accidents.
他为车投保了事故保险。

come off

phr.从…掉下，发生，举行，（计划）实现
She looks surprised when her facial mask comes off her face.
她面膜从脸上掉下来时她被吓了一跳。

savage

n.野蛮人，粗鲁的人
adj.凶猛的，猛烈的
v.（狗等）乱咬
Their wordy conversation made me feel like a total savage!
他们文邹邹的对话让我觉得自己完全就像个野人！

actual

adj.实际的，真实的
The actual fact is that crocodiles walk underwater instead of swimming.
事实上，鳄鱼在水下是靠走而不是游泳。

master

n.主人
adj.精通的，主要的
v.掌握，精通
The dog and its master are close to each other.
这条狗和他的主人很亲密。

glad

adj.高兴的
I am very glad to hear from my mother.
我很高兴收到母亲的来信。

fridge

n.电冰箱
I opened the fridge to get some food.
我打开冰箱，拿点食物。

represent

v.代表，阐明
This hand gesture represents love.
这个手势代表了爱。

modest

adj.谦虚的，谦逊的
These two businessmen may be rich,but they’re still very modest!
这两位企业家也许非常富有，但他们仍然很谦虚！

ox

n.牛，公牛
The strong ox is angry now.
这头强壮的公牛现在很生气。

nut

n.坚果
Yoghurt goes well with mixed nuts.
酸奶和混合坚果很配。

conductor

n.指挥，售票员
The conductor is the leader of the band.
指挥使乐队的老大。

well-known

adj.有名的，著名的
This is a well-known painting.
这幅画世界闻名。

rescue

v.营救，援救
n.营救，救援
The fireman rescued the little boy from the fire.
消防员把小男孩从火中救了出来。

fee

n.费用，酬金
He was shocked by the huge amount of hidden fees behind such a haircut.
他被这个发型背后的巨额隐形费用惊呆了。

autonomous

adj.自动驾驶的，自治的，独立自主的
The man is relaxing,reading a book in his autonomous car.
这个男人正在他的自动驾驶汽车中悠闲地读书。

broad

adj.宽的，宽阔的，广阔的
This road is broad enough for many cars to drive on.
这条路足够宽，可以容纳很多车行驶。

accomplish

v.完成，做到，实现
I have accomplished my tasks on schedule.
我已经完成了计划上的任务。

neighbourhood

n.社区
I live in a friendly neighbourhood where everyone knows each other.
我住在一个友好的社区里，那里每个人都相互认识。

organ

n.器官
Every organ plays an important role in our body.
每一个器官在我们身体中都扮演着重要的角色。

ceremony

n.典礼，仪式
The student was awarded his certificare during the graduation ceremony.
毕业生在毕业典礼上接受了他的毕业证书。

inventor

n.发明家
Thanks to the inventor of the telephone,we can talk anytime anywhere.
多亏了电话的发明者，我们才能随时随地地聊天。

draft

n.草稿
v.起草
This is just the draft design of a building.
这只是建筑的草图设计。

patience

n.耐心，忍耐
Fishing demands a great deal of patience.
钓鱼需要极大的耐心。

candidate

n.（官职、获奖资格等的）候选人
The candidate are presenting the speeches for the election.
候选人正在为选举发表演说。

disturb

v.打扰，妨碍，使烦恼
His snoring really disturbs me at night!
晚上，他的呼噜声真的很打扰我！

merchant

n.商人，批发商
The merchant checks the quality of his produce.
商人在检查农产品质量。

construct

v.建造，构筑
The workers are constructing a tall building.
工人们正在建造一座高楼。

dismiss

v.开除，解散，摒除，不再考虑
The boss was angry and dismissed the staff.
老板很生气，并且开除了那个职工。

feed

v.喂（养），吃饲料
My mother would feed me when I was hungry.
我饿的时候妈妈会喂我。

rapid

adj.迅速的，急促的
The rabbit ran at a rapid speed.
那只兔子飞快的跑了。

humor

n.幽默，诙谐
The show is full of humor,hahaha!
这个节目太幽默了，哈哈哈！

ridiculous

adj.可笑的，荒谬的
She looks ridiculous wearing this dress when walking in the street.
她穿着这件衣服上街，看上去太可笑了。

fluency

n.流利，流畅
He speaks with perfect fluency.
他说话极其流利。

yawn

v.打哈欠
n.乏味或枯燥的事物
v.打着哈欠说
The cat yawned lying on the grass.So cute!
猫咪躺在草地上打哈欠。太可爱了！

boycott

n.抵制
vt.抵制
The office workers are putting up a boycott.
员工们在举行抗议活动。

certificate

n.合格证书，文凭，证明，证明书
I got my graduation certificate after finishing college.
在完成大学教育后，我获得了毕业证书。

hug

v.拥抱，抱紧
n.拥抱
The happy couple hug in front of their new home.
这对开心的夫妇相拥在他们的新房子前。

pardon

v.原谅，宽恕
excl.请再说一遍
n.原谅，饶恕，赦免
The king pardoned the prisoner.
君主赦免了那名囚犯。

fur

n.毛皮，毛皮外套，人造毛
A fur coat is very warm,but very expensive!
皮毛外套很暖和，但也很昂贵。

jet

n.喷气式飞机
v.乘坐喷气式飞机
Nowadays most long-distance travel is by jet plane.
现在，大多数长途旅行都是用喷气式飞机。

bacterium

n.细菌
This kind of bacterium can cause a very dangerous disease.
这种细菌能引发非常危险的疾病。

shoulder

n.肩膀，肩部
vt.肩负，承当
The old man has pain in his shoulder.
这位老年人肩膀疼。

polite

adj.有礼貌的，客气的，文雅的
The student is very polite to his teacher.
那个学生对老师很有礼貌。

religious

adj.虔诚的
He is a religious believer,he goes to church every week.
他是个虔诚的教徒，每周都会去教堂。

pupil

n.小学生
The pupils are walking to school.
小学生们正在走路去学校。

outcome

n.结果，后果
The final outcome of the football game is 0:0.
这场足球比赛的最后结果是0:0.

unite

v.团结，联合，统一，合并
Let’s unite as one to defeat our common enemy.
让我们齐心协力，击败共同的敌人。

seminar

n.研讨会
A teacher and some students are attending a math seminar.
一名老师和学生们正在开数学研讨会。

sour

adj.酸的
The lemon has such a sour taste!
柠檬真酸啊！

salute

v.向…行举手礼，赞扬，赞颂
n.致敬，欢迎
The policeman salutes me as I walk by.
当我走过时，这位警察向我行礼。

material

n.材料，原料，资料
adj.物质的，实体的
Many different materials are needed for construction work.
建造工程需要很多种不同的建筑材料。

pine

n.松树
v.（因死亡、离别等）难过，悲伤
The pine tree on Huangshan is very famous.
黄山的迎客松非常有名。

vacation

n.休假，假期
v.休假，度假
The girl took a vacation on the beach.
这个女孩在海边度假。
