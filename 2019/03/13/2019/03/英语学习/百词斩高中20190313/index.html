复习

slavery

n.奴隶制，奴隶身份
Lincoln announced the slavery should be abolished.
林肯宣布奴隶制应该被废除。

celebrate

v.庆祝，赞美
We are celebrating the coming New Year!
我们正为新年的到来而庆祝！

rough

adj.粗糙的
The hands of the farmer are rough.
这个农民的手很粗糙。

fancy

adj.华丽装饰的，别致的，引人注目的
n.喜爱，幻想，想象力
vt.想象，幻想
This fancy gold tableware must have been so expensive!
这套华丽的金色餐具肯定很贵！

immediate

adj.立即的，立刻的
This letter demands my immediate response.
这封信需要我立即回复。

walnut

n.核桃，胡桃
Look,this walnut has a heart-shaped core.
看，这个核桃仁是心形的。

organize

v.整理，组织
Please organize the cards by number.
请按编号整理好卡片。

warehouse

n.仓库
The warehouse is full of boxes.
仓库里堆满了货箱。

litter

n.垃圾
v.乱丢
Lots of litter was left on the beach by tourists.
沙滩上有很多游客留下的垃圾。

liberate

v.解放，释放
She felt her heart liberated together with the birds.
她感到自己的心随着鸟儿一起被解放了。

possess

v.拥有，占有，缠住，迷住
Take the key and now you possess a house.
把钥匙拿去。现在你已经拥有一套房子了。

national

adj.国家的，民族的
n.国民
Baseball is the national game of the U.S.
棒球是美国的国民运动。

humorous

adj.幽默的，搞笑的，诙谐的
This guy was so humorous and made everyone laugh.
这个家伙很幽默，把大家都逗笑了。

instant

adj.即食的
n.瞬间
Making a cup of instant coffee is a simple process.
制作一杯速溶咖啡的过程很简单。

hatch

v.孵出，孵
n.船舱盖
Look,the eggs are hatching!
看啊，小鸡出壳了！

thunder

n.雷，雷声，轰隆声
v.大声抱怨，吼叫
Thunder and lightning scare me so much!
打雷和闪电吓坏我了！

convenience

n.方便
Sometimes I eat pre-cooked meals for convenience.
有时为了方便，我会吃事先做好的饭菜。

anchor

n.锚，担任（广播电视新闻节目）的支持人
The ship dropped the anchor and hit the shark on head.
船抛出锚，打到了一只鲨鱼。

wedding

n.婚礼，婚宴
The couple kissed each other at their wedding.
新婚夫妻在婚礼上亲吻对方。

dilemma

n.进退两难的窘境
I am in a dilemma.Both ways are so dangerous!
我处在进退两难的困境里，两条路都好危险啊！

institution

n.机构
The United Nations is an international institution.
联合国是一家国际机构。

violence

n.暴力
The two boys used wiolence to end the quarrel.
这两个男孩动用武力结束了争吵。

offshore

adj.近海的，海岸的
adv.离岸的，近海的
Offshore oil extraction is the best way to get oil.
近海原油开采是得到石油的最好方式。

Atlantic

adj.大西洋的
n.大西洋
The northern part of Atlantic Ocean lies between Europe and North America.
北大西洋位于欧洲和北美洲之间。

refreshments

n.点心，茶点
Help yourself to the refreshments on the table.
请自取桌上的茶点。

unconscious

adj.不省人事的，未察觉的
We found him unconscious on the floor.
我们发现他倒在地上，不省人事。

devote

vt.奉献
The woman devotes her entire life to her religion.
这个女人把一辈子都奉献给了宗教信仰。

donate

v.捐赠
We donated a sum of money to the charity.
我们向慈善机构捐了一笔钱。

comrade

n.战友
My comrade and I are good friends.
我和我的战友是好朋友。

gradually

adv.逐步地
The full moon gradually changes to a new moon.
圆月会逐渐变成新月。

expect

v.期待
She’s expecting her baby to be born soon.
她期待着宝宝的到来。

sheet

n.纸张，被单
Please write your name on this sheet of paper.
请在这张纸上写下你的名字。

alphabet

n.字母表
The English alphabet has 26 letters.
英语字母表有26个字母。

guarantee

v.保证，担保
n.保证金
I guarantee that you will have our best service.
我保证你会得到我们最优质的服务。

indeed

adv.的确，真正地，事实上
My coworkers told me that I did a good job indeed.
我的同事告诉我真的干的不错！

due to

prep.由于，取决于
She cannot hear things clearly due to her old age.
由于年岁过高，她的耳朵听不清。

rapid

adj.迅速的，急促的
The rabbit ran at a rapid speed.
那只兔子飞快的跑了。

cure

v.治愈，治疗
These pills will cure your sickness.
这些药片能治好你的病。

humor

n.幽默，诙谐
The show is full of humor,hahaha!
这个界面太幽默了，哈哈哈！

ridiculous

adj.可笑的，荒谬的
She looks ridiculous wearing this dress when walking in the street.
她穿着这件衣服上街，看上去太可笑了。

fluency

n.流利，流畅
He speaks with perfect fluency.
他说话极其流畅。

yawn

v.打哈欠
n.乏味或枯燥的事物
v.打着哈欠说
The cat yawned lying on the grass.So cute!
猫咪躺在草地上打哈欠。太可爱了！

boyfott

n.抵制
vt.抵制
The office workers are putting up a boycott.
员工们在举行抗议活动。

hug

v.拥抱，紧抱
n.拥抱
The happy couple hug in front of their new home.
这对开心的夫妇相拥在他们的新房子前。

pardon

v.原谅，宽恕
excl.请再说一遍
n.原谅，饶恕，赦免
The king pardoned the prisoner.
君主赦免了那名囚犯。

新的学习

willingness

n.自愿
He had a great willingness to help people in need.
他非常乐意帮助别人，总是雪中送炭。

island

adj.内陆的，国内的
adv.在内陆地
Switzerland is an inland country.
瑞士是一个内陆国家。

accessible

adj.可进入的，可使用的
The building is only accessible if you have a card.
有卡才能进入这栋大楼。

temporary

adj.暂时的，临时的
She looks happy with her new temporary tattoo.
她对这个临时的新纹身很满意。

outstanding

adj.杰出的，突出的，显著的
His outstanding ability allowed him to win the contest.
他杰出的能力使他赢得了竞赛。

absorb

vt.吸收，汲取
Sponges can absorb a lot of water.
海绵可以吸取很多水。

tentative

adj.试探性的，犹豫不决的
When walking in darkness,every step you take is tentative.
在黑暗中行走时，你走的每一步都是试探性的。

thunderstorm

n.雷暴雨，大雷雨
The loud thunderstorm kept me awake all night.
雷暴雨的巨响让我整晚没睡。

lid

n.盖，盖子
The waiter is taking the lid off the plate.
服务员正把盘子上的盖子揭开。

quarrel

n.争吵，口角
v.争吵，争论
I had a terrible quarrel with my wife.
我跟我的妻子大吵了一架。

resist

v.抵抗，抵制
The overweight girl managed to resist the temptation of hamburgers.
这个胖女孩抵制住了汉堡的诱惑。

suitcase

n.小提箱，手提箱
My suitcase looks too old.
我的手提箱看上去太旧了。

foolish

adj.愚蠢的，傻乎乎的
His expression is really foolish.
他的表情看起来很傻。

Tibet

n.西藏
Let’s go to Tibet for vacation.
我们去西藏度假吧。

evident

adj.显然的，明显的，明白的
It is evident who the real murderer is.
真正的凶手是谁很明显。

biscuit

n.饼干，软饼
I love to eat biscuits when I drink coffee.
我喝咖啡时喜欢吃点饼干。

plug

n.插头
vi.插入（插头）
vt.塞
He wants to pull out the plug.
他想把插头拔出来。

beach

n.海滩，河滩
We spent a wonderful day at the beach.
我们在海滩边度过了美妙的一天。

embassy

n.大使馆，大使馆全体人员
The US embassy in the U.K. is located in London.
美国驻英大使馆位于伦敦。

nose

n.鼻子，前部，嗅觉
The girl has such a cute little nose.
小女孩有一个如此可爱的小鼻子。

beneath

prep.在…下方
adv.在下方
Most of the iceberg is beneath the surface.
冰上的大部分在海面之下。

politician

n.政治家，政客
The female politician is giving an election speech.
女政治家正为精选做演讲。

variety

n.品种，种类，变化，多样性，多样化
There is a wide variety of fruits on the table.
桌子上有各种各样的水果。

drum

n.鼓，圆桶
v.击鼓
The little guy played that song on the drums.
这个小家伙用鼓演奏出那首曲子。

excellent

adj.优秀的，极好的
He’s an excellent student who always gets excellent scores.
他是个优秀的学生，总是得高分。

mop

n.拖把（俗称地拖）
v.用拖把拖洗，擦，抹
Use this mop to clean the floor.
用这个拖把来清洁地板。

curriculum

n.课程，（学校或专业的）全部课程
The curriculum includes math,science and English.
这些课程包含数学、科学和英语。

hurricane

n.飓风
Run!The hurricane is coming!
快跑！飓风来了！

evidence

n.证据，证明
v.证明
They are gathering evidence to solve the case.
为了侦破案件，他们正在收集证据。

recipe

n.食谱
She follows the recipe to cook.
她照着食谱来做饭。

tap

n.塞子，水龙头
v.监听，窃听（电话）
Water flows from the tap.
水从水龙头流出来。

extreme

adj.极端的
n.极端
The roller coaster is an extreme test of courage.
过山车是对勇气的极端考验。

yummy

adj.美味的，好吃的
That pizza looks so yummy,give me a slice!
这个披萨看起来太美味了，给我一片吧！

headache

n.头痛，头痛的事
Her headache looks so painful!
她看起来头疼的很厉害。

disability

n.残疾，残疾状态
His disability didn’t stop him from running.
残疾并没有阻止他奔跑的脚步。

silk

n.丝，丝绸
Silk is a kind of smooth and soft material.
丝绸是一种光滑柔软的材料。

seal

n.火漆印
There is a red seal on the envelope.
信封上有一个红色的印章。

possession

n.财产，所有物，拥有
My house is my most expensive possession.
我的房子是我最贵重的财产。

poem

n.诗，韵文，诗体文
This poem is written by Li Bai.
这首诗是李白写的。

relief

n.解脱
Ahh,it’s such a relief to finally finish my work.
啊，完成工作真是放松啊。

rectangle

n.长方形，矩形
The rectangle has two long and two short sides.
长方形有两条长边、两条短边。

cushion

n.坐垫
v.减轻，缓和
There are several cushions on the bed.It’s comfortable.
床上有几个垫子，很舒服呢。

destination

n.目的地，终点
The destination of their journey is the west.
他们的目的地是西天。

silence

n.沉默
v.使沉默
It is better to maintain silence when you do not know what to say.
不知道该说什么的时候保持沉默就好。

grand

adj.壮丽的，宏伟的，重大的
n.一千美元，大钢琴
This grand building has always been an attraction for tourists.
这座宏伟的建筑遗址吸引着游客前来参观。

inspire

v.激发，激励，使生灵感
His speech inspired so many people to fight for equality.
他的演讲激励了很多人为平等为奋斗。

swell

v.（使）膨胀，（使）鼓起
n.增大，膨胀，肿胀，汹涌
The fish’s body swelled to protect itself.
这条鱼把身体膨胀起来保护自己。

motor

n.发动机，马达
That motorcycle has a big motor.
那辆摩托车有一个很大的发动机。

disappointed

adj.失望的
He was disappointed that his favorite football team lost.
他很失望，因为他最喜欢的足球队输了。

proper

adj.得体的，正确的，完全的
At a cocktail party,you should wear a proper outfit.
在酒会上，你应该穿得体的衣服。

poisonous

adj.有毒的
One bite from a poisonous snake could kill you.
被毒蛇咬一口就能要你的命。
