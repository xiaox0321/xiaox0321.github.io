复习

statesman

n.政治家
Statesmen always capture the attention of media.
政治家总是受到媒体的关注。

relate

v.相关，有联系，讲述
The family tree shows everyone I’m related to.
这个家谱显示了和我有关联的所有人。

irrigation

n.灌溉
The farmers have a new irrigation system to water the fields.
农民们有了新的灌溉系统来灌溉农田。

sauce

n.酱，调味汁
Wow!The tomato sauce in the bowl looks so delicious.
哇！碗里的番茄酱看起来很好吃的样子。

disease

n.疾病
The man has a heart disease.Get him to the hospital!
这个男人有心脏病，快送他去医院！

desperate

adj.绝望的，孤注一掷的
The unemployed man looks so desperate.
那个失业的男人看上去如此的绝望。

bound

adj.受约束的，有义务的
v.束缚，跳跃着前行
n.跳跃
These ropes are bound to the ring.
这些绳子和铁环绑在一起。

buffet

n.自助餐，（火车内）餐室，冷餐桌
v.反复敲打，打击，重击
You can eat as much as you like at the buffet.
吃自助餐的时候，你可以想吃多少就吃多少。

physician

n.内科医生
My physician told me to eat more fruits and vegetables.
我的内科医生告诉我要多吃水果和蔬菜。

racial

adj.种族的
Racial origin has nothing to do with making friends.
种族背景毫不影响结交朋友。

deposit

v.存款，存放
n.存款，定金
I deposit money in my piggy bank when I can.
当我有钱我会往我的存钱罐里存钱。

sail

v.航行
n.帆，航行
The big boat sails off into the sunset.
这艘大船朝着落日航行。

pronounce

v.发音，发表，宣布
Open your mouth wide when you pronounce this sound.
发这个音时，嘴巴要张大。

theatre

n.戏院，剧院
Let’s go to the theatre and watch a play!
我们去剧院看戏吧！

chest

n.胸膛
We can see his strong chest under his shirt.
我们可以看到他衬衫下强壮的胸膛。

training

n.训练，培养
You shoulc be prepared before training.
训练前你得准备好。

sniff

v.嗅，吸气
n.嗅，吸气（声）
You might see dogs at the airport sniffing for illegal drugs.
你可能会在机场看见狗在嗅毒品的味道。

accelerate

v.加速，增长，使增速，促进
The car accelerated from 60km/h to 100km/h.
汽车从60km/h加速到了100km/h。

quantity

n.数量，数目
I eat a large quantity of food every day.
我每天都吃大量食物。

punctuation

n.标点，全部标点符号
The meaning of a sentence often depends on its punctuation.
一句话的意思经常取决于标点符号。

various

adj.各种各样的
We saw various types of fish during our dive.
在潜水时，我们看到了各种各样的鱼。

pile

n.一堆
v.堆积，累积
What a tall pile of books!
多么高的一摞书！

museum

n.博物馆，展览馆
There are many pieces of art in the museum.
博物馆里有很多艺术作品。

Greece

n.希腊
Greece is a beautiful country with a long history.
希腊是个有着悠久历史的美丽国度。

awkward

adj.尴尬的，不方便的
He felt a bit awkward when receiving a bunch of crops.
收到一束大麦，他有点尴尬。

probable

adj.可能的，大概的
n.很有希望被选中的人
It’s probable for a lucky man to win in gambling.
在赌博中运气好的人是有可能赢的。

petrol

n.（英）汽油
“please fill my car up with petrol!”the driver said.
司机说：”麻烦加满油！”

glory

n.光荣，荣誉
Winning the first prize brings him much glory.
得了第一名他感到非常光荣。

sculpture

n.雕刻，雕塑
The lion sculpture is made of stone.
狮子雕塑是用石头刻的。

relay

n.接力赛跑
v.转播
In a 400-meter relay,a runner passes the baton to the next person.
在400米接力比赛中，运动员将接力棒传给下一个人。

cage

n.笼，鸟笼
v.关进（笼子）
Get me out of this cage!Where’s the key?!
把我从这笼子里面放出去！钥匙在哪儿？！

garlic

n.大蒜
Garlic makes any meal taste better.
大蒜让所有菜肴更好吃了。

sentence

n.句子
v.判决，宣判
Note the importance of this sentence.
注意这个句子的重要性。

pilot

n.飞行员
adj.实验性的
v.给…领航，驾驶
Wow!The pilots in the movie look so cool!
哇塞！这部电影里的飞行员啊好酷好帅啊！

mustard

n.芥末
adj.芥末色的
I like to add some mustard on my sushi.
我喜欢往寿司上加点芥末。

agriculture

n.农业
We depend on agriculture to give us food.
我们依靠农业给我们提供食物。

新的学习

hydrogen

n.氢，氢气
The girl’s hydrogen balloon just flew away.Quick,catch it!
女孩的氢气球飞走了。快，抓住它！

tin

n.罐头
These tins contain different colors of paint.
这些罐子里装着不同颜色的颜料。

encourage

v.鼓励，支持
I encourage my girlfriend to do exercise.
我鼓励女友做运动。

contribute

v.捐献，捐助
Many people contribute money to help people in need.
很多人捐钱来帮助有需要的人们。

festival

n.节日，欢宴，庆祝
Traditionally people spend time with their families during the Chinese Spring Festival.
人们历来在春节期间与家人共享时光。

expense

n.花费，费用
Buying a car can be a great expense.
买车是笔大费用。

unconditional

adj.绝对的，无条件的
Mothers have unconditional love for their children.
妈妈对孩子的爱是无条件的。

physicist

n.物理学者，物理学家
Einstein was a great physicist.
爱因斯坦是个伟大的物理学家。

nature

n.大自然
We all like to enjoy the beauty of nature.
我们都喜欢享受自然之美。

brick

n.砖块，砖
Builders are using bricks to build the wall.
建筑工人在用砖块砌墙。

confidential

adj.机密的，表示信任的
These confidential documents are top secret.
这些秘密文件是绝对机密的。

silly

adj.傻的，愚蠢的
n.（通常指小孩子）淘气鬼，傻孩子
He looks so silly with his head in his clothes.
他把头缩进衣服里，看起来很傻。

bathtub

n.浴盆，浴缸
I left my red towel hanging on the edge of the bathtub.
我把一条红毛巾忘在浴缸沿上了。

obey

v.服从，听从，遵从
He always obeys his wife without question.
他对他夫人唯命是从。

workmate

n.同事，工友
I enjoy working with my beautiful workmates.
我喜欢与我漂亮的同事们一起工作。

defeat

v.打败
n.击败，战胜
Black defeated White.
黑棋战胜了白旗。

leftover

n.剩菜，残余物
adj.残余的
The leftovers were a feast for the hungry dog.
对于饥肠辘辘的狗来说剩菜都是宴席。

Oceania

n.大洋洲
I would love to travel to one of the islands in Oceania.
我想去大洋洲的某个小岛旅行。

ground

n.地面，土壤，场地，原因
The color of the ground is green from grass.
地面的颜色是草绿色。

bake

v.烤，烘培
I learnt how to bake pizza from my mum.
我从妈妈那里学到了如何烤披萨。

acquire

v.获得
The little boy acquired knowledge of the universe from reading.
小男孩通过阅读学到了很多关于宇宙的知识。

approximately

adv.近似地，大约
Pi is approximately equal to 3.1416.
圆周率大约等于3.1416.

sew

v.缝，缝纫
vt.缝补
She uses the machine to sew a new dress.
她用机器缝制新裙子。

mushroom

n.蘑菇
vi.迅速发展
“Give me back my hat” said the mushroom sadly.
“把帽子还给我！”蘑菇难过地说道。

concern

n.关心，担忧
v.感兴趣，涉及，关心，（使）担忧
She shows her concern by helping the old lady walk.
通过帮助那位老太太走路，她表现出来她的关心。

fundamental

adj.基本的，根本的，重要的
n.基本原理
In modern society,Wi-Fi is the most fundamental need.
现代社会中，无线网络成了人们最基本的需求。

subjective

adj.主观（上）的，个人的
The taste in art is a highly subjective matter.
品味艺术是一件主观性很强的事。

jewel

n.宝石，宝石饰物
This necklace is made of beautiful jewels.
这个项链是由漂亮的宝石做的。

present

n.礼物，赠送物
v.赠送，呈现
adj.出席的，当前的
A present for me? Thank you! You’re so sweet.
给我的礼物？谢谢，你真好！

chopsticks

n.筷子
These are four pairs of chopsticks on the bamboo mat.
竹垫上有四双筷子。

Buddhism

n.佛教
The man sitting there believes in Buddhism.
坐在那里的人信奉佛教。

carpet

n.地毯
My dream is to have a flying carpet.
我的梦想是拥有一张飞毯。
